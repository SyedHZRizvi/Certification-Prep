## Journalistic Language Patterns

| Pattern | Example | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Conditional for unverified news | *il aurait démissionné* | he is said to have resigned |
| Passive construction | *une décision a été prise* | a decision was made |
| Nominalisation | *la hausse des prix* | the rise in prices |
| Past historic (style) | *il déclara que* | he declared that |

## Pronunciation Tip for Cultural Terms

- *Lumières* = /ly.mjɛʁ/ (the Lights — the é makes /e/ sound, not "ay")
- *Molière* = /mɔ.ljɛʁ/
- *Baudelaire* = /bod.lɛʁ/
- *Flaubert* = /flo.bɛʁ/ (the -au = /o/ sound)
