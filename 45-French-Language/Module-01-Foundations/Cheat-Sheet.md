# Module 1 — Cheat Sheet: Les Fondations

## The French Alphabet + Accent Marks

| A | B | C | D | E | F | G | H | I | J | K | L | M |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ah | bay | say | day | uh | eff | zhay | ash* | ee | zhee | kah | ell | em |

| N | O | P | Q | R | S | T | U | V | W | X | Y | Z |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| en | oh | pay | ku | air† | ess | tay | ü‡ | vay | dbl-vay | eeks | ee-grek | zed |

*H = always silent  †R = guttural (back of throat)  ‡U = rounded lips + "ee" position

### Accent Marks Quick Reference
| Accent | Symbol | Effect | Key Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| Accent aigu | é | Closed "ay" sound | été (summer) |
| Accent grave | è, à, ù | Open "eh"; meaning change | père (father) |
| Accent circonflexe | ê, â, ô, û, î | Longer/open vowel | être (to be) |
| Cédille | ç | Soft "s" before a/o/u | façon (way) |
| Tréma | ë, ï | Pronounce vowels separately | Noël (Christmas) |

## Silent Letter Rules

| Rule | Example | Pronunciation |
|---|---|---|
| H is always silent | heure (hour) | "uhr" |
| Final consonants usually silent | vous, est, vingt | "voo", "ay", "van" |
| -ent (3rd pl. verb ending) is silent | parlent (they speak) | "parl" |
| Liaison: silent C links before vowel | avec eux | "ah-vek-uh" |

## Vous vs Tu

| | Vous | Tu |
|---|---|---|
| Use for | Strangers, elders, professionals, groups | Friends, family, children, peers |
| Urdu parallel | آپ (āp) | تم (tum) |
| Golden rule | Default to VOUS — switch to tu only when invited | |
