## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **B. Microservices, API-first, Cloud-native, Headless**
MACH Alliance, 2020.

### Q2: **B. An API-only, headless-only MACH commerce engine**
commercetools ships no storefront, you bring your own React/Vue/Svelte frontend.

### Q3: **C. $800 (19 USC 1321)**
Set at $800 in 2016 (up from $200 in 1993). Under restriction for Chinese-origin goods 2024-2025.

### Q4: **C. €150**
IOSS applies for non-EU shipments ≤€150. Over €150, standard import scheme applies.

### Q5: **C. February 17, 2024**
DSA Regulation 2022/2065 applied to VLOPs from Feb 17, 2024.

### Q6: **C. 45M+ EU monthly users**
VLOP = Very Large Online Platform; designated by the European Commission.

### Q7: **C. DDU = buyer pays on delivery (high abandonment); DDP = seller pre-pays (better CX)**

### Q8: **B. ~20% abandonment when courier requests duty on delivery**
Industry-standard cross-border DDU abandonment rate per logistics-association reports.

### Q9: **B. Economic nexus replacing physical nexus for US sales tax**
Overturned Quill (1992); 47 states + DC passed economic nexus laws 2018-2021.

### Q10: **B. Amazon (the marketplace)**
Marketplace facilitator laws make the marketplace the tax collector for third-party sellers.

### Q11: **B. Company accounts with hierarchical roles, RFQ workflow, tiered pricing**

### Q12: **B. June 2022**
Shopify B2B on Plus launched GA at Shopify Editions Summer '22.

### Q13: **B. Purchase Order**

### Q14: **C. Advance Ship Notice (ASN)**

### Q15: **B. Invoice**

### Q16: **C. B2B is materially higher ($500-$50K+ vs $40-$200 typical)**

### Q17: **C. 3-15 buying-committee members**
Gartner / CEB *B2B Buyer Report*, 2017 (updated 2022): average buying-committee size 6.8 individuals.

### Q18: **C. ~$200M GMV with sufficient engineering capacity**
Forrester *MACH Adoption Report* 2024, empirically validated threshold.

### Q19: **C. Remix (post-2022 rebuild)**
Shopify acquired Remix in 2022 and rebuilt Hydrogen on it.

### Q20: **B. Oxygen**
Shopify's edge-deployed hosting product for Hydrogen storefronts.

### Q21: **C. $66B**
Reported April 2023 private valuation ahead of expected IPO.

### Q22: **B. Per-item environmental penalty (€5 rising to €10/item by 2030)**

### Q23: **C. Cross-border arbitrage built on a single regulatory loophole is structurally fragile**

### Q24: **C. API-first / MACH architectures with structured Commerce APIs**
Shopify's Storefront MCP (2024) is the canonical example; OpenAI Operator + Apple Intelligence + Claude all consume structured APIs.

### Q25: **C. Stay on Shopify (or similar suite) until GMV + engineering capacity supports composable**
Premature composable adoption is a documented failure mode (Forrester 2024).

### Q26: **B. Register for IOSS in one EU member state, collect VAT at checkout, ship DDP under €150**
Standard compliance pattern for small/mid cross-border sellers entering EU.
