## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **A. Crawl → On-page → Internal linking → Off-page / E-E-A-T**
Layer 1 hygiene (crawl/index) underlies everything. Most e-commerce SEO failures are Layer 1.

### Q2: **B. `name`, `image`, `description`, `offers` (with price + currency + availability)**
Minimum for rich snippet eligibility. `aggregateRating` + `brand` add star ratings.

### Q3: **B. Filter combinations explode URL count, wasting crawl budget on thin-content duplicates**
5 filters × 4 options × 5 sort orders = 3,125 URLs per category before content.

### Q4: **B. Noindex + canonical to parent; selectively promote high-search-volume facets to static indexed URLs**
What Wayfair, Zappos, Etsy do.

### Q5: **B. Hints Google may ignore; use `noindex` for hard exclusion**
Common exam trap. Canonical is a hint, not a directive.

### Q6: **B. Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness**
Experience added Dec 2022.

### Q7: **A. August 2022 (Sep 2023 expansion + Mar 2024 core integration)**

### Q8: **B. 27.6%**
Per Ahrefs / Tim Soulo 2023 study of ~80K keywords.

### Q9: **B. 50-60 characters**
Anything longer truncates in SERP.

### Q10: **A. Noindexed URLs, redirects, 404s, canonicalized-away URLs**
Sitemap hygiene matters.

### Q11: **A. Welcome, Abandoned Cart, Abandoned Checkout, Post-Purchase, Win-Back**

### Q12: **B. Email 1 reminder; Email 2 social proof; Email 3 discount**
Discounting Email 1 trains abandonment for discount-harvesting.

### Q13: **A. Brand story + welcome offer**
Highest CTR flow; don't waste it on transactional content.

### Q14: **B. 41-47%**
Per Klaviyo 2024 benchmarks.

### Q15: **B. 5-8x higher**
Per Postscript 2024 SMS Benchmark Report.

### Q16: **A. Express written consent (non-prechecked); STOP keyword; 10DLC; quiet hours**

### Q17: **B. US business SMS (since 2023)**
A2P 10-digit-long-code registration with carriers.

### Q18: **B. 25-95% profit lift**
Per Reichheld, *The Loyalty Effect* (HBS Press, 1996); confirmed in Reichheld & Markey 2011.

### Q19: **A. $1,000 annual spend**
Insider (free) → VIB ($350) → Rouge ($1,000).

### Q20: **B. ~60%**
Per Q4 2024 Starbucks disclosure. 34.3M active members.

### Q21: **A. Educational / how-to → Buying guides + comparisons → Use-case + problem-solver**

### Q22: **A. Buying guides internal-link to 5-20 PDPs, building category-level topical authority**
REI's *Expert Advice* hub is the canonical example.

### Q23: **B. Not against policy; manipulative content (regardless of production) is**
Google Search Central, March 2024 spam policies update.

### Q24: **B. $500-$1,500 per unauthorized message**
Per-message statutory damages. 50K × $500 = $25M floor.

### Q25: **A. Near-zero marginal cost; replacing 30% revenue with paid = 3-4x cost**
The lifecycle program is the highest-ROI marketing investment most brands have.

### Q26: **A. HCU demotes pure editorial scale; first-hand Experience required; AI-saturated SERPs**
Glossier's 2014 playbook benefited from a less-saturated organic SERP. The 2026 environment requires layered authenticity signal.
