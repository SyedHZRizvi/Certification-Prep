## ✅ After watching

1. Recite the difference between Cat 5e / Cat 6 / Cat 6a in speed AND distance.
2. List the top 12 ports you'll see on the exam.
3. Recite the DHCP D-O-R-A process.
4. Explain APIPA and when you'd see a 169.254.x.x address.
5. Identify the difference between hub, switch, and router by what each does at OSI layers.

If you can answer all 5, you're ready for the [Quiz](./Quiz.md).
