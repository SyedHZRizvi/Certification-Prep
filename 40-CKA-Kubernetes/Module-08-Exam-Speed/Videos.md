## Viewing Guide

| Tier | When to Watch | Goal |
|------|--------------|------|
| Essential | Before your first timed practice exam | Build core speed habits |
| Recommended | After your first practice exam, before your second | Fill gaps revealed by practice |
| Optional | Final week before exam day | Polish edge cases and environment familiarity |

Work through these videos actively — have a terminal open and run every command as you see it. Passive viewing of kubectl commands does not build muscle memory. Typing them does.
