## 💭 After Watching — Reflection Questions

Take 5 minutes to answer these without looking at notes. If you can't answer all of them confidently, re-watch the relevant video.

1. **What is the fundamental architectural difference between a container and a VM?** (If your answer doesn't mention the kernel, it's incomplete.)

2. **Trace the journey of a `kubectl apply -f pod.yaml` command** — which components are involved, in what order, until the container is running?

3. **What happens if the kube-scheduler process dies?** Running pods continue or stop? New pods get scheduled or not? Why?

4. **Why would `docker ps` return no results on a CKA exam node even though pods are running?**

5. **What is the difference between `docker stop` and `docker kill`? What signals are sent?**

6. **You delete a pod that was writing logs to `/var/log/app.log` inside the container. Is that file recoverable? Why or why not?**

7. **Which Kubernetes component is responsible for knowing that a Deployment needs 3 replicas but currently has 2?** How does it find out, and what does it do about it?
