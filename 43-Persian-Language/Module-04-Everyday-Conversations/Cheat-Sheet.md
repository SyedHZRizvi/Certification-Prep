# Module 4 Cheat Sheet — روزمرہ گفتگو (Everyday Conversations)

---

## At the Bazaar / Shopping (بازار)

| Persian | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| این چند است؟ | *in chand ast?* | How much is this? |
| خیلی گران است | *kheyli gerān ast* | It's very expensive |
| می‌تونم کمتر بگیرم؟ | *mitūnam kamtar begiram?* | Can I get it cheaper? |
| چقدر می‌خواید بدید؟ | *cheqadr mikhāhid bedid?* | How much do you want to pay? |
| آخرین قیمت | *ākharin qeymat* | Final price |
| باشه، قبوله | *bāshe, qabule* | Okay, it's a deal |
| تخفیف می‌دید؟ | *takhfif midid?* | Do you give a discount? |
| خیلی ارزان | *kheyli arzān* | Very cheap |

## At a Restaurant (رستوران)

| Persian | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| منو لطفاً | *manu lotfan* | Menu, please |
| بفرمایید | *befarmāyid* | Here you go / Go ahead |
| سفارش می‌دید؟ | *sefaresh midid?* | Are you ready to order? |
| می‌خوام ... بخورم | *mikhām ... bkhoram* | I want to eat ... |
| خوشمزه‌ست! | *khoshmaze-st!* | It's delicious! |
| نوش جان | *nush-e jān* | Bon appétit |
| دستت درد نکنه | *dastat dard nakone* | Thanks for cooking |
| حساب لطفاً | *hesāb lotfan* | Bill, please |

## Iranian Home Visit (مهمانی)

| Persian | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| خوش اومدید | *khosh umadid* | Welcome |
| بفرمایید تو | *befarmāyid tu* | Please come in |
| قدم به چشم | *qadam be cheshm* | Most welcome (lit. steps on eyes) |
| راحت باشید | *rāhat bāshid* | Make yourself comfortable |
| ممنون از دعوتتون | *mamnun az da'vatatun* | Thank you for the invitation |
| چایی بیارم؟ | *chāyi biāram?* | Shall I bring tea? |
| شیرینی بخورید | *shirini bkhorid* | Please have some sweets |
| نه ممنون، سیرم | *na mamnun, siram* | No thank you, I'm full |
| لطف دارید | *lotf dārid* | You're very kind |
| خانه‌ات آبادان | *khāne-at ābādān* | May your home prosper |

## Making Friends (آشنایی)

| Persian | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| اسمت چیه؟ | *esmat chiye?* | What's your name? |
| اسمم ... هست | *esmam ... hast* | My name is ... |
| خوشبختم | *khoshbakhtam* | Nice to meet you |
| از کجایی؟ | *az kojāyi?* | Where are you from? |
| چه رشته‌ای می‌خونی؟ | *che reshte-yi mikhuni?* | What are you studying? |
| شماره‌ات رو می‌دی؟ | *shomāre-at ro medi?* | Can I have your number? |
| حتماً! | *hatman!* | Definitely! |
| دوست دارم بیشتر آشنا بشیم | *dust dāram bistar āshnā beshim* | I'd like to get to know you better |

## Taarof (تعارف) — Essential Courtesy Phrases

| Persian | Transliteration | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| بفرمایید | *befarmāyid* | Please / Here you go / Go ahead |
| قدم به چشم | *qadam be cheshm* | You're most welcome |
| خواهش می‌کنم | *khāhesh mi-konam* | Please / You're welcome |
| عزیزم | *azizam* | My dear |
| جان | *jān* | dear (suffix after names) |
| دستت درد نکنه | *dastat dard nakone* | Thanks for your effort |
| نوش جان | *nush-e jān* | Bon appétit |
| خداحافظ | *khodāhāfez* | Goodbye (may God protect you) |
| مجدداً بیاید | *mojaddadan biāyid* | Come again |
