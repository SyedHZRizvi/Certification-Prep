|---|---|---|

## Past Tense Negation

Add **نـ (*na-*)** directly before the past stem:

| Positive | Negative | English |
|---|---|---|
| رفتم | نرفتم | I didn't go |
| خوردم | نخوردم | I didn't eat |
| دیدم | ندیدم | I didn't see |
| گفتم | نگفتم | I didn't say |
| آمدم | نیامدم | I didn't come |

## Future Tense

### Formal Future: خواه + past stem
| Persian | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| خواهم رفت | *khāham raft* | I will go |
| خواهی رفت | *khāhi raft* | You will go |
| خواهد رفت | *khāhad raft* | He/she will go |
| خواهیم رفت | *khāhim raft* | We will go |
| خواهید رفت | *khāhid raft* | You will go (pl.) |
| خواهند رفت | *khāhand raft* | They will go |

### Colloquial Future: Present tense + time word
| Persian | Transliteration | English |
|---|---|---|
| فردا می‌رم | *fardā miram* | I'm going tomorrow |
| بعداً زنگ می‌زنم | *ba'dan zang mizanam* | I'll call later |
| این هفته می‌بینمت | *in hafte mibinamat* | I'll see you this week |

## Conditional Sentences

| Pattern | Example | English |
|---|---|---|
| اگر + present/subjunctive + result | اگر بیایی، خوشحال می‌شم | If you come, I'll be happy |
| اگر + past + result past | اگر پول داشتم، می‌رفتم | If I had money, I would go |
| اگه بشه (colloquial) | اگه بشه می‌آم | If possible, I'll come |

## All Tenses — Summary

| Tense | Formula | Example | English |
|---|---|---|---|
| Present | می + present stem + ending | می‌روم | I go |
| Present Cont. | دارم + می + present stem | دارم می‌روم | I am going |
| Past Simple | past stem + ending | رفتم | I went |
| Past Cont. | داشتم + می + present stem | داشتم می‌رفتم | I was going |
| Future (formal) | خواه + past stem | خواهم رفت | I will go |
| Future (coll.) | present + time word | فردا می‌رم | I'll go tomorrow |
