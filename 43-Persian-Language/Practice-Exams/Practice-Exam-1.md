# 🧪 Practice Exam 1 — Persian Language (A1–A2)

> **Conditions:** Set a 20-minute timer. 15 questions. No dictionary — test what you've absorbed.
> Take this AFTER completing Modules 1–2. Covers alphabet, numbers, greetings, basic vocabulary, and Persian-Urdu cognates.
> There is no pass mark for this exam — it is diagnostic. Review all missed questions in Module 1 and Module 2.

## 📝 Questions

### 1. The 4 letters unique to Persian (not in Arabic script) are:
A. ب، ت، ث، ج
B. پ، چ، ژ، گ
C. ص، ض، ط، ظ
D. خ، غ، ق، ک

### 2. Persian script is written in which direction?
A. Left to right, like English
B. Top to bottom, like Japanese
C. Right to left, like Urdu and Arabic
D. Both left to right and right to left depending on context

### 3. The Persian word for "water" (آب — āb) is a cognate with which Urdu word?
A. پانی (pāni)
B. آب (āb) — identical
C. دریا (daryā)
D. نہر (nahr)

### 4. What does سلام (salām) mean?
A. Goodbye
B. Thank you
C. Hello / Peace
D. Sorry

### 5. The Persian number ۷ (haft) corresponds to which Urdu word?
A. ہفت — identical (also means week in ہفتہ)
B. سات (sāt)
C. ستہ (sita)
D. سات — unrelated

### 6. What does ممنونم (mamnunam) mean?
A. I am sorry
B. Thank you
C. Goodbye
D. How are you?

### 7. The Persian word خانه (khāne — house) is most similar to which Urdu word?
A. گھر (ghar)
B. محل (mahal)
C. خانہ (khāna) — nearly identical
D. کوٹھی (kothi)

### 8. How do you say "Good night" in Persian?
A. صبح بخیر (sobh be-kheyr)
B. شب بخیر (shab be-kheyr)
C. خداحافظ (khodāhāfez)
D. عصر بخیر (asr be-kheyr)

### 9. What is the Persian word for "friend"?
A. آشنا (āshnā)
B. همسایه (hamsāye)
C. دوست (dost)
D. برادر (barādar)

### 10. The Persian number بیست (bist) corresponds to which numeral?
A. 12
B. 15
C. 18
D. 20

### 11. What does عشق (ishq) mean in Persian (and Urdu)?
A. Hope
B. Love / Passion
C. Sadness
D. Beauty

### 12. Which of these is NOT a Persian-Urdu cognate?
A. کتاب (book)
B. استاد (teacher)
C. گلاس (glass — from English)
D. امتحان (exam)

### 13. The Persian greeting خوش آمدید (khosh āmadid) means:
A. How are you?
B. See you later
C. Welcome
D. Good morning

### 14. What does ببخشید (bebakhshid) mean?
A. You're welcome
B. Thank you very much
C. Excuse me / Sorry
D. Please come in

### 15. Which statement about the Persian alphabet is TRUE?
A. Persian has exactly 28 letters, same as Arabic
B. Persian has 32 letters — 28 Arabic + 4 unique Persian letters (پ چ ژ گ)
C. Persian uses a completely different script from Urdu
D. Persian letters are not connected in writing
