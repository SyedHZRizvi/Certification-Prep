## 📝 Questions

### 1. Which sentence correctly uses the present continuous tense in Persian?
A. من دیروز رفتم (man diruz raftam)
B. من الان می‌روم (man alān mi-ravam)
C. من فردا خواهم رفت (man fardā khāham raft)
D. من رفته‌ام (man rafte-am)

### 2. What is the direct object marker in Persian?
A. که (ke)
B. از (az)
C. را (rā)
D. به (be)

### 3. Which sentence means "I don't know" in colloquial Persian?
A. نمی‌دانم (nami-dānam)
B. نمی‌دونم (nami-dunam)
C. نمی‌دونم — this is incorrect Persian
D. Both A and B are correct (A = formal, B = colloquial)

### 4. The ezāfe construction کتاب دوست (ketāb-e dust) means:
A. The book and the friend
B. A book for a friend
C. The friend's book / the book of the friend
D. A book like a friend

### 5. Which tense is دیدم (didam)?
A. Present tense: I see
B. Simple past: I saw
C. Present perfect: I have seen
D. Past continuous: I was seeing

### 6. How do you say "He was going" (past continuous) in Persian?
A. رفت (raft)
B. می‌رود (mi-ravad)
C. می‌رفت (mi-raft)
D. رفته بود (rafte bud)

### 7. Which is the correct plural of کتاب (book)?
A. کتاب‌ها (ketāb-hā)
B. کتاب‌ان (ketāb-ān)
C. کتاب‌یها (ketāb-ihā)
D. کتاب‌ون (ketāb-un)

### 8. What does ta'arof (تعارف) mean in Iranian culture?
A. A type of Persian sweet
B. The elaborate system of social courtesy — declining offers graciously
C. A formal business title
D. A type of Persian calligraphy

### 9. Which phrase would you use to begin a formal Persian email?
A. هی دوست (hey dust)
B. با سلام و احترام (bā salām o ehterām)
C. چطوری؟ (chetori?)
D. اوکی، پس بزن (okay, pas bezan)

### 10. Which Persian sentence means "If I had money, I would travel"?
A. اگر پول دارم، سفر می‌کنم
B. اگر پول داشتم، سفر می‌کردم
C. اگر پول داشته باشم، سفر خواهم کرد
D. چون پول دارم، سفر می‌کنم

### 11. What does this proverb mean? عجله کار شیطان است (ajale kār-e sheytān ast)
A. Patience is the key to success
B. Haste is the devil's work / Haste makes waste
C. The early bird catches the worm
D. Hard work leads to rewards

### 12. In formal written Persian, "I am going" is می‌روم (mi-ravam). What is the colloquial equivalent?
A. می‌روم — no change
B. می‌رم (mi-ram)
C. روم (ravam)
D. می‌رود (mi-ravad)

### 13. Which word means "office meeting" in Persian?
A. دفتر (daftar)
B. جلسه (jalase)
C. گزارش (gozāresh)
D. پروژه (proje)

### 14. How do you say "My head hurts" in colloquial Persian?
A. سرم درد می‌کنه (saram dard mi-kone)
B. به سرم درد آمد
C. سر من درد دارد
D. درد سر من می‌شود

### 15. Which Persian expression means "to take the plunge" (be courageous)?
A. آب زیر کاه (āb zir-e kāh)
B. دل به دریا زدن (del be daryā zadan)
C. کاسه داغ‌تر از آش (kāse dāgh-tar az āsh)
D. از ماست که بر ماست

### 16. The Persian verb باید (bāyad) means:
A. Maybe / perhaps
B. Must / should (obligation)
C. I can
D. I want

### 17. Which sentence is grammatically correct for "The book was written" (passive)?
A. نامه نوشت
B. نامه را نوشتم
C. نامه نوشته شد
D. نامه نوشته می‌کند

### 18. What is the Persian equivalent of "walls have ears"?
A. دیوار موش داره، موش هم گوش داره
B. دیوار گوش داره
C. دیوار بزرگ است
D. موش‌ها می‌شنوند

### 19. Which register difference is correct?
A. Formal: خوبه / Colloquial: خوب است
B. Formal: خوب است / Colloquial: خوبه
C. Both خوبه and خوب است are equally formal
D. خوبه is more formal than خوب است

### 20. The solar Hijri year 1404 corresponds approximately to which Gregorian year?
A. 1850
B. 1983
C. 2025
D. 2100

### 21. What is the meaning of صبر و ظفر هر دو دوستان قدیمند?
A. Haste makes waste
B. Patience and victory are old friends
C. Friends are better than money
D. Old friends are the best friends

### 22. Which word means "deadline" in business Persian?
A. وقت (vaqt)
B. مهلت (mohlat)
C. تاریخ (tārikh)
D. ساعت (sā'at)

### 23. In Persian, the question "Where are you from?" is:
A. کجا می‌روی؟
B. کجا هستی؟
C. اهل کجا هستی؟ / اهل کجایی؟
D. کجایی می‌آیی؟

### 24. Which sentence uses the subjunctive correctly after باید?
A. باید رفت (bāyad raft)
B. باید می‌رود (bāyad mi-ravad)
C. باید برود (bāyad beravad)
D. باید رفته است

### 25. The phrase به نظر من (be nazar-e man) means:
A. I don't know
B. In my opinion
C. As far as I can see (visually)
D. According to the news
