# Module 2 Quiz — بنیادی الفاظ (Core Vocabulary)

**26 Multiple Choice Questions | Family, Body, Food, Verbs, Adjectives, Time**

---

**1.** What does **پدر** (*pedar*) mean in Persian?

- A) Mother
- B) Brother
- C) Father
- D) Uncle

**Answer: C** — پدر = father. Compare: مادر (*mādar*) = mother.

---

**2.** The Persian word **دختر** (*dokhtar*) is also used in Urdu. What does it mean?

- A) Son
- B) Daughter / girl
- C) Sister
- D) Aunt

**Answer: B** — دختر = daughter / girl. It is a direct Persian-Urdu cognate.

---

**3.** Which word means "sister" in Persian?

- A) برادر
- B) مادر
- C) خواهر
- D) دختر

**Answer: C** — خواهر (*khāhar*) = sister. برادر = brother, مادر = mother.

---

**4.** The Persian word **چشم** (*cheshm*) means:

- A) Ear
- B) Nose
- C) Eye
- D) Mouth

**Answer: C** — چشم = eye. This is a cognate: اردو چشم (literary) also means eye.

---

**5.** What body part does **زبان** (*zabān*) refer to?

- A) Tooth
- B) Tongue / language
- C) Lip
- D) Throat

**Answer: B** — زبان = tongue AND language (same word for both). Urdu زبان has the same dual meaning.

---

**6.** In Persian, **خون** (*khun*) means:

- A) Bone
- B) Skin
- C) Blood
- D) Breath

**Answer: C** — خون = blood. Urdu خون is identical.

---

**7.** What does **نان** (*nān*) mean?

- A) Water
- B) Tea
- C) Meat
- D) Bread

**Answer: D** — نان = bread. Urdu نان is the same word for flatbread.

---

**8.** Which word means "tea" in Persian?

- A) آب
- B) شیر
- C) چای
- D) شربت

**Answer: C** — چای (*chāy*) = tea. Direct cognate with Urdu چائے.

---

**9.** The Persian spice **زعفران** (*za'ferān*) is used in many Iranian dishes. What is it?

- A) Turmeric
- B) Cumin
- C) Saffron
- D) Cardamom

**Answer: C** — زعفران = saffron, Iran's most famous spice.

---

**10.** What does **گوشت** (*gusht*) mean?

- A) Fish
- B) Vegetable
- C) Fruit
- D) Meat

**Answer: D** — گوشت = meat. Urdu گوشت is identical.

---

**11.** In Persian, the word for "house" or "home" is:

- A) اتاق
- B) باغ
- C) خانه
- D) حمام

**Answer: C** — خانه (*khāne*) = home/house. Urdu خانہ is the same.

---

**12.** What does **میز** (*miz*) mean?

- A) Chair
- B) Table
- C) Door
- D) Window

**Answer: B** — میز = table. Used in both Persian and Urdu.

---

**13.** The Persian infinitive **رفتن** (*raftan*) means:

- A) To come
- B) To eat
- C) To go
- D) To see

**Answer: C** — رفتن = to go. Past stem: رفت (*raft*); Present stem: رو (*row*).

---

**14.** What does **خوردن** (*khordan*) mean?

- A) To sleep
- B) To eat
- C) To drink
- D) To run

**Answer: B** — خوردن = to eat. (Note: in colloquial Persian, خوردن is also used for "drinking.")

---

**15.** The verb **دیدن** (*didan*) means:

- A) To hear
- B) To think
- C) To speak
- D) To see

**Answer: D** — دیدن = to see. Past stem: دید (*did*); Present stem: بین (*bin*).

---

**16.** Which verb means "to write" in Persian?

- A) خواندن
- B) نوشتن
- C) گفتن
- D) شنیدن

**Answer: B** — نوشتن (*neveshtan*) = to write.

---

**17.** What does **دوست داشتن** (*dust dāshtan*) mean?

- A) To have a friend
- B) To meet someone
- C) To like / love
- D) To miss someone

**Answer: C** — دوست داشتن literally "to have a friend" but idiomatically means "to like" or "to love."

---

**18.** The adjective **خوب** (*khub*) is shared between Persian and Urdu. What does it mean?

- A) Bad
- B) Big
- C) Good
- D) New

**Answer: C** — خوب = good. Used in both languages.

---

**19.** What does **گران** (*gerān*) mean?

- A) Big
- B) Expensive / heavy
- C) Beautiful
- D) Strange

**Answer: B** — گران = expensive / heavy (depending on context). Urdu گراں has the same meaning.

---

**20.** The adjective **بیمار** (*bimār*) means:

- A) Tired
- B) Happy
- C) Sick / ill
- D) Strong

**Answer: C** — بیمار = sick/ill. Urdu بیمار is the same.

---

**21.** What does **امروز** (*emruz*) mean?

- A) Yesterday
- B) Tomorrow
- C) Today
- D) Now

**Answer: C** — امروز = today.

---

**22.** The Persian word for "week" is:

- A) ماه
- B) سال
- C) ساعت
- D) هفته

**Answer: D** — هفته (*hafte*) = week. Urdu ہفتہ is identical.

---

**23.** What does **همیشه** (*hamisha*) mean?

- A) Sometimes
- B) Never
- C) Always
- D) Often

**Answer: C** — همیشه = always. Urdu ہمیشہ is identical.

---

**24.** In Persian, **گرم** (*garm*) means:

- A) Cold
- B) Fast
- C) Warm / hot
- D) Wet

**Answer: C** — گرم = warm/hot. Urdu گرم is the same.

---

**25.** Which of the following Persian words for family members is a direct Urdu cognate?

- A) پدر (father)
- B) مادر (mother)
- C) خواهر (sister)
- D) شوهر (husband)

**Answer: D** — شوهر (*showhar*) = husband is used in both Persian and Urdu (شوہر).

---

**26.** The verb **کار کردن** (*kār kardan*) means:

- A) To rest
- B) To travel
- C) To work
- D) To study

**Answer: C** — کار کردن = to work. کار (*kār*) = work, کردن = to do. Urdu کام کرنا is structurally similar.
