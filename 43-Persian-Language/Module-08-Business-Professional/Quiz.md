# Module 8 Quiz — فارسی تجاری (Business Persian)

**25 Multiple Choice Questions | Formal Correspondence, Presentations, Legal Vocabulary, Media Persian, Negotiation, Diplomatic Persian**

---

**1.** In a formal Persian business letter, the phrase **به استحضار می‌رسانم** (*be estehzār mi-resānam*) means:

- A) I am writing to request a meeting
- B) I respectfully bring to your attention
- C) I have received your letter
- D) I am closing this correspondence

**Answer: B** — به استحضار می‌رسانم = "I respectfully bring to your attention" — a key phrase in formal Persian correspondence, establishing humble formal register.

---

**2.** Which phrase means "It is humbly requested" in very formal Persian?

- A) به استحضار می‌رسانم
- B) پیرو نامه شماره
- C) مستدعی است
- D) پیشاپیش متشکریم

**Answer: C** — مستدعی است (*mostad'i ast*) = "It is humbly requested" — very elevated formal register used in official Persian correspondence.

---

**3.** What does **به پیوست تقدیم می‌شود** mean?

- A) Please find enclosed herewith
- B) Congratulations on your appointment
- C) Following our telephone conversation
- D) With best wishes for your success

**Answer: A** — به پیوست تقدیم می‌شود (*be peyvast taqaddom mi-shavad*) = "Enclosed herewith" or "Please find attached" — used when sending documents with a letter.

---

**4.** In Iranian business culture, the stage called **ایجاد اعتماد** (*ijād-e e'temād*) refers to:

- A) Signing the final contract
- B) Building personal trust before business
- C) Getting government approval
- D) Writing the formal proposal

**Answer: B** — ایجاد اعتماد = "building trust" — the essential first stage in Iranian business, where personal relationship must come before transaction.

---

**5.** What is the Persian term for a **public joint-stock company**?

- A) شرکت سهامی خاص
- B) شرکت سهامی عام
- C) سازمان دولتی
- D) نهاد خصوصی

**Answer: B** — شرکت سهامی عام (*sherkat-e sahāmi-ye 'ām*) = public joint-stock company. خاص = private, عام = public.

---

**6.** The Persian word **مالیات** (*māliāt*) means:

- A) Exchange rate
- B) Customs
- C) Tax
- D) License

**Answer: C** — مالیات = tax. This word is also used in Urdu (مالیات = taxation/tax system). The root مال = wealth/property, shared in Arabic-Persian-Urdu.

---

**7.** **نرخ ارز** (*nerkh-e arz*) in Persian business vocabulary means:

- A) Commercial law
- B) Exchange rate
- C) Annual budget
- D) Import duty

**Answer: B** — نرخ ارز = exchange rate. نرخ = rate, ارز = foreign currency.

---

**8.** In formal Persian presentations, what phrase opens the presentation to the audience?

- A) خداحافظ حضار
- B) با سلام خدمت حضار محترم
- C) بروید بیرون لطفاً
- D) جلسه را شروع می‌کنم

**Answer: B** — با سلام خدمت حضار محترم (*bā salām khedmat-e hozzār-e mohtaram*) = "Greetings to the honorable audience" — the standard formal Persian presentation opening.

---

**9.** How do you close a formal Persian presentation by thanking the audience?

- A) خداحافظ
- B) از توجه شما متشکرم
- C) جلسه تمام شد
- D) ممنون، برید

**Answer: B** — از توجه شما متشکرم (*az tavajjoh-e shomā moteshakkeram*) = "Thank you for your attention" — the standard formal closing phrase.

---

**10.** The Persian word for "parliament" used in BBC Persian news is:

- A) دولت
- B) رئیس‌جمهور
- C) مجلس
- D) انتخابات

**Answer: C** — مجلس (*majles*) = parliament in Iran. Urdu speakers will recognize: مجلس is used in Urdu too (assembly/gathering), directly borrowed from Arabic via Persian.

---

**11.** In Persian news media, **تورم** (*torom*) refers to:

- A) Unemployment
- B) Inflation
- C) Growth
- D) Stock market

**Answer: B** — تورم = inflation — a key economic term in BBC Persian and Iranian financial news.

---

**12.** The word **تحریم** (*tahrim*) in Persian news context means:

- A) Negotiations
- B) Elections
- C) Sanctions
- D) Agreement

**Answer: C** — تحریم = sanctions — extremely common in BBC Persian and Iran International news. Urdu equivalent: پابندیاں.

---

**13.** Persian news headlines often use present tense for recent past events. What does this convention reflect?

- A) A grammatical error
- B) The colloquial speaking style
- C) Immediacy — present tense conveys breaking news
- D) A difference from written Persian grammar

**Answer: C** — In Persian headlines, present tense (ایران اعلام می‌کند = "Iran announces") conveys immediate, breaking-news register — immediacy is the purpose.

---

**14.** In a Persian negotiation, **پیشنهاد اولیه‌ی ما این است که** means:

- A) We reject your offer
- B) Our initial proposal is that...
- C) We have reached agreement
- D) Our condition is final

**Answer: B** — پیشنهاد اولیه‌ی ما این است که... = "Our initial proposal is that..." — standard negotiation opening phrase.

---

**15.** Which phrase signals a polite counter-proposal in Persian negotiation?

- A) قرارداد نهایی شد
- B) به توافق رسیدیم
- C) این پیشنهاد برای ما قابل قبول نیست، چون...
- D) پیشنهاد اولیه‌ی ما این است که

**Answer: C** — این پیشنهاد برای ما قابل قبول نیست، چون... = "This proposal is not acceptable to us, because..." — the polite counter-proposal formula.

---

**16.** How do you say "We have reached an agreement" in Persian?

- A) قرارداد نهایی شد
- B) به توافق رسیدیم
- C) توافق ممکن نیست
- D) مذاکره ادامه دارد

**Answer: B** — به توافق رسیدیم (*be tavāfoq residim*) = "We have reached an agreement." به = to, توافق = agreement, رسیدیم = we reached.

---

**17.** In a formal Persian report, **خلاصه‌ی اجرایی** refers to:

- A) Methodology section
- B) Appendices
- C) Executive summary
- D) Introduction

**Answer: C** — خلاصه‌ی اجرایی (*kolāse-ye ejrāyi*) = executive summary. خلاصه = summary, اجرایی = executive/operational.

---

**18.** The formal report transition phrase **با توجه به داده‌های فوق** means:

- A) In conclusion, we recommend
- B) Given the above data
- C) As shown in the chart below
- D) It is recommended that

**Answer: B** — با توجه به داده‌های فوق (*bā tavajjoh be dāde-hā-ye fawq*) = "Given the above data" — a standard analytical transition phrase.

---

**19.** The diplomatic Persian address for a head of state is:

- A) آقای رئیس
- B) جناب آقای رئیس‌جمهور
- C) سلام رئیس
- D) مدیر عامل محترم

**Answer: B** — جناب آقای رئیس‌جمهور = "His Excellency Mr. President" — the formal diplomatic address for a president in Persian.

---

**20.** Which Persian phrase means "We condemn the said action" in diplomatic language?

- A) تعهدات خود را تأیید می‌کنیم
- B) خواستار گفتگوی سازنده هستیم
- C) اقدام مزبور را محکوم می‌کنیم
- D) اعلام نگرانی می‌کنیم

**Answer: C** — اقدام مزبور را محکوم می‌کنیم = "We condemn the said action" — standard diplomatic condemnation language.

---

**21.** Why do Urdu speakers find Persian diplomatic vocabulary particularly accessible?

- A) Persian and Urdu share Latin roots
- B) Both draw from the same Arabic-Persian administrative tradition of the Mughal and Ottoman empires
- C) Diplomatic Persian was invented by Pakistani scholars
- D) English is used for all Iranian diplomacy

**Answer: B** — Persian diplomatic vocabulary (تعهد، مذاکره، اعلامیه) is shared with Urdu because both languages drew from the same Arabic-Persian administrative tradition of the Mughal and Ottoman empires.

---

**22.** The Persian word **گمرک** (*gomrok*) means:

- A) Company registration
- B) Commercial law
- C) Customs
- D) Ministry

**Answer: C** — گمرک = customs (as in customs duties / border control). This word appears in both Persian and Urdu business contexts.

---

**23.** The phrase **خواهشمند است دستور فرمایید** in a formal letter means:

- A) We regret to inform you
- B) It is kindly requested that you instruct/order
- C) Please disregard this letter
- D) We are pleased to announce

**Answer: B** — خواهشمند است دستور فرمایید (*khāheshmand ast dastur farmāyid*) = "It is kindly requested that you instruct/order" — formal Persian directive in official correspondence.

---

**24.** Which day(s) of the week constitute the Iranian weekend (office closed)?

- A) Saturday and Sunday
- B) Friday and Saturday
- C) Thursday and Friday
- D) Sunday only

**Answer: C** — Thursday (پنجشنبه) and Friday (جمعه) are Iran's weekend — offices are closed. This is critical for scheduling international business meetings.

---

**25.** The Persian phrase **اقدام لازم معمول گردد** in formal correspondence means:

- A) Please review the attached documents
- B) Necessary action be taken
- C) I look forward to your reply
- D) This matter is hereby closed

**Answer: B** — اقدام لازم معمول گردد (*eqdām-e lāzem ma'mul gardad*) = "Necessary action be taken" — a formal directive phrase, typical in official government and business correspondence.
