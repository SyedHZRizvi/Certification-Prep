There is no single timeline — each certification has its own depth, weight, and exam style, so the realistic finish line varies course by course and student by student.

Two of our flagship tracks — Scrum Master (PSM I) and PMP — can typically be completed in 8–12 weeks, but only when the student follows the study plan strictly: a few focused study sessions every weekday, weekly mock-exam reviews, and consistent practice-question reps with disciplined review of every miss.

Every other track — AWS, Azure, AI & GenAI, CompTIA Security+, ASCM & ISM supply chain credentials, AI-Based Digital Marketing, Bitcoin & Cryptocurrency, and E-Commerce — has its own nature and its own timeline. Some are foundational and shorter; others are layered ladders that build over several months. Your personal pace depends on your prior background, weekly study hours, target exam date, and how confidently you score on the practice and mock exams before booking the real sitting.

The pragmatic rule that works for every course: study the material, score 85% or higher consistently on the practice and final mock exams, then sit for the real exam. Mastery first; speed second.
