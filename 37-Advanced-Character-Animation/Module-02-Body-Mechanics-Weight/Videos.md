## 📚 Recommended Viewing

<div class="vg-card-grid">

<a class="vg-card" href="https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=Kevin+Koch+animator+weight+overlapping+action+secondary+motion">

  <div class="vg-card-meta">Kevin Koch · ~22 min</div>

  <div class="vg-card-meta">Animation Education · ~18 min</div>
  <div class="vg-card-desc">Covers all five phases of a jump from crouch to landing. Special attention to the apex stretch (when to apply, when not to) and the energy-storage principle in the anticipation crouch.</div>
</a>

<a class="vg-card" href="https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=Dermot+Redmond+body+mechanics+weight+VFX+studio">

  <div class="vg-card-meta">Dermot Redmond · ~20 min</div>
