## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **B.** Avoid / Transfer / Mitigate / Accept.
### Q2: **B.** Expected loss = 5% × $1M = $50K. Treatment costs $200K → accept.
### Q3: **B.** 3TG: Tin, Tungsten, Tantalum, Gold.
### Q4: **B.** Dodd-Frank §1502 requires RCOI + Form SD.
### Q5: **B.** Recovery Time Objective.
### Q6: **A.** Recovery Point Objective = acceptable data-loss window.
### Q7: **B.** MTD must exceed RTO or the business stops before you recover.
### Q8: **B.** Engage, remediate, escalate, document — ISM principles + ethics.
### Q9: **B.** £36M turnover threshold.
### Q10: **B.** US Uyghur Forced Labor Prevention Act (2022).
### Q11: **C.** Scope 3 = all other indirect including supply chain.
### Q12: **B.** Decline + disclose + document.
### Q13: **B.** ISO 31000 = risk management.
### Q14: **A.** SA 8000 = social accountability.
### Q15: **B.** Refuse, document, escalate. UK Bribery Act has no facilitation exception.
### Q16: **B.** Contractual BCP requirement for critical suppliers with periodic review.
### Q17: **B.** Layer: transfer (insurance) + mitigate (dual-source, safety stock).
### Q18: **C.** "Aggressive cost reduction" is not an ISM principle. Cost matters but isn't a principle.
### Q19: **A.** People · Planet · Profit.
### Q20: **B.** Risk owner = accountable manager.
### Q21: **B.** Decline + escalate + document.
### Q22: **B.** Disclose, document, consider recusal to preserve fairness.
### Q23: **B.** Critical disruptions originate deeper. Map beyond Tier 1.
### Q24: **B.** Accept + document + monitor.
### Q25: **B.** AS9100 = aerospace.
### Q26: **B.** IATF 16949 = automotive.
### Q27: **B.** Refuse + return + document + remind of confidentiality.
### Q28: **B.** Decline + cite policy + escalate. No "minor" ethics violations.
