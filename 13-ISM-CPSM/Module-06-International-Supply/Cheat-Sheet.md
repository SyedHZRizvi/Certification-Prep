## 📦 Incoterms 2020, All 11

### Any Mode (7)

| Code | Name | Seller does → |
|---|---|---|
| EXW | Ex Works | Least. Buyer takes from seller's door. |
| FCA | Free Carrier | Delivers to named place / first carrier. |
| CPT | Carriage Paid To | Pays main carriage. |
| CIP | Carriage and Insurance Paid To | Pays carriage + ALL-RISK insurance. |
| DAP | Delivered At Place | Delivers to destination (not unloaded). |
| DPU | Delivered at Place Unloaded | Delivers AND unloads. (Replaced DAT.) |
| DDP | Delivered Duty Paid | Most. Including import duties. |

### Sea / Inland Waterway Only (4)

| Code | Name | Risk Transfer |
|---|---|---|
| FAS | Free Alongside Ship | At quay alongside vessel |
| FOB | Free On Board | When loaded ON vessel |
| CFR | Cost and Freight | When loaded ON vessel (seller pays freight) |
| CIF | Cost, Insurance, Freight | When loaded ON vessel + min insurance |

🧠 **Memorize the trend:** EXW → DDP, seller's responsibility GROWS.

### 2020 Changes
- DAT → **DPU** (broader)
- CIP requires **all-risk** insurance (was minimum)
- CIF still minimum insurance

## 🚫 Anti-Corruption

| Law | Country | Key Point |
|---|---|---|
| FCPA | US | Bribery of foreign govt officials. Narrow facilitation exception. |
| UK Bribery Act 2010 | UK | Broader: private + corp "failure to prevent." NO facilitation exception. |
| Sapin II | France | Similar |

**Always:** Refuse + Document + Escalate.

## 🌐 Cross-Cultural Quick Reference

### Hofstede (6 dimensions)
Power distance · Indiv/Collective · Masc/Fem · Uncertainty avoidance · Long-term orientation · Indulgence/Restraint

### Hall
- High-context: Japan, China, Arab, much of LATAM, implicit, relationship-driven
- Low-context: US, Germany, Scandinavia, explicit, contract-driven

🧠 Cross-cultural exam answer: **listen, learn, adapt.**

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

- ✅ "Use Incoterms 2020 precisely; not US 'FOB Origin/Destination'"
- ✅ "DPU replaced DAT; CIP needs all-risk; CIF still minimum"
- ✅ "Hedge known exposure via Treasury"
- ✅ "Refuse + document + escalate for any bribery request"
- ✅ "Certificate of Origin proves FTA eligibility"
- ✅ "Listen, learn, adapt in cross-cultural negotiation"

## ⚠️ Usually Wrong

- ❌ "FOB applies to air freight"
- ❌ "Invoice in our currency = no FX"
- ❌ "Pay the facilitation fee, it's customary"
- ❌ "Skip the Certificate of Origin"
- ❌ "DDP is cheapest" (it's convenient, not cheap)
