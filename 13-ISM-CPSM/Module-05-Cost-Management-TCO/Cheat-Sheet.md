> Print. Drill. Compute.

---

## 🎯 TCO Equation

```
TCO = Acquisition + Operating + Disposal
```

| Phase | Examples |
|---|---|
| Acquisition | Price, freight, tariffs, install, tooling |
| Operating | Energy, consumables, maintenance, training, downtime |
| Disposal | Decommissioning, recycling, hazardous fees (-) resale value |

---

## 🧮 Should-Cost Build-Up

```
Raw materials + Direct labor + Overhead
+ Tooling amort + Logistics + SG&A
+ Supplier margin
= SHOULD-COST
```

Compare to quoted price → negotiate gap with facts.

---

## 🎯 Target Costing

```
Market price - required margin = TARGET COST
```

Design TO the target. Common in auto, electronics.

---

## 🔧 VA / VE

| Tool | When |
|---|---|
| Value Analysis | EXISTING products in production |
| Value Engineering | DESIGN of new products |

Common wins: tolerance relaxation · material substitution · part consolidation · standardization · COTS replacement

## 📊 Price Indices

PPI · CRU steel · Platts oil · semiconductor index

Indexed pricing: `New = Base × (Current index / Base index)`

Use in FP-EPA contracts on multi-year commodities.

---

## 🛡️ Hedging Instruments

| Tool | Definition |
|---|---|
| Forward | Custom OTC future date/price |
| Future | Standardized exchange-traded forward |
| Option | Right (not obligation) for premium |
| Swap | Exchange one cash flow for another |

🚫 Hedge **risk**, not for speculation. Treasury executes.

---

## 💵 Payment Terms

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Net 30 | Pay in 30 days |
| 2/10 Net 30 | 2% off if paid in 10 days (~36.5% annualized!) |
| Net 60/90 | Working capital lever |

🧠 SCF (supply chain finance) = buyer-facilitated supplier receivables financing.

---

## 📊 Investment Math

| Metric | Formula |
|---|---|
| ROI (Return on Investment) | (Gain - Cost) / Cost × 100% |
| Payback | Investment / Annual savings |
| NPV | Σ discounted cash flows - investment (positive = invest) |
| IRR | Discount rate where NPV = 0 |

## 🧯 Savings vs Avoidance

| Term | Example |
|---|---|
| Savings | $10 last yr → $8 this yr = $2 savings |
| Avoidance | Asked +15%, got +5% = 10% avoided |

🚫 Don't conflate.

---

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

- ✅ "Total cost of ownership, not unit price"
- ✅ "Should-cost build-up grounds the negotiation"
- ✅ "VA/VE to attack over-specification"
- ✅ "Indexed pricing on multi-year commodities"
- ✅ "Hedge known exposures via Treasury"
- ✅ "Report savings and avoidance separately"

## ⚠️ Usually Wrong

- ❌ "Lowest price wins"
- ❌ "100% fixed on volatile multi-year commodity"
- ❌ "Hedge to make money"
- ❌ "Avoidance = savings"
- ❌ "Net 120 to fragile small supplier"
