## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **B.** SRM is lifecycle, value, and risk-focused.
### Q2: **A.** Pareto — small number of strategic suppliers, big chunk of spend.
### Q3: **D.** Personal preference has no place in a scorecard.
### Q4: **B.** Supplier development is exactly the right play for a strategic supplier with a fixable gap.
### Q5: **B.** Quarterly Business Review.
### Q6: **D. All of the above.** All three are recognized US diversity categories.
### Q7: **B.** Tier-2 = your primary suppliers' diverse subcontractors.
### Q8: **B.** Fundamental capability gap + financial instability = development is throwing money away.
### Q9: **B.** Escalate one level at a time. Don't skip levels or go nuclear.
### Q10: **B.** Spend analysis → segment → deep SRM where it matters.
### Q11: **A.** Categories that move to leverage/commodity warrant a more transactional approach.
### Q12: **B.** New suppliers need first-article inspection and qualification audits before scale-up.
### Q13: **A.** PPM is the standard quality unit.
### Q14: **B.** Engage, escalate, document, prepare for contractual remedies. Module 7 reinforces this is ISM ethics in action.
### Q15: **B.** PIP + parallel dual-sourcing protects supply while giving the supplier a chance to recover.
### Q16: **B.** Full QBRs with exec sponsorship.
### Q17: **B.** Risk-based audit frequency.
### Q18: **B.** Standard corrective action, replace if it continues. Don't over-invest in Tier-3.
### Q19: **B.** US federal contracts and many enterprise customers require tier-2 reporting.
### Q20: **B.** Vested = outcome-based, win-win, shared incentives.
### Q21: **B.** Structured transition with knowledge transfer.
### Q22: **B.** Ethics scenario — decline + disclose + document. Module 7 will reinforce.
### Q23: **B.** Supplier-driven innovation contribution is a world-class metric.
### Q24: **B.** Co-developed scorecards work. Surprises don't.
### Q25: **B.** Exec sponsorship + QBR + escalation = governance.
### Q26: **B.** Treating everyone as strategic dilutes effort.
