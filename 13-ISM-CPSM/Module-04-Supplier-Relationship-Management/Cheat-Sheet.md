# 📋 Module 4 Cheat Sheet: Supplier Relationship Management

> Print. Drill. Use weekly.

---

## 🏷️ Supplier Tiering (Pareto)

| Tier | Label | % Suppliers | % Spend | Playbook |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Strategic / Partner | 1–5% | 30–50% | Joint roadmap · Exec sponsorship · QBRs |
| 2 | Preferred / Key | 10–20% | 30–40% | Scorecards · Multi-year contracts · Improvement |
| 3 | Approved / Transactional | 30–50% | 15–20% | Standard terms · Performance monitoring |
| 4 | Tail / Spot | 30–50% | 5–10% | Catalog / P-card · Rationalize |

| Dimension | Example Metric |
|---|---|

## 🤝 Partnership vs Transactional

| | Transactional | Partnership |
|---|---|---|
| Horizon | Short | Multi-year |
| Info | Minimal | Open |
| Risk | Each | Shared |
| Cost | Unit price | TCO |
| Innovation | Buyer-led | Co-developed |
| Governance | PO + invoice | QBR + exec sponsor |

## 🛠️ Supplier Development Triggers

INVEST when:

- Strategic tier
- High switching cost
- Capability gap is fixable
- Supplier is motivated
- Positive ROI (Return on Investment)

DON'T invest when:

- Tier-3/4
- Fundamental tech mismatch
- Supplier financially fragile
- Many alternates exist

## 🏭 Audit Types

| Audit | Trigger | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Quality | All Tier 1–2 | Annual |
| Financial | Risk indicators | Annual / event |
| Compliance | Trade, FCPA, ESG | Risk-based |
| Social / ethical | High-risk regions | Annual |
| Cybersecurity | Data-access suppliers | Annual |
| First-article | New parts | Per part |

---

## 🚨 Escalation Ladder

```
Level 1: Buyer ↔ Supplier account manager
Level 2: Category manager ↔ Supplier sales director
Level 3: Supply VP ↔ Supplier VP
Level 4: Executive sponsors / C-suite
```

🚫 Don't skip levels.

---

## 📈 QBR Agenda

1. Scorecard performance
2. Open issues + escalations
3. Cost / innovation pipeline
4. Risk review
5. Forecast + outlook
6. Strategic initiatives (roadmap, ESG, diversity)
7. Action items + owners

---

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

- ✅ "Tier the supply base first"
- ✅ "Co-develop scorecards with suppliers"
- ✅ "Risk-based audit frequency"
- ✅ "Invest in supplier development for strategic gaps"
- ✅ "Use the escalation ladder"
- ✅ "Plan supplier transitions"
- ✅ "Decline and disclose gifts during negotiation"

## ⚠️ Usually Wrong

- ❌ "Treat all suppliers the same"
- ❌ "Switch immediately when they fail"
- ❌ "Skip to the supplier CEO (Chief Executive Officer)"
- ❌ "Diversity is just CSR"
- ❌ "Score everyone identically"
