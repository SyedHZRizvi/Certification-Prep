## 🧭 Core Negotiation Vocabulary

| Term | One-liner |
|---|---|
| BATNA | Best Alternative To Negotiated Agreement |
| ZOPA | Zone of Possible Agreement (overlap of acceptable ranges) |
| Reservation price | Walk-away point |
| Target price | What you want |
| Anchor | First number on the table |

---

## 🎯 Distributive vs Integrative

| Style | Pie | Outcome | Use For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Distributive | Fixed | Win-lose | One-time, commodity, leverage quadrant |
| Integrative | Expandable | Win-win | Strategic suppliers, long-term partnerships |

---

## 🪜 5-Phase Negotiation Process

```
Prepare → Open → Bargain → Close → Implement
```

80% of success = preparation.

## 🎭 Tactics & Counters

| Tactic | Counter |
|---|---|
| Anchor | Counter-anchor with data |
| Good cop / bad cop | Call it out |
| Nibble | "We can, what do we get?" |
| Take it or leave it | Silence; test if real |
| Bogey | Probe deeper |
| Salami | Bundle issues |
| Deadline pressure | Walk if BATNA permits |
| Silence | Sit with it |

## ⚖️ Commercial Law Map

| Law | Applies To |
|---|---|
| **UCC** | US sale of goods |
| **Common law** | Services contracts |
| **CISG** | Int'l sale of goods (signatories, unless opted out) |
| **FAR** | US federal government contracts |

UCC Statute of Frauds: goods ≥ **$500** → must be in writing.

---

## 💰 Contract Types & Risk

| Type | Cost-Risk Borne By |
|---|---|
| Firm Fixed-Price | Supplier |
| FP + Incentive Fee | Mostly supplier |
| FP + Economic Price Adjustment | Shared via index |
| Cost-Plus Fixed Fee | Buyer |
| Cost-Plus Incentive Fee | Mostly buyer |
| Cost-Plus Award Fee | Mostly buyer |
| Time & Materials | Buyer |

🧠 **Rule:** Fixed = supplier risk. Cost-plus = buyer risk. T&M = buyer risk.

## 🌪️ Force Majeure

Excuses performance during **unforeseeable, unavoidable** events (war, disaster, pandemic). Must be:

- Listed in clause
- Genuinely unforeseen
- Reasonable mitigation attempted

---

## 🛑 Termination Types

| Type | Compensation |
|---|---|
| Convenience | Work done + wind-down |
| Default / cause | Usually nothing; possible damages |
| Insolvency | Per law (often automatic) |
| Mutual | Negotiated |

---

## 📊 SLAs

Must be **specific · measurable · time-bound · consequence-defined · escalation-pathed**

Examples: 99.5% uptime · on-time delivery % · MTTR · first-call resolution

---

## ⚖️ Dispute Resolution Ladder

```
Direct negotiation → Executive escalation → Mediation → Arbitration → Litigation
```

| | Binding | Cost | Time | Confidential |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mediation | No | Low | Weeks | Yes |
| Arbitration | Yes | Med | Months | Yes |
| Litigation | Yes | High | Years | No |

---

## 🧪 Warranties

| Type | Source |
|---|---|
| Express | Explicitly stated |
| Implied: merchantability | UCC default, fit for ordinary use |
| Implied: fitness for purpose | UCC default, buyer relied on seller's skill |

Can be disclaimed with specific language ("AS IS").

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

- ✅ "Know your BATNA before entering"
- ✅ "Anchor with data when you have it"
- ✅ "Use integrative for strategic relationships"
- ✅ "Match contract type to scope clarity"
- ✅ "Engage legal early"
- ✅ "Negotiate → mediate → arbitrate → litigate"
- ✅ "Decline gifts of significant value during active negotiation"

## ⚠️ Usually Wrong

- ❌ "Sue first"
- ❌ "Fixed-price on undefined scope"
- ❌ "Sign supplier's draft without redline"
- ❌ "Lowest price = best deal"
- ❌ "Always anchor first" (only with data)
