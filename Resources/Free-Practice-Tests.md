## 📋 PRACTICE STRATEGY

### Phase 1: Module-Level Quizzes
- After each module, take its quiz.
- Aim for 10/12 minimum.
- **Don't proceed if scoring <8/12.**

### Phase 2: Domain/Multi-Module Tests
- After every 3-4 modules, take a free practice test on those topics.

### Phase 3: Full-Length Mock Exams
- 3-5 full-length mock exams in the final 2 weeks.
- **Simulate exam conditions** — timed, no notes, single sitting.

### Phase 4: Review > Practice
- For every wrong answer:
  1. Identify why you got it wrong (knowledge gap? Trick wording? Misread?)
  2. Add to flashcards
  3. Re-read relevant module section
  4. Try the question again 3 days later
