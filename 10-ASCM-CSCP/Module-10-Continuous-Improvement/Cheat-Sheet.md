## ⛓️ TOC 5 Focusing Steps

1. **Identify** the constraint
2. **Exploit** the constraint
3. **Subordinate** everything else to it
4. **Elevate** the constraint
5. **Repeat**, new constraint emerges

### Throughput Accounting
- T = Revenue − truly variable costs
- I = Inventory (money tied up)
- OE = Operating expense
- Net profit = T − OE
- ROI = (T − OE) / I

**Drum-Buffer-Rope** = constraint sets pace, buffer protects, rope releases at constraint speed.

## 🚀 Kaizen vs BPR

| | Kaizen | BPR |
|---|--------|-----|
| Pace | Continuous small | Discontinuous radical |
| Scope | Frontline | Cross-functional |
| Risk | Low | High |
| Driver | Bottom-up | Top-down |
| Improvement | 5-20% | 50-200% (or fail) |

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns To Recognize

- ❌ Lean = layoffs
- ❌ Six Sigma for manufacturing only
- ❌ Kaizen as a one-time event
- ❌ Stopping TOC at the first constraint
- ❌ OEE > 100%
- ❌ Ignoring non-financial perspectives (skipping BSC)
- ❌ BPR every problem (too risky)
