## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **D. Ocean**
Ocean is the cheapest per ton-mile but slowest.

### Q2: **B. Intermodal**
Single container across modes without rehandling = intermodal.

### Q3: **D. 4PL / LLP**
4PL orchestrates; doesn't own assets.

### Q4: **D. Perfect order**
Perfect = on-time + in-full + undamaged + correct docs.

### Q5: **B. Warehouse core operations**
Standard 6-step flow.

### Q6: **C. Zone picking**
Pickers stay; orders move.

### Q7: **B. E-commerce FC**
GTP shines in high-throughput parcel operations.

### Q8: **B. Customs-supervised, deferred duty**
Bonded = legally controlled, duty held until release.

### Q9: **D. Air**
Time-critical + high-value + low-weight = air's sweet spot.

### Q10: **B. Drop-shipping**
Manufacturer ships direct; retailer never holds inventory.

### Q11: **A. In-transit merge**
Pieces consolidated at a hub then forwarded.

### Q12: **A. TEU**
Twenty-foot Equivalent Unit.

### Q13: **A. ULDs**
Unit Load Devices used in air cargo.

### Q14: **B. High-velocity, predictable SKUs**
Speed + predictability make crossdock economical.

### Q15: **D. Lowest price**
Perfect-order definition has nothing to do with price.

### Q16: **B. Hybrid / mixed mode**
The right answer is matching mode to SKU urgency / value.

### Q17: **A. 48 × 40 (GMA)**
North American standard. EUR is European.

### Q18: **D. Return**
Reverse logistics = SCOR Return.

### Q19: **B. Multi-function outsourced logistics**
That's 3PL by definition.

### Q20: **D. Open-pit mining**
Mining is not a last-mile innovation.

### Q21: **B. Freight contract + receipt**
B/L documents the carrier-shipper agreement and goods receipt.

### Q22: **B. Oversized industrial loads**
Open trailer is for non-standard or oversized cargo.

### Q23: **B. Maximum cubic density**
Random storage maximizes density at the cost of findability.

### Q24: **A. On Time In Full**
The classic supplier-performance KPI.

### Q25: **A. Higher inventory but faster local delivery**
More nodes → more inventory but shorter customer legs.
