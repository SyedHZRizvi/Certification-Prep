## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **A. RFI**
RFI gathers market intel and capability data.

### Q2: **D. Strategic**
Custom + high impact + high risk → strategic.

### Q3: **B. Spend analysis**
Step 1. You can't strategize spend you don't understand.

### Q4: **B. Well-defined spec, price quotes**
RFQ = pure price competition on a known spec.

### Q5: **B. Operating, maintenance, disposal across lifecycle**
TCO captures the full lifecycle cost beyond price.

### Q6: **C. Strategic custom components**
Reverse auctions strip out the relationship that strategic items need.

### Q7: **D. Routine**
Low risk, low impact, high transaction volume → automate via e-catalog.

### Q8: **B. Ensure supply (dual-source, safety stock)**
Bottleneck strategy = supply security, not cost compression.

### Q9: **A. The buyer**
Cost-plus has the buyer absorbing cost overruns.

### Q10: **B. Service-Level Agreement**
SLA defines performance commitments and penalties.

### Q11: **A. Best Alternative To a Negotiated Agreement**
Your walk-away leverage in negotiation.

### Q12: **A. Quality and on-time delivery**
Dickson 1966 and modern scorecards both put these at the top.

### Q13: **B. Joint R&D, shared scorecards, equity**
Top of the SRM ladder = deepest collaboration.

### Q14: **B. Develop alternates and safety stock**
Bottleneck mitigation = de-risk supply.

### Q15: **B. TCO**
The four classic TCO buckets.

### Q16: **B. Competitive bidding / reverse auction**
High impact + low risk = use scale to drive price down.

### Q17: **D. Compute EOQ**
EOQ is an inventory technique, not a sourcing step.

### Q18: **B. Quality**
Quality has consistently ranked #1 since 1966.

### Q19: **A. Best pricing of any customer**
MFN guarantees price equality.

### Q20: **B. Defined and stable scope**
FP only makes sense when both parties can pre-price the work.

### Q21: **C. Procurement tech stack**
All three sit inside the modern source-to-pay platform family.

### Q22: **B. Hold firm on terms**
Strong BATNA = leverage to walk away if terms aren't met.

### Q23: **B. FX hedging + multi-currency**
The standard mitigation playbook for currency risk.

### Q24: **B. Deep partnership, multi-year, joint planning**
Strategic suppliers deserve relationship investment.

### Q25: **B. Competitive RFQs / reverse auctions**
Commodity + many suppliers + scale = leverage; use price competition.
