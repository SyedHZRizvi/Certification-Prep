- **Two passes:** First pass answer everything you know; second pass tackle flagged.
- **Eliminate wrong** answers first; pick best remaining.
- **Read every word** — "MOST," "BEST," "EXCEPT" change the answer.
- **Trust your prep.** No need to second-guess answers you nailed.
- **Hydrate, sleep 8 hours**, eat a balanced meal beforehand.
