## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **C. 11**
Incoterms 2020 has 11 terms.

### Q2: **A. Make goods available at seller's premises**
EXW = seller does the absolute minimum.

### Q3: **D. DDP**
DDP = seller does everything including import duties.

### Q4: **C. Insurance (Clause C minimum)**
CIF = CFR + insurance.

### Q5: **A. DPU**
DAT became DPU in 2020 for broader location applicability.

### Q6: **B. Clause A (broad)**
CIP requires Clause A. CIF only requires Clause C.

### Q7: **B. Containerized cargo**
FCA passes risk at the carrier, which is more accurate than FOB's "ship's rail" for containers.

### Q8: **B. 6**
HS is globally harmonized at 6 digits; national extensions go to 10.

### Q9: **B. Below-fair-value imports**
Anti-dumping protects against unfair price dumping.

### Q10: **B. Standard WTO rate between members**
MFN = Most Favored Nation. Default WTO rate.

### Q11: **B. Freight contract + cargo receipt**
B/L serves both as a contract and a receipt; sometimes also as a document of title.

### Q12: **B. Letters of credit**
UCP 600 governs LC processing rules.

### Q13: **B. Issuing bank**
Buyer's bank issues the LC at the buyer's request.

### Q14: **B. Repeated shipments**
Revolving LC auto-renews for recurring trade.

### Q15: **B. 60-day forward contract**
Lock a known rate for a known future obligation. Textbook hedge.

### Q16: **B. Defer duty**
FTZs defer duties; they don't eliminate them once goods enter the domestic market.

### Q17: **B. Temporary admission**
ATA Carnet = "passport" for trade samples and pro equipment.

### Q18: **B. Clear import and unload**
DAP = seller delivers ready for unloading; buyer handles unloading + import.

### Q19: **B. FTA preferential tariff**
Rules of Origin determine FTA eligibility.

### Q20: **A. Destination port (cost); origin port (risk passes at loading)**
CFR splits cost (to destination) from risk (passes at origin).

### Q21: **B. Trusted-trader / security programs**
All three are trusted-trader programs with mutual recognition.

### Q22: **B. FCA**
FCA passes risk when handed to the carrier at the agreed point.

### Q23: **B. DAP French port**
DAP = seller bears cost AND risk to French port. CFR splits the two.

### Q24: **B. Invoicing in matching currency**
Natural hedge offsets without derivatives.

### Q25: **B. Transaction value**
Primary WTO method; price actually paid or payable.

### Q26: **B. Adds its own payment guarantee**
Confirming bank stacks its guarantee on top of the issuing bank.
