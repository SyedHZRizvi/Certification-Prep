## 📜 Incoterms 2020 — All 11 (MEMORIZE)

### Any mode of transport (7)
| Code | Name | Seller / Buyer Split |
|------|------|---------------------|
| **EXW** | Ex Works | Seller does the minimum |
| **FCA** | Free Carrier | Seller hands to carrier (export cleared) |
| **CPT** | Carriage Paid To | Seller pays carriage; risk passes at origin carrier |
| **CIP** | Carriage AND Insurance Paid To | CPT + Clause A insurance |
| **DAP** | Delivered at Place | Seller delivers ready to unload |
| **DPU** | Delivered at Place Unloaded (was DAT) | Seller unloads at destination |
| **DDP** | Delivered Duty Paid | Seller does everything incl. duty |

### Sea & inland waterway only (4)
| Code | Name | Seller / Buyer Split |
|------|------|---------------------|
| **FAS** | Free Alongside Ship | Seller delivers alongside vessel |
| **FOB** | Free On Board | Seller until on board; risk at loading |
| **CFR** | Cost and Freight | Seller pays freight to dest; risk at loading |
| **CIF** | Cost, Insurance and Freight | CFR + minimum Clause C insurance |

🔥 **Cost ≠ Risk transfer point** for CFR / CIF / CPT / CIP. Always check both.

🔥 **2020 changes:** DAT → DPU. FCA permits onboard B/L endorsement.

## 📊 HS Code Structure

```
   85       8517      8517.13       8517.13.0000
   Chapter  Heading   Subheading    National extension
```
First 6 digits = global; last 2–4 = country-specific tariff line.

## 💳 Letter of Credit Cheat Sheet

### Parties (5)
- Applicant (buyer)
- Beneficiary (seller)
- Issuing bank
- Advising bank
- Confirming bank (optional)
- Negotiating bank (examines docs, pays)

### Types
- Sight (pays on presentation)
- Usance (pays at future date)
- Irrevocable (default)
- Revolving (repeat shipments)
- Standby (guarantee of last resort)
- Back-to-back (intermediary chain)

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns To Recognize

- ❌ Using FOB for containers (use FCA)
- ❌ Calling Incoterms "title transfer"
- ❌ Confusing tariff (rate) and duty (amount)
- ❌ Using CIF when buyer needs broad-form insurance (use CIP Clause A)
- ❌ Ignoring rules of origin and losing FTA benefit
- ❌ Treating UCP 600 as Incoterms
