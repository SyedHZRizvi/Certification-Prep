## ⚠️ ISO 31000 Risk Process

1. Establish context
2. Identify risks
3. Analyze (likelihood × impact)
4. Evaluate / prioritize
5. Treat (4 T's)
6. Monitor & review
7. Communicate (throughout)

### 4 T's of Treatment
- Tolerate · Treat · Transfer · Terminate

---

## 🛠️ FMEA, Failure Mode & Effects Analysis

```
   RPN = Severity × Occurrence × Detection
```
Each scored 1–10. High RPN = high priority.

   Ethical         ← Right to do
   Legal           ← Required by law
   Economic        ← Required to survive

---

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns To Recognize

- ❌ "Sustainability = recycling only"
- ❌ Confusing RTO and RPO
- ❌ Risk transfer mistaken for risk elimination
- ❌ Reporting scope 1+2 only and claiming net zero
- ❌ Ignoring multi-tier supplier visibility
- ❌ Treating ISO 14001 as a government law
- ❌ Pure efficiency without resilience
