## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **B. Backward flows for value recovery / disposal**
Reverse logistics is broader than returns — recalls, repair, EOL, packaging returns all count.

### Q2: **C. RMA authorization**
RMA pre-validates eligibility and triggers the label. Saves cost vs accepting any return.

### Q3: **B. Disposition decision**
Gatekeeping = "what happens next?" — restock, refurbish, remanufacture, liquidate, recycle, destroy.

### Q4: **B. Fully disassembled and restored, new-equivalent warranty**
Remanufactured typically comes with new-product warranty (Caterpillar Reman, OEM remanufactured parts).

### Q5: **B. Closed-loop**
Cores returned by buyers; CAT remanufactures and resells — products cycle back through their own supply chain.

### Q6: **B. Refuse**
7Rs: Refuse → Reduce → Reuse → Refurbish → Recycle → Recover → Dispose.

### Q7: **B. People, Planet, Profit**
Standard 3P / Triple Bottom Line framework.

### Q8: **B. Direct fleet/facilities**
Your boilers, your trucks, your generators = Scope 1.

### Q9: **B. Purchased energy**
Grid electricity, district heating = Scope 2.

### Q10: **C. Scope 3**
Purchased transportation from carriers is Scope 3 (upstream/downstream value chain).

### Q11: **B. Global corporate emissions standard**
GHG Protocol is the foundational accounting standard (WRI + WBCSD).

### Q12: **A. Logistics-specific GHG accounting**
GLEC Framework (Smart Freight Centre) provides mode-by-mode emissions factors.

### Q13: **C. 50–100×**
Air freight is dramatically more carbon-intensive than ocean per ton-km.

### Q14: **B. Environmental management systems**
ISO 14001 = EMS umbrella. Generic for any organization.

### Q15: **C. GHG quantification**
ISO 14064 series: Part 1 organization-level, Part 2 project-level, Part 3 verification.

### Q16: **B. Multi-stakeholder ESG reporting framework**
GRI is the most widely-used voluntary sustainability reporting standard.

### Q17: **B. Mandatory EU corporate sustainability reporting**
CSRD (effective phased from 2024) replaces the older NFRD and applies to far more firms.

### Q18: **B. Electronic waste end-of-life**
Waste from Electrical and Electronic Equipment directive — extended producer responsibility.

### Q19: **B. Loaded return trips**
Backhaul = revenue + emissions saved on the return leg.

### Q20: **B. Mode shift**
Moving freight from truck to rail (or rail to ocean) yields the biggest CO₂ wins per ton-km.

### Q21: **B. Firm-level**
Closed-loop = your value chain. Circular economy = systemic, society-wide design principle.

### Q22: **B. Last-resort after internal reductions**
Offsets are a complement, not a substitute. Frameworks like SBTi prioritize reductions.

### Q23: **B. Remanufacturing**
Disassemble + restore = remanufacture. Refurbish is lighter; recycle destroys form.

### Q24: **A. Diversion rate**
Diversion rate = waste diverted (recycled/reused/composted) / total waste.
