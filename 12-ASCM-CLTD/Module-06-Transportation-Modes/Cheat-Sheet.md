# 📋 Module 6 Cheat Sheet: Transportation Modes

> One page. Print it. Review weekly.

---

## 🚦 Mode Comparison Matrix

| Mode | Cost | Speed | Reliability | Flexibility | Emissions |
|------|------|-------|-------------|-------------|-----------|
| Truck | High | High | High | Very high | High |
| Rail | Low | Low-med | Med | Low | Low |
| Ocean | Lowest | Very low | Med | Low | Very low |
| Air | Highest | Very high | High | Med | Very high |
| Pipeline | Lowest (fluids) | Slow but continuous | Highest | None | Very low |
| Intermodal | Med | Med | Med | Med-high | Low |
| Parcel | High/unit | High | High | Very high | Med |

**Cost rank (cheapest → most expensive):** Pipeline → Ocean → Rail → Truck → Air

## 🚛 Truck Size Tiers

| Range | Mode |
|-------|------|
| <150 lbs | Parcel |
| 150–10,000 lbs | LTL |
| 10,000+ lbs / 10+ pallets | FTL |

**US HOS:** 11-hr drive, 14-hr duty, 10-hr off, 60/70-hr week.

## 🚢 Ocean Containers

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| TEU | 20' container equivalent |
| FEU | 40' container = 2 TEU |
| FCL | Full Container Load |
| LCL | Less-than-Container Load |
| BAF | Bunker (fuel) adjustment |
| CAF | Currency adjustment |
| GRI | General Rate Increase |
| THC | Terminal Handling Charge |

**Typical transits:**
- Asia → USWC: 11–16 days
- Asia → USEC (Panama): 26–35 days
- Asia → Europe (Suez): 28–35 days

## ✈️ Air Freight Math

```
Volumetric kg = (L × W × H in cm) / 6,000
Chargeable kg = max(Actual kg, Volumetric kg)
```

🧠 Air ratio is 1:6,000 cm³.

ULDs: LD3 (narrow-body), PMC/P1P (main-deck pallet).

## 🚂 Rail Terminology

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| COFC | Container On Flat Car |
| TOFC | Trailer On Flat Car (piggyback) |
| Doublestack | Two containers stacked (NA) |
| Drayage | Truck moves between rail/port and customer |

Rail is ~3-4× more fuel-efficient than truck per ton-mile.

## 🔄 Intermodal vs Multimodal

| Intermodal | Multimodal |
|------------|------------|
| Multiple modes, standardized equipment (container) | Multiple modes, ONE contract |
| Many BOLs | One BOL |

---

## 📦 Parcel Pricing Factors

- Zone (1–8)
- Weight (actual / dim)
- Service (Ground, 2-day, Overnight)
- Accessorials (residential, sig, oversized)
- Surcharges (peak, fuel, address correction)

🧠 Last-mile = 28–53% of parcel cost.

## 💰 Total Landed Cost

```
TLC = Product + Freight + Duties/Taxes + Insurance +
      Handling + Inventory carrying + Brokerage
```

🚨 Cheapest freight ≠ cheapest TLC.

---

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

Usually CORRECT:

- ✅ "Choose mode on total landed cost, not freight alone"
- ✅ "Intermodal for long-haul cost-savings (>700 mi)"
- ✅ "Air for high-value/urgent/perishable"
- ✅ "Pipeline most reliable; ocean cheapest per ton-mile"

Usually WRONG:

- ❌ "FTL = LTL"
- ❌ "Intermodal = multimodal"
- ❌ "Air is always uneconomic"
- ❌ "Last-mile is the cheapest leg"

---

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns

- ❌ Picking by freight rate alone
- ❌ Ignoring carrier financial stability
- ❌ Forgetting HOS impact on rates
- ❌ Treating zone-skipping as universally cheaper
