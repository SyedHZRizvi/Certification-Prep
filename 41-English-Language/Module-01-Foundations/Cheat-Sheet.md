## "To Be" Conjugation

| Pronoun | Positive | Negative | Question |
|---------|----------|----------|---------|
| I | I **am** tall. | I **am not** tall. | **Am** I tall? |
| You | You **are** ready. | You **are not** ready. | **Are** you ready? |
| He | He **is** here. | He **is not** here. | **Is** he here? |
| She | She **is** a nurse. | She **is not** a nurse. | **Is** she a nurse? |
| It | It **is** cold. | It **is not** cold. | **Is** it cold? |
| We | We **are** friends. | We **are not** friends. | **Are** we friends? |
| They | They **are** late. | They **are not** late. | **Are** they late? |

**Contractions:** I'm · you're · he's · she's · it's · we're · they're
**Negative contractions:** isn't · aren't · (am not has no contraction!)

## Top 3 Errors to Avoid

| Error | Wrong | Right |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Missing article | I am student. | I am **a** student. |
| Wrong verb order | I food eat. | I **eat** food. |
| He/She confusion | She is my brother. | **He** is my brother. |
