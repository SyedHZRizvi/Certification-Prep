## Vocabulary Learning Strategies Summary

| Strategy | How to Do It |
|----------|-------------|
| Spaced Repetition | Review: day 1 → day 2 → day 5 → day 12 → day 30 |
| Context Learning | Always learn words in full sentences |
| Word Families | happy → happiness → happily → unhappy |
| 5-Second Rule | Use new word in YOUR OWN sentence immediately |
| Thematic Groups | Learn: kitchen words, body words, family words together |
