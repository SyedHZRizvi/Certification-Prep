# Module 3 Cheat Sheet — Basic Grammar (A2)

---

## Article Rules at a Glance

| Article | Use When | Examples |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **a** | Before consonant sounds, first mention | a book, a cat, a university |
| **an** | Before vowel sounds (/a/,/e/,/i/,/o/,/u/) | an apple, an hour, an umbrella |
| **the** | Specific / known / only one / 2nd mention | the sun, the president, close the door |
| **(none)** | General plural / proper nouns / meals | Books are useful. / Pakistan / breakfast |

**Sound trap:** a university (sound = /juː/) | an hour (h is silent, sound = /aʊ/)

## Plural Rules

| Rule | Singular | Plural |
|------|----------|--------|
| +s | book, dog, car | books, dogs, cars |
| +es (s/sh/ch/x/z) | bus, dish, church | buses, dishes, churches |
| y → ies | city, baby | cities, babies |
| f/fe → ves | leaf, knife | leaves, knives |
| Irregular | man, woman, child, tooth, foot, person | men, women, children, teeth, feet, people |
| No change | sheep, fish | sheep, fish |

## Present Simple vs Present Continuous

| | Present Simple | Present Continuous |
|--|---------------|-------------------|
| **When** | Habits, routines, facts | Right now, temporary |
| **Signal words** | every day, always, usually, never | now, at the moment, currently |
| **Structure** | S + V(+s/es) | S + am/is/are + V-ing |
| **Negative** | S + don't/doesn't + V | S + am/is/are + not + V-ing |
| **Question** | Do/Does + S + V? | Am/Is/Are + S + V-ing? |
| **Example** | She works every day. | She is working now. |

**-ing spelling:** drop silent e: make→making | double consonant: sit→sitting

## Prepositions of Place

| Preposition | Meaning | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| in | inside | cat in the box |
| on | on surface | book on the table |
| at | specific point | at school / at home |
| under | below | shoes under the bed |
| behind | at the back | car behind the house |
| in front of | at the front | standing in front of the door |
| next to | beside | bank next to the post office |
| between | in the middle | park between school and hospital |
| above | higher than | fan above the table |

## Top 5 Grammar Errors (Urdu Speakers)

| # | Error | Wrong | Right |
|---|-------|-------|-------|
| 1 | Missing article | She is student. | She is **a** student. |
| 2 | He/she confusion | She is my brother. | **He** is my brother. |
| 3 | Missing -s (3rd person) | She work here. | She **works** here. |
| 4 | Wrong tense | I eat now. | I **am eating** now. |
| 5 | SOV word order | I food eat. | I eat food. |
