## 🏗️ Account Hierarchy (Where Things Live)

```
Account
└── Campaign  ← BUDGET, BIDDING, GEO (Generative Engine Optimization), SCHEDULE, NETWORKS
    └── Ad Group  ← KEYWORDS, AUDIENCES, ADS
        └── Keyword (with match type)
        └── RSA Ad
        └── Extensions
```

🧠 **Memorize**: Budget = campaign, NEVER ad group. Bidding = campaign too.

## 🎯 Match Types (2026)

| Type | Syntax | Use when |
|------|--------|----------|

| Phrase | `"keyword"` | Default safe middle |

| BMM | `+kw +kw` | ❌ RETIRED 2021, trap answer |

## 💰 Bidding Strategy Cheat Card

| Strategy | Use when |
|----------|----------|
| Manual CPC (Cost Per Click) | New account, full control |
| Maximize Clicks | Traffic only, no conv goal |
| Maximize Conversions | Have tracking + < 30 conv/mo |
| **tCPA** | Have CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) target + ≥30 conv/mo |
| **tROAS** | Ecom + values + 50+ conv/mo |
| Target Imp Share | Brand defense |

🚨 **MEMORIZE the 30-conversion rule**, below this threshold, tCPA starves.

---

## 📝 RSA Spec

```
Headlines:    up to 15 × 30 chars  (min 3)
Descriptions: up to 4 × 90 chars   (min 2)
Display Path: 2 × 15 chars  (optional)
```

**Hit "Excellent" Ad Strength by:**
- Filling all 15 + 4 slots
- Varying themes (benefit / proof / urgency / brand)
- 3+ keywords in headlines
- No duplicate phrasing

---

## 🏆 Ad Rank Formula

```
Ad Rank = Max CPC × Quality Score + Extensions impact
```

**Quality Score (1–10) components:**
1. Expected CTR (Click-Through Rate)
2. Ad Relevance
3. Landing Page Experience

🧠 Higher QS = lower CPC + better position. **QS 10 at $2 beats QS 5 at $4.**

## 📅 Weekly Negative Routine (15 min, every Monday)

1. Search Terms report → sort by Cost desc
2. Top 20 queries, was each relevant?
3. Irrelevant → add to shared negative list
4. Relevant but poor, consider new ad group

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns

- ❌ Premature tCPA (< 30 conv/mo)
- ❌ Display opt-in left on a Search campaign
- ❌ State-wide geo for local biz
- ❌ Brand + generic in same campaign
- ❌ 15 identical headlines
- ❌ No conversion tracking → Smart Bidding
