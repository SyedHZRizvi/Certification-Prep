## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **B. Demand-Side Platform**
DSP = where advertisers BUY programmatic inventory. SSP = where publishers SELL. Ad Exchange = the marketplace connecting them.

### Q2: **B. Supply-Side Platform**
Publishers (NYT, CNN, Hulu) use SSPs to expose their inventory to DSPs. Magnite and PubMatic are common SSPs.

### Q3: **B. The largest INDEPENDENT DSP**
The Trade Desk is the largest non-Google, non-Meta DSP. Strong on CTV, used by most agencies.

### Q4: **B. Google's enterprise programmatic DSP**
DV360 is part of Google Marketing Platform, a different product from Google Ads. Used by enterprise + agencies. Typical minimum spend ~$2K/mo.

### Q5: **B. SMB-friendly retargeting setup**
AdRoll is the classic "first programmatic platform a DTC brand tries." Self-serve, starts ~$300/mo.

### Q6: **B. Dynamic product retargeting (DTC ecom)**
Criteo's specialty: showing the exact product a user viewed, cropped from your catalog, with the abandoned-cart price, on every site they visit afterward.

### Q7: **B. Unifies customer data and syncs audiences to ad platforms in real time**
A CDP sits between your website/app/CRM and your ad platforms, pushing audience updates in seconds. Solves the lag problem.

### Q8: **B. 24-hour delay between converting and being removed from retargeting audiences**
Without a CDP, a customer who buys at 10am might keep seeing retargeting ads until the next day's audience sync. With CDP: removed within seconds.

### Q9: **B. 3**
Standard frequency cap: 3 impressions per user per day, 12 per week. More = harassment.

### Q10: **B. Feature the abandoned product + a discount/incentive**
Cart abandoners have proven intent. Show them the EXACT product they almost bought + a small incentive to close. This is the highest-converting retargeting tier.

### Q11: **B. 3 stages (day 1-3, 4-7, 8-14)**
Casper's playbook: aggressive product+discount → social proof → urgency → exclude after 15 days.

### Q12: **C. Exclude the user from retargeting**
Conversion likelihood drops to near-zero after ~15 days. Stop spending on them. Use the budget on fresh visitors.

### Q13: **B. 28-day click + 1-day view**
Pre-iOS-14 Meta default was 28-day click + 1-day view. Post-iOS-14 it shrank to 7-day click + 1-day view.

### Q14: **A. 7-day click + 1-day view**
Current default. The shorter window means modern Meta numbers underreport conversions vs. pre-2021 reports.

### Q15: **B. Credits ONLY the last ad clicked**
Last-click attribution gives 100% credit to the final ad clicked, ignoring all earlier touches. Easy to compute but heavily biased toward bottom-funnel channels.

### Q16: **C. 25-30**
Per Wynter's 2024 buyer research, modern DTC customers average 25-30 brand interactions before purchase. Single-touch attribution credits 1 of 30.

### Q17: **B. Marketing Mix Modeling**
MMM is a statistical model (often using regression) that estimates each channel's impact on sales over time. It works even without per-user tracking, using only aggregated data.

### Q18: **B. The TRUE causal impact of a channel**
Incrementality testing uses A/B holdout groups: half the audience sees an ad, half doesn't. The difference in conversion rate = the channel's incremental lift.

### Q19: **B. Made-For-Advertising**
MFA = sites built solely to harvest programmatic ad spend, with low-quality content, dense ads, and no real audience.

### Q20: **B. 20%**
The ANA's 2024 study estimated ~20% of open-web programmatic display spend goes to MFA sites. Block lists are critical.

### Q21: **B. Industry-standard ad verification tools**
DoubleVerify and IAS verify viewability, fraud, brand safety. Used by enterprise advertisers. SMB/DTC typically uses DSP-level block lists instead.

### Q22: **B. Connected TV, programmatic streaming inventory**
CTV = Hulu, Disney+, Roku, Peacock, etc. Streaming TV bought programmatically. Fastest-growing programmatic channel.

### Q23: **C. $50,000+/quarter**
CTV CPMs are $25-$60. To get meaningful reach, you need substantial budget. SMBs under $10K/mo should skip CTV.

### Q24: **B. 38%**
ASOS publicly shared at IAB Engage 2023 that Criteo's dynamic retargeting drove 38% of their retargeting conversions, by showing the exact product + size + price the user viewed.

### Q25: **B. Past purchasers (within last 30-60 days)**
Exclude recent buyers from retargeting to avoid wasting budget. Reactivation campaigns at 60-90 days are different (use a separate audience + offer).
