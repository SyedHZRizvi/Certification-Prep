# 📋 Module 10 Cheat Sheet: Capstone Daily Ops

> Print this. Tape it to your monitor. This is your operations bible during the 21-day capstone.

---

## 📅 21-Day Timeline

```
WEEK 1, FOUNDATION
  Day 1: Brief
  Day 2: Media brief + ICP validation
  Day 3: GA4 + conv tracking
  Day 4: Pixel + CAPI verify
  Day 5: Landing page
  Day 6: Creative (AI pipeline)
  Day 7: Campaign drafts in Ads Editor

WEEK 2, LAUNCH + LEARN
  Day 8: Google launch
  Day 9: Meta launch
  Day 10-14: Daily ops (no major edits in first 5 days)

WEEK 3, SCALE + DOCUMENT
  Day 15: Scale OR diagnose
  Day 16: DCT new creative
  Day 17: Refresh underperformers
  Day 18: Watch 5 Hotjar replays
  Day 19: Calc final metrics
  Day 20: Stop spend (let conv attribute)
  Day 21: Write deliverables
```

## 📊 Daily Ops Routine (15 min)

```
GOOGLE (5 min):
  ☐ Spend pacing OK?
  ☐ Disapproved ads?
  ☐ Broken URLs?
  ☐ Mondays: Search Terms → add 5-10 negatives

META (5 min):
  ☐ Spend pacing OK?
  ☐ Disapproved ads?
  ☐ Frequency > 4x? Refresh creative
  ☐ CPM > $25? Audience too narrow

CONVERSION (5 min):
  ☐ Today's CPA vs target
  ☐ 3-day trend
  ☐ Note one observation
```

## ⚠️ Don't Do These

```
❌ Skip the brief ("we'll figure it out")
❌ Launch without verified tracking
❌ Spend > $7/day on first 5 days
❌ Edit campaigns daily in week 1 (resets learning)
❌ Quit on day 7
❌ Hide small-sample caveats
❌ Compare to industry benchmarks too early
```

---

## 🎯 First 14 Days, What's Normal vs What's Wrong

| Metric | Normal | Diagnose |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Day 1-3 CTR | 1-5% | < 0.5% = creative issue |
| Day 1-3 CPM | $5-$25 | > $40 = audience too narrow |
| Day 1-3 Conv | 0-3 (small) | Don't panic, learning phase |
| Day 7 CPA | Could be 2-3x target | Diagnose, don't pause |
| Day 14 CPA | Should be ≤ target | If above: pivot |
