## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **B. Sponsored boosts of existing organic TikTok videos**
Spark Ads take an organic TikTok post (yours or a creator's with permission via a Spark Code) and run it as paid. The engagement (likes/comments) stays on the original post.

### Q2: **B. A Spark Code generated by the creator**
The creator goes to Settings → Privacy → Authorize Account → Generate code. They give you the code; you paste it into Ads Manager.

### Q3: **C. 30–80% higher CTR**
Spark Ads consistently outperform In-Feed Ads on CTR because they look organic and have inherited social proof. This is the 2026 industry consensus.

### Q4: **B. 1.5–3 seconds (faster than Meta)**
TikTok users scroll faster than Meta users. Hook within ~2 seconds or lose them. The For You algorithm penalizes weak retention quickly.

### Q5: **B. Events API**
TikTok calls its server-side conversion tracking the "Events API." Same purpose as Meta's CAPI: bypasses iOS ATT.

### Q6: **C. Underperforms native TikTok by ~40%**
Same vertical aspect ratio but different visual grammar. TikTok requires sound-first, faster cuts, trending audio, lower production value. Cross-posted Reels feel "off" on TikTok.

### Q7: **B. Meta's Advantage+ Audience (HINT-based)**
Smart Targeting is TikTok's algorithmic audience. You can provide interest seeds but TikTok serves outside them if it finds better converters.

### Q8: **B. 50/week per ad group**
Like Meta's 50-conversion learning phase or Google's 30-conversion threshold, TikTok Cost Cap needs ~50 conversions per ad group per week to stabilize.

### Q9: **C. TopView**
TopView is the full-screen video shown when the user opens TikTok. Premium awareness placement (and premium price).

### Q10: **B. $3–$10**
TikTok CPMs often beat Meta's. $3–$10 is a typical 2026 range for US DTC apparel. Above $15 is unusually high.

### Q11: **B. Thought-Leadership Ads (TLAs)**
TLAs let you boost an individual employee's personal LinkedIn post. They look organic and consistently outperform brand-page ads.

### Q12: **B. Look organic, build trust on individuals, viewers can engage on the personal post**
TLAs work because (1) they look like real posts in-feed, (2) viewers trust individuals more than brand pages, and (3) the engagement happens on the person's profile, building real connection.

### Q13: **B. Targeting specific named companies' employees**
Account List Upload is the foundation of LinkedIn ABM. Upload 1,000 target company names; LinkedIn matches their employees; you target by job function/title within those accounts.

### Q14: **B. 1,000+ accounts (varies by match rate)**
LinkedIn typically matches ~50-70% of uploaded accounts. Start with 1,000+ to ensure usable audience size after matching.

### Q15: **B. Interactive multi-step sponsored InMails with buttons**
Conversation Ads present a message + buttons. Each button click triggers the next message. Used heavily for lead gen and ABM nurture.

### Q16: **B. Targeting precision makes lead-to-deal conversion rate dramatically higher**
LinkedIn CPCs are 5-10x higher than Meta, but the leads convert to closed-won deals at much higher rates because targeting (job title, company size) is precise. Compare CPL or cost-per-closed-won, not CPC.

### Q17: **B. To build and manage ICP-matched prospect lists for sales teams**
Sales Nav is a sales tool, not an ad platform. It builds lists, maps buying committees, enables InMail, syncs to CRM. Pairs with Campaign Manager for paid + outbound coordinated motion.

### Q18: **B. 10x enterprise pipeline growth in 12 months**
Notion's case study (SaaStr Annual 2024) cited 10x enterprise pipeline growth driven by LinkedIn ABM (800 target accounts + CEO TLAs + Conversation Ads + HubSpot integration).

### Q19: **B. Awareness (TLA) → Consideration (video) → Conversion (Lead Gen Form)**
Sequential funnel + retargeting between stages. Awareness gets attention, consideration warms up, conversion captures lead with auto-filled form.

### Q20: **B. Profile data (name, email, company, title)**
Lead Gen Forms auto-fill from the user's LinkedIn profile. Conversion rates are 3-5x higher than off-platform forms because users don't have to type anything.

### Q21: **B. LinkedIn (Matched Audiences + TLAs)**
LinkedIn is the only platform with reliable job-title + company-size + seniority targeting at scale. For "VP Marketing at 500+ employee companies," LinkedIn is uniquely positioned.

### Q22: **B. TikTok + Meta Reels**
Visual product, sound-first content, hook-driven scrolling, TikTok and Meta Reels are the right pair for DTC consumer apparel. LinkedIn has no consumer activewear shoppers.

### Q23: **B. Website tracking pixel**
Insight Tag is LinkedIn's equivalent of the Meta Pixel, installs on your site, tracks visitors for retargeting and conversion measurement.

### Q24: **B. Polished studio shoots with brand-name-first openings**
TikTok punishes polished, brand-first creative. Wins: UGC, founder selfies, trending sounds, fast cuts, sound-on. Fails: studio b-roll, logo intros, slow brand storytelling.

### Q25: **B. 80% budget on TLAs from executives + ABM account lists**
Drift's publicly-shared SaaStr 2022 playbook: $50K-$200K/qtr, 80% on TLAs from CEO + 5 execs, 2,000 target accounts via Matched Audiences, Lead Gen Forms wired to HubSpot.
