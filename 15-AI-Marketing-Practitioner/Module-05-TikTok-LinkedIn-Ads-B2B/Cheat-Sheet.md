# 📋 Module 5 Cheat Sheet: TikTok + LinkedIn Ads

> One page. Print it. Tape it to your monitor.

---

## 🎵 TikTok Hierarchy

```
Business Center → Ad Account → Campaign → Ad Group → Ad
```

**Server-side tracking:** TikTok **Events API** (their CAPI)

---

## 🎬 TikTok Formats — When to Use

| Format | Use for |
|--------|---------|
| **Spark Ads** ⭐ | Default — boost organic UGC; 30-80% better CTR than In-Feed |
| In-Feed Ads | When no organic post to boost |
| TopView | Brand launches, big awareness |
| Branded Hashtag | Engagement campaigns |
| Catalog/Video Shopping | DTC ecom |

---

## ⚡ TikTok Hook (Tighter Than Meta)

```
1.5–3 seconds OR scroll
```

5 hooks that work:

- Direct address ("If you own a [X]...")
- Question
- Pattern interrupt
- Curiosity gap
- Stitch/Duet (open with screenshot)

## 🏗️ B2B Campaign Stack

```
1. TLA (Awareness)   — CEO posts boosted
2. Sponsored Video   — Consideration
3. Lead Gen Form     — Conversion
4. Conversation Ad   — High-intent ABM
```

Retarget between every stage.

## 🔌 ABM Full Stack

```
Sales Nav → Target list
→ Upload to Matched Audiences
→ Serve TLAs to those accounts
→ Lead Gen Form
→ HubSpot/CRM sync
→ Sales follow-up
```

## 🆚 TikTok vs LinkedIn

| You're selling... | Use |
|-------------------|-----|
| Consumer apparel | TikTok + Meta |

| Local services | Neither — Google |
| Coaching/courses | TikTok (TOFU) + LinkedIn (BOFU) |
| Job postings | LinkedIn |

---

## ⚠️ Top Mistakes

**TikTok:**
- ❌ Cross-posting Reels (40% worse)
- ❌ Polished studio look
- ❌ Brand-name-first openings
- ❌ Sound off / no captions

**LinkedIn:**
- ❌ Comparing CPCs to Meta (wrong math)
- ❌ Brand banners only (use TLAs)
- ❌ No retargeting between stages
- ❌ Not using Account List Upload
