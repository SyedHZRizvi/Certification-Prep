# 📋 Capstone 1 Deliverable Rubric: SEO + Content Audit

> The rubric for grading your SEO audit before publishing. Print this. Grade honestly.

---

## 🎯 The Deliverable

```
□ 15–20 page PDF audit document
□ Linked Google Sheet with raw keyword + technical data
□ 90-day content roadmap (8–12 articles, prioritized)
□ Published publicly on portfolio site (Notion / Webflow / etc.)
□ LinkedIn post with the link
□ Sent to subject + asked for testimonial
□ Case study writeup using P-C-A-R-L on portfolio
```

---

## 📐 The Section-By-Section Rubric

| Section | Failing | Passing | Excellent |
|---------|---------|---------|-----------|
| **1. Executive Summary** | Missing or >2 pages | 1 page, findings listed | 1 page, 5 ranked findings, 3 top recs, business framing |
| **2. Keyword Opportunity** | <1 page, generic | 3–4 pages, Ahrefs data, 1 competitor | 3–4 pages, 3+ competitor Content Gap, quick-win list, traffic upside calc |
| **3. Technical SEO Scan** | <2 pages, no tool used | 3–4 pages, Screaming Frog crawl + 5 issues | 3–4 pages, Top 10 issues w/ screenshots, S/M/L effort labels, business impact |
| **4. Content Gap Analysis** | Hand-wavy | 2 pages, listed gaps | 2–3 pages, topic clusters, specific article ideas, competitor proof |
| **5. Top-Page Audit** | Missing | 2 pages, 2–4 pages reviewed | 2–3 pages, 5–10 pages with specific recs, "fix these 3 first" callout |
| **6. 90-Day Content Roadmap** | <8 articles or no priority | 8–12 articles listed | 8–12 articles with: keyword, volume, KD, word count, priority, month, rationale |
| **7. Recommendations Summary** | Bullet list | Prioritized list | Top 10 in priority order, with S/M/L effort + "do these 3 first" |
| **Voice & Polish** | Reads like a template | Sounds like you | Distinct voice, no jargon dumps, business owner can read it |
| **Show Your Work** | Few screenshots | Most claims sourced | Every claim sourced; 30+ screenshots throughout |
| **Public Publishing** | On hard drive | Drafted on portfolio | Live + LinkedIn-posted + testimonial requested |

### To Pass: ≥8 cells at Passing or better, no Failing.
### To Be Recruiter-Grade: ≥7 cells at Excellent.

## 📊 The 90-Day Content Roadmap Template

```
| # | Article Title | Target Keyword | Volume | KD | Words | Priority | Month | Why |
```

8–12 rows. Priority labels: P0 = ship this month / P1 = next 30 days / P2 = stretch.

---

## 🚨 The 5 Trap Findings (NEVER Include)

```
1. ❌ "Bounce rate is high" (not a ranking factor)
2. ❌ "Need more pages" (without specific topics)
3. ❌ "Need more backlinks" (without specifics)
4. ❌ "Missing meta keywords" (deprecated since 2009)
5. ❌ "Site speed is bad" (without CWV numbers)
```

If any of these are in your draft, delete + replace with specific findings.

---

## 🏆 Power Phrases In Your Writeup

When writing recommendations:

- ✅ "Costing approximately X visits/month based on..."
- ✅ "Competitor [X] ranks #1 for this with a [length] article on [topic]..."
- ✅ "Quick win: ranked #11 for [keyword], moving to top 5 estimated to..."
- ✅ "Top priority: closes the #1 content gap..."

Avoid:

- ❌ "Should consider improving..."
- ❌ "Best practice is..."
- ❌ "Generally speaking..."
- ❌ "Could be better..."

## ✏️ Quick Self-Check Before Publishing

Cover the answers; recite:

1. The 7 sections of my audit ___
2. The 5 trap findings I avoided ___
3. My roadmap has ___ articles, prioritized as ___
4. My subject's permission level is ___
5. My Loom walkthrough is ___ minutes (optional)

If you can answer all 5, ship it. ✅

```
"I did an SEO + content audit for [Subject] as a portfolio piece.
They had [stat] and zero strategy. I pulled Ahrefs data, ran a 
Screaming Frog crawl, did content gap analysis vs 3 competitors. 
Found [#] technical issues, [#] missing keywords, and built a 
12-article 90-day roadmap. Estimated upside was [X] visits/month. 
If I redid it I'd [honest lesson]."
```

Practice this 5 times out loud. It's the version you'll use 30+ times.
