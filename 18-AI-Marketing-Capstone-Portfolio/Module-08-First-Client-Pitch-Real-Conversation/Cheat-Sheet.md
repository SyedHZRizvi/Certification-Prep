# 📋 Module 8 Deliverable Rubric: First-Client Pitch

> Print this. Grade before considering yourself "done with the course."

---

## 🎯 The Deliverables

```
□ 7-slide pitch deck built (Canva or similar)
□ 1-page proposal template ready to fill in
□ LinkedIn DM template + cold email template written
□ 30-min discovery call agenda (5/20/5) written
□ 5 specific prospects researched + named
□ 5 cold messages SENT this week (the real one)
□ Outreach tracking sheet active
□ Follow-up cadence set in calendar (Day 4, 10, 21)
□ Pricing tiers decided (3 tiers recommended)
□ 90-second pitch practiced out loud 5+ times
```

---

## 📐 Section-By-Section Rubric

| Component | Failing | Passing | Excellent |
|-----------|---------|---------|-----------|
| **Pitch Deck** | None | Drafted | 7 slides, hook open, customizable Slide 3, pricing visible |
| **Proposal Template** | None | Drafted | 1-page, signed-ready, payment terms included |
| **Outreach Scripts** | None | One channel | LinkedIn DM + cold email + follow-ups |
| **Prospect List** | None / vague | 3–4 named | 5 named with links + hook for each |
| **Messages Sent** | 0 | 1–3 | 5 sent with specific personalization |
| **Follow-Up Setup** | None | Reminded in head | Calendar set Day 4 / 10 / 21 |
| **Pricing** | Undecided | 1 tier | 3 tiers with rationale |
| **Call Agenda** | None | Drafted | Written + practiced |
| **Tracking** | None | Doc | Sheet with status + dates |
| **Mindset** | Avoiding sending | Sending hesitantly | Calm with 4/5 ghosting math |

### To Pass: ≥8 cells Passing+
### Recruiter/Client-Grade: All cells Excellent

## 🎯 The 7-Slide Deck

```
1. Hook + Identity (photo, name, who you are)
2. The 5 capstones grid (2x3 visual)
3. THE most-relevant capstone (customized per prospect)
4. My process (week by week)
5. What I cost (3 pricing tiers visible)
6. Why me (3 differentiators with proof)
7. Next steps + CTA (Call to Action) ("no hard feelings" option)
```

Built in Canva. ~1 hour.

## ✉️ Cold Outreach Templates

### LinkedIn DM (80 words)

```
Hi [Name],

I noticed you shipped [specific recent thing]. Really [specific reaction].

I'm a marketer who's been building a portfolio this year. 
One of the pieces is [1-line specific].

Curious, is [content/SEO (Search Engine Optimization)/paid/automation] something you're thinking 
about for [their product]? If not, no worries.

Either way, would love to follow your build.

[You]
```

### Cold Email (~100 words)

```
Subject: Quick note on [their product]

Hi [Name],

[Specific compliment, researched]

I'm a marketer who recently built [1-line case study reference].

If you're thinking about [specific area] for [their company], 
I'd love to share what I've learned, no pitch, just useful context.

15-min call this week or next?

[You]
[Portfolio link] (signature only)
```

## 📞 30-Min Discovery Call Agenda

```
0:00, Intro + agenda (3 min)
3:00, Your story + 1 capstone (5 min), STOP at 5 min
8:00, Their situation (15 min), ALL the time on them
23:00, Recap + project fit (3 min)
26:00, Next step (3 min)
29:00, End ON TIME
```

5/20/5 rule. You: 5 min. Them: 20 min. Next: 5 min.

## 📝 1-Page Proposal Template

```
# Proposal: [Project Name]
## For: [Client] | From: [You] | Date: [Date]

## What You Want
[Their words, 1-2 sentences]

## What I'll Do
- Phase 1 (Week 1): [Deliverable]
- Phase 2 (Week 2): [Deliverable]
- Phase 3 (Week 3-4): [Deliverable]

## What You'll Have
[Specific outcomes + artifacts]

## Timeline
[Start] → [End], X weeks

## Investment
$[Amount] structured as:
- $[X] upfront
- $[Y] midpoint  
- $[Z] delivery

## I Need From You
[Specific time / access / asset]

## Next Step
Reply "Approved", I'll send contract + invoice. Start [date].
```

## 🔄 Follow-Up Cadence

```
Day 0:  Initial message
Day 4:  Soft check ("wanted to make sure this didn't get buried")
Day 10: Value follow-up (share an insight, no ask)
Day 21: Final ("if not the right time, no problem")
Post Day 21: Move to monthly touchpoint
```

## 💰 Pricing Tiers (Decide Yours)

Suggested for early-career marketers:

| Tier | Scope | Range |
|------|-------|-------|
| **Small** | One deliverable (e.g., audit) | $500–$2,000 |
| **Medium** | Multi-week project | $2,000–$5,000 |
| **Large** | Monthly retainer or large project | $5,000–$15,000/mo |

Most clients pick the middle. Don't undersell.

---

## 🚨 Anti-Patterns To Avoid

- ❌ Generic openers ("Love your work")
- ❌ Multiple links in message 1
- ❌ Long pitch in cold message
- ❌ Sending 1 message and quitting
- ❌ Skipping research (each prospect deserves 5+ min)
- ❌ Talking about you for 20 min on discovery call
- ❌ No pricing in the deck
- ❌ 8-page proposals
- ❌ Begging / desperate follow-ups
- ❌ Not sending the messages (the cardinal sin)

---

## 🏆 Power Phrases For Outreach + Pitch

In cold messages:

- ✅ "I noticed you shipped [specific]..."
- ✅ "Curious, is [thing] on your roadmap?"
- ✅ "If not, no worries. Either way I'd love to follow your build."

In discovery calls:

- ✅ "Tell me about your business in 2 minutes."
- ✅ "Where are you stuck on [area]?"
- ✅ "What have you tried?"
- ✅ "If we worked together, here's what week 1 looks like..."

In proposals:

- ✅ "Reply 'Approved' and we'll start [date]"

Avoid:

- ❌ "I'd love to hop on a call to discuss synergies..."
- ❌ "I'm passionate about helping companies..."
- ❌ "Please let me know if interested..."
- ❌ "I can do anything you need..."

## 🎤 The Final 90-Second Pitch

```
"I'm a marketer who's spent 6 weeks building 5 case studies, 
SEO audit, paid campaign, automation, attribution, AI agent.
I work AI-native and always publish post-mortems.
Currently sending pitches to [audience type], looking for 1-2 
projects in the next month.
Curious, would [capstone type] be useful for [their company]?
If not, no worries."
```

5 reps. Memorize. Use 30 times.

## 🎯 The 30-Day Action Plan After Module 8

```
Week 1: Send 5 cold messages
Week 2: Follow up Day 4s + Day 10s; send 5 more new
Week 3: First discovery calls (likely); send 5 more new
Week 4: First proposals (likely) + signed (likely 1)
```

Sustainable rhythm: 5 new messages/week + follow-ups. Most students who do this for 30 days get at least 1 paid project.
