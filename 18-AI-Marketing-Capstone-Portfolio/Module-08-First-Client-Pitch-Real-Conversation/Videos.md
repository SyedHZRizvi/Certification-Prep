## ✅ After watching

Answer these in your notebook (without re-watching):

1. What's the 7-slide deck structure?
2. What's the 5/20/5 rule for discovery calls?
3. Why don't you send the portfolio link in cold message 1?
4. What's the follow-up cadence after no reply?
5. What's the difference between a proposal and a contract?

If you can answer all 5, you're ready to send the 5 cold messages. If not, re-watch Essential #2 and #3.
