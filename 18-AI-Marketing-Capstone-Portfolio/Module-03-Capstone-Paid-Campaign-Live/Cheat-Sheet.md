# 📋 Capstone 2 Deliverable Rubric: Live Paid Campaign

> The rubric for grading the campaign + post-mortem. Print this. Grade before publishing.

---

## 🎯 The Deliverable

```
□ Live campaign launched (Google + Meta or single channel)
□ ≥$150 spent, ≥14 days runtime
□ Conversion tracking installed + verified
□ 3+ creative variants per platform
□ Daily optimization log (14 entries)
□ Final results table (per channel + total)
□ Post-mortem write-up (1,500–2,500 words) published on public URL
□ LinkedIn carousel (6–10 slides) posted
□ Subject testimonial collected
□ Loom walkthrough (optional polish, recommended)
□ Case study page on portfolio site with all artifacts linked
```

---

## 📐 The Section-By-Section Rubric

| Component | Failing | Passing | Excellent |
|-----------|---------|---------|-----------|
| **Subject Choice** | Hypothetical | Real subject, verbal OK | Real subject, written permission, testimonial planned |
| **Brief Document** | None | Written | Written + dated before launch, includes success criteria |
| **Conversion Tracking** | Missing | Installed | Installed + verified + documented in post-mortem |
| **Creative Variants** | 1 per platform | 2–3 per platform | 3+ per platform, mix of AI-generated + UGC + static |
| **Live Runtime** | <7 days | 10–13 days | 14+ days with daily check-ins |
| **Daily Log** | Missing | Sporadic | 14 dated entries, decisions w/ rationale |
| **Final Numbers Table** | Vague claims | Listed numbers | Full table: spend, CPC, CPL, conversions, ROAS, by channel |
| **Post-Mortem Hook** | Generic open | Decent open | Opens with most interesting moment |
| **Failure Documentation** | Hidden | Mentioned | Failures featured prominently w/ screenshots |
| **Lessons Section** | Missing | Generic | 3+ specific actionable lessons |
| **Public Publication** | Not published | Drafted | Public URL + LinkedIn carousel posted |
| **Voice & Honesty** | Spun positive | Neutral | Honest, scrappy, vulnerable |

### To Pass: ≥9 cells at Passing+ with 0 Failing
### Recruiter-Grade: ≥9 Excellent, 0 Failing

## 🚨 Pre-Launch Checklist

```
□ Subject confirmed + written permission
□ Brief document complete + dated
□ Google Ads + Meta Ads accounts created
□ Billing added (both platforms)
□ Daily $/day cap set
□ Google conversion tracking: tag installed + tested
□ Meta Pixel: installed + Events Manager shows test event
□ 3 creatives per platform ready (mix UGC / AI / static)
□ Landing page audited (mobile-friendly, conversion path works)
□ Negative keyword seed list (Google)
□ Audience built (Lookalike or Interest, on Meta)
□ Daily calendar slot booked (10 min × 14 days)
□ Day-1 launch screenshot folder ready
```

## ✍️ Post-Mortem Structure (1,500–2,500 words)

```
1. Hook              (~150 words, most interesting moment)
2. The Setup         (~300 words, subject, goal, budget)
3. The Plan          (~250 words, brief + creatives + audience)
4. The 14-Day Story  (~700 words, condensed log w/ screenshots)
5. The Numbers       (~300 words, final results table)
6. What Worked / What Didn't (~300 words, specific, screenshotted)
7. Lessons           (~200 words, honest, actionable)
8. Takeaways         (~100 words, what others can learn)
```

## 📐 LinkedIn Carousel Outline (10 slides)

```
1. Cover: "I spent $200 on Google + Meta. Here's what happened."
2. Challenge: subject + goal (1 sentence)
3. Plan: budget split + creatives shown
4. Day 3 scare (screenshot)
5. The pivot (what you changed)
6. Day 14 results (the table)
7. What worked (image + 1 line)
8. What didn't (image + 1 line)
9. Lessons (3 bullets)
10. CTA: "Full case study: [link]"
```

Built in Canva. 1:1 ratio. Posted with text hook + DM ask.

## 🚨 Anti-Patterns To Avoid

- ❌ Skipping conversion tracking
- ❌ Single creative per platform (no variant test)
- ❌ Pausing on day 2 because of "bad numbers" (give it 5+ days)
- ❌ Hiding failures in the writeup
- ❌ Vague results ("got some leads")
- ❌ Stock photos as the only creative
- ❌ No public URL (drafted but not published)
- ❌ No LinkedIn carousel (=no discovery)
- ❌ Polishing forever (=never shipping)

---

## 🏆 Power Phrases For Post-Mortem & Pitch

- ✅ "Day 3, I'd spent $40 with zero conversions..."
- ✅ "Two of three creatives bombed. Here's the third..."
- ✅ "If I redid it, I'd skip [X] and..."
- ✅ "Honest result: $X net, [pos/neg] ROAS, [N] paid customers."
- ✅ "The data showed [audience + creative] would scale; the spend wasn't enough to prove it..."

Avoid:

- ❌ "I successfully managed..." 
- ❌ "Best practice is..."
- ❌ "Overall a good experience..."
- ❌ "Despite some challenges..."

## 🎤 90-Second Recruiter Pitch (Practice Out Loud)

```
"I spent $200 of my own money on Google + Meta for [Subject's product]. 
14-day campaign, 3 creatives per platform. Spent $186. Got [N] leads 
at $[X] CPL, [Y] paying customers. ROAS came in [positive/negative]. 
The UGC-style Meta variant won by 3x; Carousel bombed. If I scaled to 
$2,000/mo, here's what I'd change: [specific things]."
```

Practice 5 times. This is your money pitch.
