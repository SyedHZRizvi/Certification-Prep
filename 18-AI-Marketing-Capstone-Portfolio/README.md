## ⚠️ The 7 Most Common Reasons Students Stall On This Course

1. ❌ **Picked a "fake" business for Capstone 2 (SEO audit).** The audit ends up generic. Pick a *real* business — yours, a friend's, or a public indie product whose owner will reply on Twitter.
2. ❌ **Skipped the $200 paid-ads budget.** A free trial is not the same case study. Spend the money. It's the cheapest tuition you'll ever pay.
3. ❌ **Didn't ship the post-mortem.** The campaign isn't the deliverable — the *write-up* is. No blog post or carousel = no portfolio piece.
4. ❌ **Built the AI agent but didn't host the repo / Loom.** If a recruiter can't see it, it doesn't exist.
5. ❌ **Polished forever instead of shipping.** "Excellent draft" beats "perfect plan." The rubrics in each Cheat-Sheet.md tell you what good-enough looks like — ship at that bar, iterate later.
6. ❌ **Rebuilt LinkedIn but never posted.** Module 7 isn't done at "headline rewritten." It's done at "5 posts live."
7. ❌ **Avoided the cold-message step in Module 8.** This is the only step that turns the portfolio into income. Most students stop one inch short of the line. Don't.
