## Exercise 8 — Professional Titles (Gender Agreement)
*Write the correct form of address.*

2. You are addressing Mr. Ahmed Khan (male manager): ___________
3. You are addressing Director Shahida Parveen (female): ___________

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. ڈاکٹر صاحبہ / ڈاکٹر عائشہ صدیقی صاحبہ

</details>
