## The Urdu Alphabet (حروفِ تہجی)

| 39 | ے | بڑی یے | e / ai | "e" egg |

*Retroflex = tongue curled back against roof of mouth — no English equivalent*

## Essential Greetings (سلام دعا)

| Urdu | Transliteration | Meaning | Response |
|------|-----------------|---------|---------|
| السلام علیکم | As-salamu alaykum | Peace be upon you | وعلیکم السلام (Wa alaykum as-salam) |
| آداب | Adaab | Respectful greetings | آداب / جی آداب |
| جی | Ji | Yes / respectful acknowledgment | — (used as filler/response) |
| شکریہ | Shukriya | Thank you | کوئی بات نہیں (Koi baat nahin — No problem) |
| بہت شکریہ | Bahut shukriya | Thank you very much | — |
| خوش آمدید | Khush aamdeed | Welcome | شکریہ (Shukriya) |
| معاف کریں | Maaf karein | Excuse me / Sorry | کوئی بات نہیں |
| براہ کرم | Baraah karam | Please | — |
| اللہ حافظ | Allah Hafiz | Goodbye (May Allah protect you) | اللہ حافظ |
| خدا حافظ | Khuda Hafiz | Goodbye (older form) | خدا حافظ |

## Honorific Pronouns (ضمیر)

| Pronoun | Urdu | Transliteration | Register | Use With |
|---------|------|-----------------|----------|---------|
| Formal "you" | آپ | Aap | Formal/Respectful | Elders, strangers, bosses, in-laws |
| Familiar "you" | تم | Tum | Informal | Friends, peers, younger people |
| Intimate "you" | تو | Tu | Intimate/Poetic | Very close friends, children, God in poetry |

**Golden Rule**: When in doubt — use **آپ**. You will never offend by being too formal.

## Script: Four Letter Forms

| Position | Description | Example (letter ب) |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| Isolated | Letter standing alone | ب |
| Initial | Beginning of a word | بَ (before next letter) |
| Medial | Middle of a word | ـبـ (connected both sides) |
| Final | End of a word | ـب (connected on left side only) |
