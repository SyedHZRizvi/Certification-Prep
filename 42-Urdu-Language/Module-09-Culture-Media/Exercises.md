## Exercise 1 — Ghazal Structure
*Label each element of this ghazal description.*

1. The first couplet where both lines share the rhyme: ___________
2. The final couplet where the poet includes their pen name: ___________
3. The poet's pen name used in the مقطع: ___________
4. The repeated refrain word at the end of every verse: ___________
5. The rhyme immediately before the radif: ___________

**Options:** ردیف / مطلع / تخلص / مقطع / قافیہ

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. مطلع (matla')
2. مقطع (maqta')
3. تخلص (takhallus)
4. ردیف (radif)
5. قافیہ (qaafiya)

</details>

## Exercise 7 — Poetry Analysis
*Read the following verse by Faiz Ahmed Faiz and answer the questions:*

**مجھ سے پہلی سی محبت مری محبوب نہ مانگ**

1. What is the speaker asking of the beloved?
2. What does this suggest has changed for the speaker?
3. What register is this verse written in — formal, literary, or colloquial?

<details>
<summary>Discussion Notes</summary>

1. The speaker is asking the beloved not to demand the same intense, single-minded love as before.
2. The speaker has encountered the world's suffering (poverty, oppression) and can no longer focus only on personal love.
3. Literary/formal — classical Urdu poetic register.

</details>
