## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **B. $50M**
Disclosed in Airbnb's 2022 investor day. The figure is what the restructure saved annually on vendor consolidation.

### Q2: **C. Hub-and-spoke**
The dominant 2026 structure for mid-to-large companies. Central shared services + embedded execution.

### Q3: **B. Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed**
Memorize. R = does the work. A = ultimately answerable. C = gives input. I = kept informed.

### Q4: **B. Accountable**
Marketing owns the *use case*, they're Accountable for MMM. Data engineering is *Responsible* for execution.

### Q5: **D. ~12,000**
Brinker's 2024 map showed ~12,000 vendors. The number has grown every year since 2011.

### Q6: **B. 30%**
Per the 30-30-30-10 rule of thumb. Infrastructure includes CDP, warehouse, sGTM, BI.

### Q7: **B. Experimental**
The 10% reserved for piloting new categories, keeps the stack adaptive.

### Q8: **B. Capability gap**
The strategist's red flag. Activation without measurement = unprovable ROI.

### Q9: **B. Strategic differentiator + capacity + data is competitive asset**
The build framework. If any leg is missing, default to buy.

### Q10: **C. 18–24 months**
Most build vs buy ROI breaks even in 18–24 months. Many builds never reach break-even because the vendor would have improved faster.

### Q11: **B. "What did we spend and what did it return?"**
The CFO's anchor question. Always lead with the spend-and-return figure.

### Q12: **D. CEO's favorite metric**
Five canonical CFO questions: spend/return, channel mix changes, incrementality, next allocation, leading indicator. Not "CEO's favorite."

### Q13: **C. Internal Slack message count**
Not a business metric. The scorecard is for revenue + cost + retention measures.

### Q14: **B. Minimum sustainability bar**
LTV:CAC < 3:1 = unsustainable. > 5:1 may mean under-investment in growth.

### Q15: **C. Consulted (Finance is Accountable)**
Procurement / Finance owns the contract. Marketing is Consulted on terms relevant to use case.

### Q16: **A. Staying purely technical**
The hardest transition in marketing. Senior strategists are measured by team output, not personal execution.

### Q17: **B. $180K–$280K**
2026 US blended Director compensation. Levels.fyi / Built In data.

### Q18: **B. Dedicated AI Ops team in hub**
The 2024–2026 emerging variant. Centralizes LLM prompts, GenAI creative production, and guardrails.

### Q19: **B. Allocating recurring tool costs to consuming spokes**
FinOps practice for fairness + accountability. Spokes that use more pay more.

### Q20: **C. 90 days**
Standard renewal practice. Below 60 days you've lost negotiating leverage.

### Q21: **B. ~$360K (1.8% of revenue)**
Per the cost-stack example in the Reading. Typical mid-market DTC range: 1–3% of revenue for stack tools.

### Q22: **C. Email / SMS / lifecycle orchestration**
The leading 2026 vendors in lifecycle marketing.

### Q23: **B. SQL + GA4 + Python**
Foundation skills for the analyst stage. Without these, you cannot advance.

### Q24: **B. $400K–$1M+**
Levels.fyi 2026 US CMO compensation range. Public-company CMOs at the top of the range can exceed $5M with equity.

### Q25: **B. Quarterly for >$50K/year tools**
The recommended cadence. Quarterly catches over-spending early without disrupting stable tools.

### Q26: **B. CFO as the strategist's best internal ally**
The senior-strategist mindset. The CFO supports honest measurement and well-defended budgets.
