## ✅ After watching

Answer these in your notebook (without re-watching):

1. State the STP framework in one sentence each.
2. What is the difference between a Key Result and an Initiative?
3. Give an example of a behavioral segmentation variable that would beat a demographic one.
4. Pick a company you know and identify its likely North Star metric. Justify.
5. What is one Porter force currently compressing margins in your industry?

If you can answer all 5 in under 5 minutes, you're ready for the [Quiz](./Quiz.md).
