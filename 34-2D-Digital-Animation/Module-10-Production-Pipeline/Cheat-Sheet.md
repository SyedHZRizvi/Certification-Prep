# ⚡ Module 10 Cheat Sheet — Production Pipeline

---

## 2D Studio Pipeline Stages

| # | Stage | Key Output |
|---|-------|-----------|
| 1 | Development | Concept, bible, character designs |
| 2 | Pre-Production | Scripts, storyboards, animatics |
| 3 | Layout | Camera staging, background placement, character guides |
| 4 | Rough Animation | Key poses, rough in-betweens (timing & performance) |
| 5 | Cleanup | Clean, on-model lines redrawn over roughs |
| 6 | Ink & Paint / Color | Digital color fill applied to clean lines |
| 7 | Composite | Characters + BG + effects assembled; color grade |
| 8 | Sound & Music | Final audio mix |
| 9 | QC / Delivery | Quality check → master delivery |

---

## Animatic: The Most Important Stage

> Change at storyboard/animatic = hours of work
> Change after rough animation = weeks of work

The animatic catches timing, pacing, and story problems **before any animation is drawn.**

---

## Software Landscape

| Software | Use | Example Shows |
|----------|-----|--------------|
| **Toon Boom Harmony** | Full broadcast pipeline | Simpsons, Rick and Morty, My Little Pony |
| **Adobe Animate** | Commercial, YouTube, indie | Hazbin Hotel (Flash era), Archer |
| **TVPaint** | High-quality hand-drawn | Cartoon Saloon (Kells, Song of the Sea) |
| **After Effects** | Compositing | Almost universal |
| **Toon Boom Storyboard Pro** | Storyboarding | Major North American studios |
| **Clip Studio Paint** | Drawing | Indie animators |
| **Blender (Grease Pencil)** | 2D-in-3D indie | Independent films |

---

## Harmony vs. Animate

| | Harmony | Animate |
|-|---------|---------|
| Price | Higher | CC subscription |
| Pipeline | Complete (draw → composite) | Needs AE for composite |
| Industry use | Major broadcast | Commercial, indie |
| Deformers | Advanced | Standard |
| Learning curve | Steeper | More accessible |

---

## Broadcast Studio vs. YouTube Creator

| | Broadcast | YouTube |
|-|-----------|---------|
| Team | 20–200+ | 1–5 |
| Time per episode | 6–24 months | 1–8 weeks |
| Budget | $250K–$1M+ | $0–$50K |
| Delivery | ProRes + timecode | H.264 MP4 |
| Pipeline | Fully specialized depts | One person wears all hats |

## Model Sheet Contents

- Front, 3/4, side, back views
- Multiple expressions
- Size comparison chart
- Exact color specifications
- Notes on stylistic rules

---

## Color Script

A visual map assigning a palette to each scene for emotional consistency across the full production. Created in development; used through ink & paint.
