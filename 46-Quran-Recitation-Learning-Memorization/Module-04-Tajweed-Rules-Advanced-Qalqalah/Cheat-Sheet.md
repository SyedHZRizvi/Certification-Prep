# Module 4 Cheat Sheet — Advanced Tajweed

## Qalqalah — 5 Letters

**Mnemonic:** قُطُب جَد

| Letter | Name | Strength |
|--------|------|---------|
| ق | Qaf | Strongest |
| ط | Ta-heavy | Strong |
| ب | Ba | Medium |
| ج | Jeem | Medium |
| د | Dal | Light |

- **Qalqalah Sughra:** Letter + Sukoon mid-word → light bounce
- **Qalqalah Kubra:** Letter at verse-end Waqf → strong bounce

---

## Meem Sakin Rules

| Rule | After مْ | Action | Ghunnah |
|------|---------|--------|---------|
| Ikhfa Shafawi | ب | Conceal Meem | Yes 2 beats |
| Idgham Shafawi | م | Merge Meem | Yes 2 beats |
| Izhar Shafawi | Anything else | Clear Meem | No |

---

## Lam Rule — Allah's Name

| Context | اللَّه Lam | Example |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| After Fatha | Thick (heavy) | قَالَ اللَّهُ |
| After Damma | Thick (heavy) | يَعبُدُ اللَّهَ |
| After Kasra | **Thin (light)** | بِسمِ اللَّهِ |

---

## Waqf Symbols

| Symbol | Name | Action |
|--------|------|--------|
| م | Lazim | MUST stop |
| ط | Mutlaq | Good to stop |
| ج | Jaiz | May stop |
| لا | La | Do NOT stop |
| ﴾ | Verse end | Sunnah to stop |

---

## Ra Rules (Quick Summary)

- Ra with Fatha/Damma → **Thick**
- Ra with Kasra → **Thin**
- Ra with Sukoon after Fatha/Damma → **Thick**
- Ra with Sukoon after Kasra → **Thin**
