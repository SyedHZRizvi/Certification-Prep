# Module 2 Cheat Sheet — Harakat & Short Vowels

## The Eight Harakat at a Glance

| Harakat | Mark | Sound | Urdu Name | Position |
|---------|------|-------|-----------|---------|
| Fatha | ـَ | short "a" | زَبَر Zabar | Above |
| Kasra | ـِ | short "i" | زِیر Zair | Below |
| Damma | ـُ | short "u" | پِیش Pesh | Above |
| Sukoon | ـْ | no vowel | جَزم Jazm | Above |
| Shaddah | ـّ | double letter | تَشدِید Tashdeed | Above |
| Fathatan | ـً | "-an" | تنوین فتحہ | Above |
| Kasratan | ـٍ | "-in" | تنوین کسرہ | Below |
| Dammatan | ـٌ | "-un" | تنوین ضمہ | Above |

---

## Long vs Short Vowels

| Short | Mark | Duration | + Letter → Long | Long Sound |
|-------|------|----------|-----------------|------------|
| Fatha ـَ | above | 1 beat | + Alif ا | ā (2 beats) |
| Kasra ـِ | below | 1 beat | + Ya ي | ī (2 beats) |
| Damma ـُ | above | 1 beat | + Waw و | ū (2 beats) |

## Shaddah Rule

Shaddah = 2 consonants merged into 1 written letter  
First consonant: implied Sukoon  
Second consonant: carries the following vowel  

Example: رَبِّ = ra + b(Sukoon) + b(Kasra) + ي → "rab-bi"

---

## Tanween Rule

Tanween = double harakat at end of nouns/adjectives  
Adds "n" to the vowel: -an / -in / -un  
Only on nouns and adjectives (NOT verbs or pronouns)

## Reading Speed Rule

| Mark | Beats |
|------|-------|
| Short vowel | 1 beat |
| Long vowel | 2 beats |
| Madd (Module 3) | 4–6 beats |
