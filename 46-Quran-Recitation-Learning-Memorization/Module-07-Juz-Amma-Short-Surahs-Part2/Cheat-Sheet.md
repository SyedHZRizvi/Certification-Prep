## An-Nasr (110) — Response to Victory

**Three actions after success:**
1. Glorify Allah (سَبِّح)
2. Praise Allah (بِحَمدِ رَبِّك)
3. Seek forgiveness (استَغفِر)

→ Humility, not pride.

---

## Al-Kafirun (109) — The Structure

- Verse 2-3: Present refusal — "I don't worship what you worship now"
- Verse 4-5: Future commitment — "I will never be a worshipper of what you worship"
- Verse 6: Principle — "Your religion is yours; mine is mine"

---

## Al-Ma'un (107) — Signs of the Denier

| Sign | Behavior |
|------|---------|
| Religious denial | Denies Day of Judgment |
| Behavioral sign | Pushes away orphans |
| Social sign | Doesn't encourage feeding the poor |
| Prayer sign | Prays heedlessly or for show |
| Community sign | Withholds simple neighborly help |

---

## Al-Fil (105) — Timeline

**570 CE:** Abraha (Abyssinian governor) marches to Mecca with elephant army → birds destroy them → Mecca/Kaaba saved.
This was the **year the Prophet ﷺ was born**.
