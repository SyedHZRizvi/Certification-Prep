# Module 6 — Juz Amma: Short Surahs — Part 1

## 🌟 The Five Jewels of Juz Amma

Juz Amma (جُزء عَمَّ — "the thirtieth part") is the last and shortest section of the Quran, and the place where new learners traditionally begin. Its surahs are mostly short, powerful, and perfectly crafted — easy to memorise, profound to understand.

In this module you will learn five of the most important short surahs:

| Surah | Number | Verses | Theme |
|-------|--------|--------|-------|
| Al-Ikhlas | 112 | 4 | Pure monotheism — who is Allah |
| Al-Falaq | 113 | 5 | Seeking refuge from external evils |
| An-Naas | 114 | 6 | Seeking refuge from internal whispers |
| Al-Kawthar | 108 | 3 | The fountain of abundance |
| Al-Asr | 103 | 3 | The formula for human success |

These five surahs together cover the complete theological framework of Islam: who Allah is (112), refuge from evil (113–114), divine gifts (108), and the path to success (103).

---

## 📖 Surah Al-Ikhlas — سُورَةُ الإِخلَاص (112)

<div style="background:linear-gradient(135deg,#f0fdf4,#dcfce7);border:2px solid #34d399;border-radius:16px;padding:2rem;margin:1.5rem 0;text-align:center;">
<div style="font-size:2.2em;direction:rtl;line-height:3;font-family:'Amiri Quran','Scheherazade New','Traditional Arabic',serif;color:#064e3b;">

بِسۡمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحۡمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

قُلۡ هُوَ ٱللَّهُ أَحَدٌ ﴿١﴾

ٱللَّهُ ٱلصَّمَدُ ﴿٢﴾

لَمۡ يَلِدۡ وَلَمۡ يُولَدۡ ﴿٣﴾

وَلَمۡ يَكُن لَّهُۥ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌ ﴿٤﴾

</div>
</div>

### Verse-by-Verse

| Verse | Translation | Key Arabic |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| 1 | Say: He is Allah, One | أَحَد = One (absolute, not merely "first") |
| 2 | Allah, the Eternal Refuge (As-Samad) | الصَّمَد = the one all depend on, who depends on none |
| 3 | He neither begot nor was He begotten | لَمۡ يَلِدۡ = did not beget; لَمۡ يُولَدۡ = was not born |
| 4 | And there is none equal to Him | كُفُوًا = equal, comparable, equivalent |

**The Theological Core:** This surah answers the three main ways humans have historically described God wrongly — that He has partners, that He was born, or that He has children. Each verse addresses one of these misconceptions:
- Verse 1: Allah is Ahad — One, not "one of many" but uniquely, absolutely One
- Verse 2: Allah is As-Samad — all creation depends on Him; He depends on nothing
- Verse 3: He does not beget (no son/daughter) and was not born (no parent/origin)
- Verse 4: Nothing is comparable to Him in any attribute

**Why it equals one-third of the Quran:** The Prophet (sallallahu alayhi wa aalihi wasallam) said this surah equals one-third of the Quran (Sahih Bukhari), because the Quran deals with three topics — Islamic law, stories, and the knowledge of Allah's attributes — and this surah is entirely devoted to the third.

**Tafseer (Al-Mizan):** Tabatabai explains that أَحَد (Ahad) is not the same as وَاحِد (Wahid). Wahid means "one" in the sense of "first in a sequence." Ahad is an absolute uniqueness that cannot be placed in any category or sequence — it describes a reality entirely its own.

---

## 📖 Surah Al-Falaq — سُورَةُ الفَلَق (113)

<div style="background:linear-gradient(135deg,#f0fdf4,#dcfce7);border:2px solid #34d399;border-radius:16px;padding:2rem;margin:1.5rem 0;text-align:center;">
<div style="font-size:2.2em;direction:rtl;line-height:3;font-family:'Amiri Quran','Scheherazade New','Traditional Arabic',serif;color:#064e3b;">

بِسۡمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحۡمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

قُلۡ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ ٱلۡفَلَقِ ﴿١﴾

مِن شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ ﴿٢﴾

وَمِن شَرِّ غَاسِقٍ إِذَا وَقَبَ ﴿٣﴾

وَمِن شَرِّ ٱلنَّفَّٰثَٰتِ فِي ٱلۡعُقَدِ ﴿٤﴾

وَمِن شَرِّ حَاسِدٍ إِذَا حَسَدَ ﴿٥﴾

</div>
</div>

### Verse-by-Verse

| Verse | Translation | Key Term |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| 1 | Say: I seek refuge in the Lord of the daybreak | الفَلَق = breaking of dawn / opening up |
| 2 | From the evil of what He has created | — |
| 3 | From the evil of darkness when it settles | غَاسِق = deep darkness; وَقَب = to settle/penetrate |
| 4 | From the evil of those who blow into knots | النَّفَّاثَات = blowers; عُقَد = knots — referring to harmful magic |
| 5 | From the evil of an envier when he envies | حَاسِد = envier; حَسَد = envy |

**Al-Falaq and An-Naas together are called المُعَوِّذَتَان** (the two refuge surahs). The Prophet (sallallahu alayhi wa aalihi wasallam) recited them every night before sleeping, and after every prayer.

**Three types of evil:**
1. **Natural evil** (darkness, night dangers)
2. **Supernatural evil** (knot-blowing — a reference to harmful practices)
3. **Human evil** (envy — which the Prophet (sallallahu alayhi wa aalihi wasallam) identified as a real spiritual harm)

## 📖 Surah An-Naas — سُورَةُ النَّاس (114)

<div style="background:linear-gradient(135deg,#f0fdf4,#dcfce7);border:2px solid #34d399;border-radius:16px;padding:2rem;margin:1.5rem 0;text-align:center;">
<div style="font-size:2.2em;direction:rtl;line-height:3;font-family:'Amiri Quran','Scheherazade New','Traditional Arabic',serif;color:#064e3b;">

بِسۡمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحۡمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

قُلۡ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ ٱلنَّاسِ ﴿١﴾

مَلِكِ ٱلنَّاسِ ﴿٢﴾

إِلَٰهِ ٱلنَّاسِ ﴿٣﴾

مِن شَرِّ ٱلۡوَسۡوَاسِ ٱلۡخَنَّاسِ ﴿٤﴾

ٱلَّذِي يُوَسۡوِسُ فِي صُدُورِ ٱلنَّاسِ ﴿٥﴾

مِنَ ٱلۡجِنَّةِ وَٱلنَّاسِ ﴿٦﴾

</div>
</div>

### Verse-by-Verse

| Verse | Translation | Key Term |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| 1 | Say: I seek refuge in the Lord of mankind | ربّ = Lord / Nurturer |
| 2 | The King of mankind | مَلِك = King |
| 3 | The God of mankind | إِلَٰه = Deity / God |
| 4 | From the evil of the retreating whisperer | وَسوَاس = whisperer; خَنَّاس = one who retreats/hides |
| 5 | Who whispers in the chests of mankind | صُدُور = chests |
| 6 | Whether of jinn or of people | — |

**Three attributes of Allah** are invoked as refuge: Rabb (nurturer), Malik (king), Ilaah (God). This triple invocation creates a complete shield.

**Al-Khannaas:** The whisperer who "retreats" — this is a name for Shaytan (Satan), who whispers evil thoughts, and when the person remembers Allah, he retreats. The action of dhikr (remembrance of Allah) is described as the mechanism that drives away the whisperer.

**Tafseer (Namoona/Shirazi):** The whispering comes from jinn AND from people — meaning that human sources of negative influence are included. Bad company, toxic relationships, and evil suggestions from others are included in the category of "whispering."

## 📖 Surah Al-Kawthar — سُورَةُ الكَوثَر (108)

<div style="background:linear-gradient(135deg,#f0fdf4,#dcfce7);border:2px solid #34d399;border-radius:16px;padding:2rem;margin:1.5rem 0;text-align:center;">
<div style="font-size:2.2em;direction:rtl;line-height:3;font-family:'Amiri Quran','Scheherazade New','Traditional Arabic',serif;color:#064e3b;">

بِسۡمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحۡمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

إِنَّآ أَعۡطَيۡنَٰكَ ٱلۡكَوۡثَرَ ﴿١﴾

فَصَلِّ لِرَبِّكَ وَٱنۡحَرۡ ﴿٢﴾

إِنَّ شَانِئَكَ هُوَ ٱلۡأَبۡتَرُ ﴿٣﴾

</div>
</div>

### Verse-by-Verse

| Verse | Translation | Key Term |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| 1 | Indeed, We have given you Al-Kawthar | الكَوثَر = abundance; scholars say it includes: the river in paradise, endless blessing, countless followers |
| 2 | So pray to your Lord and sacrifice | صَلِّ = pray; انحَر = sacrifice |
| 3 | Indeed, your enemy is the one cut off | شَانِئَك = your enemy/hater; الأَبتَر = the one who is cut off (without continuation) |

**Context:** The enemies of the Prophet (sallallahu alayhi wa aalihi wasallam) mocked him when his sons died in infancy, calling him "al-abtar" (cut off, without heirs). Allah's response: "We have given YOU abundance — and it is your enemy who will be cut off." The prophecy proved true — 1.8 billion Muslims continue his legacy; his mockers are forgotten.

**Al-Kawthar as the shortest surah:** Three verses — yet every verse contains complete meaning.

---

## 📖 Surah Al-Asr — سُورَةُ العَصر (103)

<div style="background:linear-gradient(135deg,#f0fdf4,#dcfce7);border:2px solid #34d399;border-radius:16px;padding:2rem;margin:1.5rem 0;text-align:center;">
<div style="font-size:2.2em;direction:rtl;line-height:3;font-family:'Amiri Quran','Scheherazade New','Traditional Arabic',serif;color:#064e3b;">

بِسۡمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحۡمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

وَٱلۡعَصۡرِ ﴿١﴾

إِنَّ ٱلۡإِنسَٰنَ لَفِي خُسۡرٍ ﴿٢﴾

إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَعَمِلُواْ ٱلصَّٰلِحَٰتِ وَتَوَاصَوۡاْ بِٱلۡحَقِّ وَتَوَاصَوۡاْ بِٱلصَّبۡرِ ﴿٣﴾

</div>
</div>

### Verse-by-Verse

| Verse | Translation | Key Term |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| 1 | By Time (Al-Asr) | العَصر = afternoon / passing of time |
| 2 | Indeed, mankind is in loss | خُسر = loss |
| 3 | Except those who have faith, do righteous deeds, counsel each other with truth, and counsel each other with patience | — |

**Four conditions for salvation:**
1. **Iman** (faith) — inner belief
2. **Amal Salih** (righteous deeds) — right action
3. **Tawasi bil-Haqq** — mutual counsel in truth
4. **Tawasi bis-Sabr** — mutual counsel in patience

**Why time is the oath:** Imam Al-Shafi'i said: "If people only reflected on this surah, it would be sufficient guidance for them." Time is the most precious commodity humans have — and most people lose it. The surah says: one person can win against the default loss of time, but only by fulfilling all four conditions together.

**Tafseer (Tasnim/Jawadi Amoli):** The word "except" (إِلَّا) indicates that salvation is not the default — loss is the default. Humans are born losing (time is always passing). Only active, sustained, and community-based faith changes the outcome.

## 📋 Summary

| Surah | Core Message |
|-------|-------------|
| Al-Ikhlas (112) | Allah is absolutely One — no partners, no offspring, no equal |
| Al-Falaq (113) | Seek refuge from external evils — darkness, magic, envy |
| An-Naas (114) | Seek refuge from internal whispers — from jinn and humans |
| Al-Kawthar (108) | Allah gives abundance — true legacy outlasts mockery |
| Al-Asr (103) | Human success requires faith + action + truth + patience |

# ماڈیول ۶ — جُزء عَمَّ: چھوٹی سورتیں — حصہ اول

## 🌟 پانچ قیمتی سورتیں

| سورت | نمبر | آیات | موضوع |
|------|------|------|-------|
| الاخلاص | ۱۱۲ | ۴ | توحید خالص |
| الفلق | ۱۱۳ | ۵ | بیرونی شروں سے پناہ |
| الناس | ۱۱۴ | ۶ | اندرونی وسوسوں سے پناہ |
| الکوثر | ۱۰۸ | ۳ | بے پناہ نعمت |
| العصر | ۱۰۳ | ۳ | کامیابی کا فارمولا |

---

## سورۃ الاخلاص

> قُلۡ هُوَ ٱللَّهُ أَحَدٌ ۔ ٱللَّهُ ٱلصَّمَدُ ۔ لَمۡ يَلِدۡ وَلَمۡ يُولَدۡ ۔ وَلَمۡ يَكُن لَّهُۥ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌ

ترجمہ: کہو وہ اللہ ایک ہے۔ اللہ بے نیاز ہے۔ نہ اس کی اولاد ہے نہ وہ کسی سے پیدا ہوا۔ اور کوئی اس کے برابر نہیں۔

یہ سورت قرآن کا ایک تہائی ہے (صحیح بخاری)۔

---

## سورۃ الفلق

> قُلۡ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ ٱلۡفَلَقِ ۔ مِن شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ ۔ وَمِن شَرِّ غَاسِقٍ إِذَا وَقَبَ ۔ وَمِن شَرِّ ٱلنَّفَّٰثَٰتِ فِي ٱلۡعُقَدِ ۔ وَمِن شَرِّ حَاسِدٍ إِذَا حَسَدَ

ترجمہ: کہو میں صبح کے رب کی پناہ مانگتا ہوں — ہر مخلوق کے شر سے، اندھیری رات کے شر سے، گرہوں پر پھونکنے والوں کے شر سے، اور حسد کرنے والے کے شر سے۔

---

## سورۃ الناس

> قُلۡ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ ٱلنَّاسِ ۔ مَلِكِ ٱلنَّاسِ ۔ إِلَٰهِ ٱلنَّاسِ ۔ مِن شَرِّ ٱلۡوَسۡوَاسِ ٱلۡخَنَّاسِ ۔ ٱلَّذِي يُوَسۡوِسُ فِي صُدُورِ ٱلنَّاسِ ۔ مِنَ ٱلۡجِنَّةِ وَٱلنَّاسِ

ترجمہ: کہو میں لوگوں کے رب کی پناہ مانگتا ہوں — لوگوں کے بادشاہ کی، لوگوں کے معبود کی — اس وسوسہ ڈالنے والے پیچھے ہٹنے والے کے شر سے، جو لوگوں کے سینوں میں وسوسہ ڈالتا ہے — جنوں میں سے ہو یا انسانوں میں سے۔

---

## سورۃ الکوثر

> إِنَّآ أَعۡطَيۡنَٰكَ ٱلۡكَوۡثَرَ ۔ فَصَلِّ لِرَبِّكَ وَٱنۡحَرۡ ۔ إِنَّ شَانِئَكَ هُوَ ٱلۡأَبۡتَرُ

ترجمہ: بے شک ہم نے تجھے کوثر (بے پناہ خیر) دی — پس اپنے رب کے لیے نماز پڑھ اور قربانی کر — بے شک تیرا دشمن ہی بے نام ونشان ہے۔

---

## سورۃ العصر

> وَٱلۡعَصۡرِ ۔ إِنَّ ٱلۡإِنسَٰنَ لَفِي خُسۡرٍ ۔ إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَعَمِلُواْ ٱلصَّٰلِحَٰتِ وَتَوَاصَوۡاْ بِٱلۡحَقِّ وَتَوَاصَوۡاْ بِٱلصَّبۡرِ

ترجمہ: زمانے کی قسم — انسان خسارے میں ہے — سوائے ان لوگوں کے جو ایمان لائے، نیک اعمال کیے، آپس میں حق کی تاکید کی اور صبر کی تاکید کی۔

**کامیابی کی چار شرطیں:** ایمان + عمل صالح + حق کی نصیحت + صبر کی نصیحت

</div>
