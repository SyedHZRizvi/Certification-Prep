## Al-Ikhlas (112) — Four Facts About Allah

1. He is Ahad — absolute uniqueness (not just "one of many")
2. He is As-Samad — all depend on Him; He depends on nothing
3. He does not beget and was not born
4. Nothing equals or compares to Him

**= One-third of the Quran (Sahih Bukhari)**

---

## Al-Falaq (113) — Three Evils to Avoid

1. Evil of all creation generally
2. Evil of darkness when it settles
3. Evil of envy when the envier envies

---

## Al-Asr (103) — Four Conditions for Success

1. **Iman** — faith
2. **Amal Salih** — righteous deeds
3. **Tawasi bil-Haqq** — counsel each other in truth
4. **Tawasi bis-Sabr** — counsel each other in patience

All four required together — missing any one = default loss (خُسر)
