# Module 8 Cheat Sheet — Memorization & Hifz

## Three Pillars of Hifz

| Pillar | Arabic | Rule |
|--------|--------|------|
| Consolidation | الثَّبَات (Sabt) | Don't move on until fully consolidated |
| Accuracy | التَّجوِيد | Correct Tajweed from the start |
| Revision | المُرَاجَعَة | 2-3 pages revision per 1 page new |

---

## Daily Hifz Schedule

| Time | Activity |
|------|---------|
| Fajr (30 min) | New material — brain at peak |
| After Fajr (10 min) | Review yesterday's new |
| Midday (15 min) | Review past week |
| After Asr (15 min) | Review past month |
| After Isha (10 min) | Listen to today's section |

---

## Spaced Repetition

Day 1 → Day 2 → Day 4 → Day 7 → Day 14 → Day 30

Each review refreshes the memory's "expiry date."

---

## Children's Memorization Tips

- Short sessions (10-15 min max)
- Melody + rhythm
- Immediate reward
- Explain meaning in stories
- Family recitation together
- Start with: Bismillah → Fatiha → Ikhlas

---

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Solution |
|---------|---------|
| Too fast, weak base | Slow down, Sabt first |
| No revision | 2-3× revision per 1× new |
| Wrong Tajweed | Fix immediately, not later |
| Changing mushaf | Always same edition |
| Days of zero | Even 1 verse counts |
