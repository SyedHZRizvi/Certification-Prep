# Module 3 Cheat Sheet — Tajweed Rules: Foundations

## Madd (Lengthening) Types

| Type | Arabic | Length | Trigger |
|------|--------|--------|---------|
| Natural | طَبِيعِي | 2 beats | Long vowel, nothing special follows |
| Connected | مُتَّصِل | 4–5 beats | Madd letter + Hamza in SAME word |
| Disconnected | مُنفَصِل | 4–5 beats | Madd letter end of word + Hamza starts next word |
| Ease | لِّين | 2 beats | Waw/Ya with Sukoon after Fatha |

---

## Noon Sakin & Tanween — 4 Rules

| Rule | Arabic | Letters | Action | Ghunnah |
|------|--------|---------|--------|---------|
| Clear | إِظهَار | أ ه ع ح غ خ | Pronounce Noon clearly | No |
| Merge+nasal | إِدغَام مع غُنَّة | ي ن م و | Noon merges, nasal hum | Yes (2 beats) |
| Merge clean | إِدغَام بلا غُنَّة | ل ر | Noon merges cleanly | No |
| Convert | إِقلَاب | ب only | Noon → Meem sound | Yes (2 beats) |
| Conceal | إِخفَاء | 15 letters | Partial nasal, Noon hidden | Yes (2 beats) |

**The 15 Ikhfa letters:** ت ث ج د ذ ز س ش ص ض ط ظ ف ق ك

---

## Ghunnah (Nasal Sound)

Always 2 beats. Required when:
1. Noon (نّ) or Meem (مّ) has Shaddah
2. Idgham with Ghunnah (ي ن م و after نْ/Tanween)
3. Iqlab (ب after نْ/Tanween)
4. Ikhfa (15 letters after نْ/Tanween)

---

## Coverage Check

6 (Izhar) + 6 (Idgham) + 1 (Iqlab) + 15 (Ikhfa) = **28 letters** — the complete alphabet!
