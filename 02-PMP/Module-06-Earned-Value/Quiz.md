# ✏️ PMP Module 6 Quiz: EVM

## Questions

### Q1. CV (Cost Variance) =
A. PV - EV
B. EV - AC
C. AC - EV
D. BAC - AC

### Q2. SPI (Schedule Performance Index) =
A. EV / PV
B. PV / EV
C. EV / AC
D. PV / AC

### Q3. CPI = 0.85 means:
A. 15% over budget
B. 85% under budget
C. Earning $0.85 per $1.00 spent
D. Project is ahead of schedule

### Q4. SPI = 1.10 means:
A. 10% over schedule
B. Ahead of schedule
C. Behind schedule
D. On budget

### Q5. EAC default formula (most common):
A. BAC × CPI
B. BAC / CPI
C. AC + ETC
D. BAC - AC

### Q6. ETC =
A. EAC + AC
B. EAC - AC
C. BAC - EAC
D. EAC / CPI

### Q7. VAC =
A. BAC - EAC
B. EAC - BAC
C. AC - BAC
D. BAC + EAC

### Q8. BAC = $200K, AC = $80K, EV = $60K. CPI = ?
A. 1.33
B. 0.75
C. 1.0
D. 0.50

### Q9. EV = $50K, PV = $40K, AC = $45K. SV = ?
A. -$10K
B. +$10K
C. +$5K
D. $0

### Q10. CPI = 0.8, BAC = $500K. EAC (default) = ?
A. $400K
B. $625K
C. $500K
D. $750K

### Q11. Which indicates the project is BOTH over budget AND behind schedule?
A. CPI > 1, SPI > 1
B. CPI < 1, SPI < 1
C. CPI > 1, SPI < 1
D. CV > 0, SV > 0

### Q12. TCPI > 1 means:
A. Project is ahead
B. Future work must be more efficient than past work
C. Budget surplus
D. Schedule surplus

---

## 🎯 Answers

### Q1: B (EV - AC)
### Q2: A (EV / PV)
### Q3: C (CPI = $0.85 earned per $1 spent — over budget)
### Q4: B (>1 = ahead)
### Q5: B (BAC / CPI)
### Q6: B (EAC - AC = remaining work cost)
### Q7: A (BAC - EAC)
### Q8: B (CPI = EV/AC = 60/80 = 0.75)
### Q9: B (SV = EV - PV = 50 - 40 = +10K)
### Q10: B (EAC = 500/0.8 = $625K)
### Q11: B (both <1 = bad)
### Q12: B (>1 = harder than past pace)
