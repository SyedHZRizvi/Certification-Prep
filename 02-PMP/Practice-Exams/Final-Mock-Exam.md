# 🏆 PMP Final Mock Exam (180 Questions, ~3 hr 50 min)

> Take 1-2 days before the real exam. **180 questions in 230 minutes** — a full-length simulation matching the live PMP exam exactly (180 Q / 230 min).
> Sit it in one block. Take the two 10-minute breaks where the real exam puts them (after Q60 and after Q120) so your pacing is rehearsed.
> **Target: ~61% (roughly 110/180).** PMI does not publish an official passing percentage or cut score — the exam is scored against a panel-set "Above Target / Target / Below Target / Needs Improvement" band per domain, not a fixed %. ~61% is a widely used, defensible study benchmark, not a guarantee. Aim well above it before booking.
