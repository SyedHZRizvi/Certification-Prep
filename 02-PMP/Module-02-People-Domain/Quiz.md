# ✏️ PMP Module 2 Quiz: People Domain

> 18 questions. Aim 15/18. Most exam-relevant module — drill it.

## Questions

### Q1. Two team members disagree on a technical approach. The PM should:
A. Ask their manager to decide
B. Bring them together to find a win-win solution (Confronting/Problem-solving)
C. Pick one and move on
D. Avoid the conflict

### Q2. A team has been together 6 weeks; conflicts about roles and priorities are surfacing. What stage?
A. Forming
B. Storming
C. Norming
D. Performing

### Q3. A PM has 12 stakeholders. How many communication channels?
A. 12
B. 24
C. 66
D. 132

### Q4. The PM realizes a team member is unhappy and disengaged. What FIRST?
A. Replace them
B. Have a 1-on-1 to understand the situation
C. Reassign them
D. Lower their workload arbitrarily

### Q5. Per Herzberg, a salary increase typically:
A. Strongly motivates
B. Prevents dissatisfaction but doesn't motivate long-term
C. Has no effect
D. Demotivates

### Q6. Which conflict resolution technique is generally LEAST recommended?
A. Confronting
B. Collaborating
C. Withdrawing/Avoiding
D. Compromising

### Q7. A team is performing well, members trust each other, output is high. What stage?
A. Forming
B. Storming
C. Norming
D. Performing

### Q8. The PM expresses frustration with a stakeholder by raising their voice. This shows lack of:
A. Self-regulation (EQ)
B. Empathy
C. Knowledge
D. Authority

### Q9. Maslow's hierarchy (lowest to highest):
A. Physiological, Safety, Belonging, Esteem, Self-actualization
B. Money, Status, Power, Recognition, Achievement
C. Health, Family, Career, Hobbies, Legacy
D. Plan, Execute, Inspect, Adapt, Improve

### Q10. A junior team member asks how to approach a problem. The PM should:
A. Give them the solution
B. Coach them with questions
C. Tell them to ask someone senior
D. Defer the problem

### Q11. The Team Charter is:
A. Imposed by the PM
B. Created collaboratively by the team
C. A legal contract
D. Approved by the sponsor

### Q12. A stakeholder is unaware of the project. Their engagement level is:
A. Unaware
B. Resistant
C. Neutral
D. Supportive

### Q13. The PM should default to which leadership style per PMP-2021?
A. Authoritative
B. Servant leadership
C. Laissez-faire
D. Transactional only

### Q14. Communication that uses wikis or shared drives is:
A. Push
B. Pull
C. Interactive
D. Vertical

### Q15. A virtual team is struggling with trust. The PM should:
A. Use email more
B. Schedule video calls and informal team-building
C. Replace remote members with onsite
D. Skip retrospectives

### Q16. The Theory Y manager assumes team members:
A. Need close supervision
B. Are self-motivated and capable
C. Must be threatened
D. Are inherently lazy

### Q17. A PM observes high-priority decisions are being made by junior members without consulting senior staff. They should:
A. Forbid junior members from deciding
B. Coach the team on decision authority and escalation paths
C. Take over all decisions
D. Replace the team

### Q18. What % of a PM's time is communication?
A. ~30%
B. ~50%
C. ~70%
D. ~90%

---

## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **B. Confronting/Problem-solving**
Best default. Find a win-win.

### Q2: **B. Storming**
Conflicts about roles = classic storming.

### Q3: **C. 66**
n(n-1)/2 = 12(11)/2 = 66.

### Q4: **B. 1-on-1 to understand**
Empathy first.

### Q5: **B. Prevents dissatisfaction**
Hygiene factor.

### Q6: **C. Withdrawing/Avoiding**
Doesn't resolve anything.

### Q7: **D. Performing**
High output + trust = performing.

### Q8: **A. Self-regulation**
Goleman's EQ component.

### Q9: **A. Physiological → Safety → Belonging → Esteem → Self-actualization**

### Q10: **B. Coach with questions**
Build their capability.

### Q11: **B. Created collaboratively**
Team owns its norms.

### Q12: **A. Unaware**
Direct from stakeholder engagement levels.

### Q13: **B. Servant leadership**
The PMP-2021 mindset.

### Q14: **B. Pull**
Receiver pulls info from a shared source.

### Q15: **B. Video calls + team-building**
Virtual teams need MORE relationship-building.

### Q16: **B. Self-motivated and capable**
Theory Y.

### Q17: **B. Coach on decision authority**
Don't disempower; clarify.

### Q18: **D. ~90%**
