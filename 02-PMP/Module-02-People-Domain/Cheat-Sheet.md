# 📋 PMP Module 2 Cheat Sheet: People Domain (42% of exam!)

---

## 🎭 Tuckman's 5 Stages

```
Forming → Storming → Norming → Performing → Adjourning
   ↓          ↓          ↓           ↓            ↓
 Polite    Conflict   Settled   High output   Disbands
   ↓          ↓          ↓           ↓
 Lead     COACH    FACILITATE   HANDS-OFF
```

---

## 🤬 Conflict Resolution (BEST → WORST)

1. ✅ **Confronting / Problem-solving** ⭐ DEFAULT
2. ✅ **Collaborating**
3. ⚠️ **Compromising**
4. ⚠️ **Smoothing / Accommodating**
5. ⚠️ **Forcing** (only emergencies)
6. ❌ **Withdrawing** (worst)

## 💪 Motivation Theories

| Theory | Key Insight |
|--------|-------------|
| **Maslow** | Lower needs first (Physiological→Safety→Belonging→Esteem→Self-actualization) |
| **Herzberg** | Hygiene avoids dissatisfaction; Motivators motivate |
| **McGregor X/Y** | X = control needed; Y = self-motivated |
| **McClelland** | Achievement, Affiliation, Power |

🎯 PMP-2021 = **Theory Y**

## 🛡️ Servant Leader Toolkit (DEFAULT BEHAVIORS)

✅ **Always do:**
- Listen first
- 1-on-1s
- Remove impediments
- Facilitate dialogue
- Coach, don't tell
- Develop people
- Recognize contributions
- Empower decisions

❌ **Almost never:**
- Replace immediately
- Escalate first
- Give the answer
- Strict authority
- Send stern emails

---

## 🎯 Power Phrases for People Questions

✅ Right answers often contain:
- "Have a 1-on-1..."
- "Coach the team..."
- "Facilitate a discussion..."
- "Understand their concerns..."
- "Empower them to..."

❌ Wrong answers:
- "Replace the underperformer"
- "Escalate to HR"
- "Tell them to follow the plan"
- "Send an email to clarify"
