# ✏️ PMP Module 10 Quiz: Stakeholders

## Questions

### Q1. The 5 stakeholder engagement levels are:
A. Unknown, Aware, Engaged, Active, Leading
B. Unaware, Resistant, Neutral, Supportive, Leading
C. Hostile, Skeptical, Open, Friendly, Champion
D. Low, Medium, High, Very High, Critical

### Q2. A stakeholder has high power AND high interest. Best engagement strategy:
A. Monitor minimally
B. Keep informed
C. Manage closely
D. Keep satisfied

### Q3. The Salience Model uses which 3 dimensions?
A. Power, Interest, Influence
B. Power, Legitimacy, Urgency
C. Power, Interest, Attitude
D. Power, Interest, Frequency

### Q4. PM identifies a resistant stakeholder. They should:
A. Ignore them
B. Force them to comply
C. Engage to understand concerns and address them
D. Replace them

### Q5. The Stakeholder Register is:
A. A one-time identification
B. Continuously updated
C. Confidential to PM
D. Optional

### Q6. The Sponsor:
A. Manages the team daily
B. Approves charter, funds, removes top-level impediments
C. Reports to the PM
D. Codes the deliverables

### Q7. A stakeholder is unaware of the project. Engagement level:
A. Unaware
B. Resistant
C. Neutral
D. Cannot determine

### Q8. PM should escalate to Sponsor when:
A. Daily issues arise
B. Major impediment is outside team's control after team has tried
C. PM is busy
D. Team disagrees with PM

### Q9. In agile, stakeholders are engaged via:
A. Annual reviews only
B. Sprint Reviews and continuous feedback
C. Final delivery only
D. Email reports

### Q10. The Power-Interest Grid for "low power, high interest":
A. Manage closely
B. Keep informed
C. Keep satisfied
D. Monitor

---

## Answers
1.B  2.C  3.B  4.C  5.B  6.B  7.A  8.B  9.B  10.B
