# 📋 PMP Module 10 Cheat Sheet: Stakeholders

---

## 🔄 4 Processes
1. Identify Stakeholders
2. Plan Engagement
3. Manage Engagement
4. Monitor Engagement

---

## 📊 Power-Interest Grid

```
              INTEREST →
            LOW            HIGH
       ┌────────────────────────────┐
  HIGH │ Keep         │ Manage      │
       │ Satisfied    │ Closely  ⭐ │
POWER  ├──────────────┼─────────────┤
       │  Monitor     │ Keep        │
   LOW │  (min)       │ Informed    │
       └────────────────────────────┘
```

---

## 🎯 5 Engagement Levels

1. Unaware
2. Resistant
3. Neutral
4. Supportive
5. Leading

🎯 Move toward **Leading** (or at minimum, **Supportive**)

---

## 🎲 Salience Model (3D)
- **Power** — can enforce
- **Legitimacy** — appropriate involvement
- **Urgency** — time pressure

---

## 🎯 Strategies

| Stakeholder | Strategy |
|-------------|----------|
| Resistant | Listen, address concerns, engage |
| Supportive | Keep informed, leverage |
| Neutral | Updates; identify movers |
| Leading | Empower, recognize |
| Unaware | Inform |

---

## 👔 Sponsor's Role
- Funds the project
- Approves Charter
- Champions/escalation point
- Defines success criteria
