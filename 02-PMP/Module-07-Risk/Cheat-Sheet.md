# 📋 PMP Module 7 Cheat Sheet: Risk

---

## ⚠️ Risk Definition
**Uncertain event with POSITIVE or NEGATIVE effect.**

---

## 🛡️ THREAT Strategies (Negative)

| Strategy | What |
|----------|------|
| **Avoid** | Eliminate threat |
| **Transfer** | Shift to 3rd party (insurance) |
| **Mitigate** | Reduce probability/impact |
| **Accept** | Take risk; have plan B |
| **Escalate** | Outside PM authority |

🧠 **A-T-M-A-E**

## 🎁 OPPORTUNITY Strategies (Positive)

| Strategy | What |
|----------|------|
| **Exploit** | Make sure it happens |
| **Enhance** | Increase prob/impact |
| **Share** | Partner with someone |
| **Accept** | Take if it occurs |
| **Escalate** | Outside PM authority |

🧠 **E-E-S-A-E**

---

## 💰 EMV
**EMV = Probability × Impact**

For threats: prefer LESS negative
For opportunities: prefer MORE positive

---

## 🔄 6 Risk Processes

1. Plan Risk Mgmt
2. Identify Risks
3. Qualitative Analysis (P-I matrix)
4. Quantitative Analysis (Monte Carlo, EMV)
5. Plan Responses
6. Implement + Monitor

---

## ⚖️ Risk vs Issue

| Risk | Issue |
|------|-------|
| Future, uncertain | Now, happening |
| Probability <100% | Probability = 100% |
| Risk Register | Issue Log |

---

## 🎯 Tolerances/Thresholds
- **Appetite**, overall willingness
- **Tolerance**, acceptable variation
- **Threshold**, specific limit
