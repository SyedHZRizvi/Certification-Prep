# ✏️ PMP Module 7 Quiz: Risk

## Questions

### Q1. PM buys insurance to handle a major risk. This strategy is:
A. Avoid
B. Transfer
C. Mitigate
D. Accept

### Q2. PM removes risky scope from the project to eliminate a threat. This is:
A. Avoid
B. Transfer
C. Mitigate
D. Accept

### Q3. A team member just resigned. This is:
A. A risk
B. An issue
C. Both
D. Neither

### Q4. Risks can be:
A. Only threats (negative)
B. Only opportunities (positive)
C. Both threats and opportunities
D. Random events

### Q5. EMV = ?
A. Probability + Impact
B. Probability × Impact
C. Probability / Impact
D. Probability - Impact

### Q6. Risk: 30% × $100K loss. EMV = ?
A. -$30K
B. +$30K
C. -$130K
D. +$70K

### Q7. PM identifies an opportunity to finish early. To make sure it happens, PM adds resources. This is:
A. Exploit
B. Enhance
C. Share
D. Accept

### Q8. Risk Appetite refers to:
A. A specific risk threshold
B. Overall willingness to take risk
C. Number of risks identified
D. Risk owner

### Q9. After identifying risks, the next step is:
A. Plan responses
B. Implement responses
C. Qualitative analysis
D. Close risks

### Q10. The Risk Register is:
A. A one-time document
B. Continuously updated
C. Confidential to PM only
D. Optional

### Q11. PM joint-ventures with a partner to share an opportunity. This is:
A. Exploit
B. Share
C. Enhance
D. Transfer

### Q12. A risk goes outside the PM's authority to address. The PM:
A. Ignores it
B. Documents and accepts
C. Escalates
D. Closes the project

---

## 🎯 Answers
1.B  2.A  3.B  4.C  5.B  6.A  7.A  8.B  9.C  10.B  11.B  12.C
