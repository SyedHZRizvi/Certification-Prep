## 🎯 Answers

### Q1: **B. Sponsor**
### Q2: **B. Longest path**
### Q3: **B. Zero float**
### Q4: **B. (O + 4M + P) / 6**
### Q5: **B. Fast-track** (no extra cost; runs in parallel)
### Q6: **A. Costs more** (adding resources)
### Q7: **B. Scope Statement + WBS + WBS Dictionary**
### Q8: **B. Known risks**
### Q9: **C. Sponsor**
### Q10: **B. Lowest level, typically <80 hours**
### Q11: **C. Analyze impact, change control**
### Q12: **B. Throughout the project**
### Q13: **A. Scope+Schedule+Cost baselines (without mgmt reserve)**
### Q14: **C. -25% to +75%**
### Q15: **B. Initiating, Planning, Executing, M&C, Closing**
### Q16: **A. Updates to plan and baselines**
### Q17: **B. Earliest possible project completion** (it's the shortest possible duration)
### Q18: **B. Analogous**

### Q19: **B. 13 days**
PERT = (O + 4M + P) / 6 = (6 + 4(12) + 24) / 6 = (6 + 48 + 24) / 6 = 78 / 6 = 13 days.

### Q20: **B. 2 days delayed**
Float = 4 days; delay = 6 days; net impact = 6 - 4 = 2 days delay because the task now extends past its float window onto the critical path.

### Q21: **B. Crashing**
Crashing adds resources/cost to shorten duration. Fast-tracking (parallel work) is for when there's no extra budget. Scope cut and skipping QA are PMP traps.

### Q22: **C. Request from Management Reserve**
Unknown unknowns (risks not in the register) are funded by the Management Reserve, controlled by the sponsor. Contingency is for known risks.

### Q23: **C. Analyze impact first**
The PMP-correct sequence is analyze impact → submit to CCB → approve/reject → update plans. Never reject or approve without analysis.

### Q24: **B. Authorizes and grants PM authority**
The Charter is the formal authorization document signed by the sponsor that grants the PM authority to apply organizational resources.

### Q25: **B. WBS**
The 100% rule says the WBS captures 100% of project scope — every work package totals up to the full project.

### Q26: **B. Throughout the project, stored in OPAs**
Lessons learned are captured continuously and become OPAs for future projects (both successes and failures).

### Q27: **C. Hybrid sharing risk**
T&M shares risk: hourly labor + materials means the buyer pays for time spent, but pricing is pre-agreed. Fixed Price = seller risk; Cost-Reimbursable = buyer risk.

### Q28: **D. Management Reserve**
PMB = Scope + Schedule + Cost baselines, INCLUDING contingency reserves but EXCLUDING the management reserve (which is sponsor-controlled).

### Q29: **C. -5% to +10%**
Definitive estimates have the tightest range (-5% to +10%). ROM is -25% to +75%; budgetary is -10% to +25%.

### Q30: **C. Bottom-up**
Bottom-up estimating sums detailed estimates of each work package — most accurate but most time-consuming.
