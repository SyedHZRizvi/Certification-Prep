# ✏️ PMP Module 3 Quiz: Process Domain

## Questions

### Q1. The Project Charter is signed by:
A. The Project Manager
B. The Sponsor
C. The Stakeholders
D. The CCB

### Q2. The Critical Path is:
A. The shortest path through the network
B. The longest path through the network
C. The most expensive path
D. The path with most resources

### Q3. A task on the critical path has:
A. Maximum float
B. Zero float
C. Variable float
D. Float depending on resources

### Q4. PERT formula:
A. (O + M + P) / 3
B. (O + 4M + P) / 6
C. (O + P) / 2
D. (M × 4)

### Q5. To reduce duration without spending more money:
A. Crash the schedule
B. Fast-track the schedule
C. Hire more people
D. Buy more equipment

### Q6. Crashing typically:
A. Costs more
B. Costs less
C. Has no cost impact
D. Impossible to determine

### Q7. The Scope Baseline includes:
A. Just the Scope Statement
B. Scope Statement + WBS + WBS Dictionary
C. WBS + Schedule
D. Charter + Scope Statement

### Q8. Contingency Reserve is for:
A. Unknown unknowns
B. Known risks (known unknowns)
C. Profit margin
D. Sponsor's discretion

### Q9. Management Reserve is controlled by:
A. The PM
B. The CCB
C. The Sponsor
D. The team

### Q10. A WBS work package is:
A. A single team task
B. The lowest level of decomposition (typically <80 hours)
C. A summary report
D. A Gantt chart

### Q11. A stakeholder demands a scope addition. The PM should FIRST:
A. Reject — there's a baseline
B. Add it; the customer is always right
C. Analyze impact and submit through change control
D. Escalate to sponsor

### Q12. Lessons learned should be:
A. Captured only at project end
B. Captured throughout the project
C. Optional
D. Confidential

### Q13. The Performance Measurement Baseline (PMB) includes:
A. Scope, schedule, cost baselines (without management reserve)
B. Just the cost baseline
C. WBS + Charter
D. Risk register + plans

### Q14. ROM (Rough Order of Magnitude) estimate range:
A. ±10%
B. -5% to +10%
C. -25% to +75%
D. ±50%

### Q15. The 5 Process Groups:
A. Plan, Execute, Test, Deploy, Close
B. Initiating, Planning, Executing, Monitoring & Controlling, Closing
C. Vision, Plan, Build, Test, Release
D. Charter, Plan, Execute, Review, Close

### Q16. Approved change requests result in:
A. Updates to the project plan and baselines
B. Immediate implementation
C. Lessons learned only
D. Sponsor sign-off only

### Q17. The Critical Path's longest path indicates:
A. The cheapest schedule
B. The earliest possible project completion
C. Highest risk path
D. Most resource-intensive path

### Q18. Estimating from past similar projects is:
A. Bottom-up
B. Analogous
C. Parametric
D. Three-point

---

## 🎯 Answers

### Q1: **B. Sponsor**
### Q2: **B. Longest path**
### Q3: **B. Zero float**
### Q4: **B. (O + 4M + P) / 6**
### Q5: **B. Fast-track** (no extra cost; runs in parallel)
### Q6: **A. Costs more** (adding resources)
### Q7: **B. Scope Statement + WBS + WBS Dictionary**
### Q8: **B. Known risks**
### Q9: **C. Sponsor**
### Q10: **B. Lowest level, typically <80 hours**
### Q11: **C. Analyze impact, change control**
### Q12: **B. Throughout the project**
### Q13: **A. Scope+Schedule+Cost baselines (without mgmt reserve)**
### Q14: **C. -25% to +75%**
### Q15: **B. Initiating, Planning, Executing, M&C, Closing**
### Q16: **A. Updates to plan and baselines**
### Q17: **B. Earliest possible project completion** (it's the shortest possible duration)
### Q18: **B. Analogous**
