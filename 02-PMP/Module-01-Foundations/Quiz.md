## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **B. Temporary endeavor producing unique result**
Direct from PMI definition.

### Q2: **A. Responsibility, Respect, Fairness, Honesty**

### Q3: **C. Government regulations**
EEFs = external/cultural. Templates and lessons learned are OPAs.

### Q4: **B. Refuse and disclose**
Ethics: Responsibility + Honesty + Fairness require this.

### Q5: **B. Limited or no authority**
Functional orgs: functional managers have authority; PMs are coordinators.

### Q6: **B. Ways of Working, Power Skills, Business Acumen**
Refreshed Talent Triangle (recent).

### Q7: **B. Predictive**
Stable + regulated = predictive fits.

### Q8: **B. 8 Performance Domains**
PMBOK 7 / new PMP structure.

### Q9: **C. Documentation**
The 8: Stakeholders, Team, Approach, Planning, Work, Delivery, Measurement, Uncertainty.

### Q10: **B. Communicating**
~90% of PM time.

### Q11: **C. Directly manages projects**
Directive = high control. Supportive = low. Controlling = compliance.

### Q12: **C. Lessons learned database**
OPA = internal artifact.

### Q13: **B. Report truth + address pressure**
Honesty value applies.

### Q14: **B. Principles and Performance Domains**
Major shift from PMBOK 6's process-heavy approach.

### Q15: **B. Talking to stakeholders/team and coaching**
The PMP-2021 mindset.

### Q16: **B. Controlling**
Controlling PMOs require compliance with frameworks/templates. Supportive only suggests; Directive owns the projects outright.

### Q17: **B. Building a new corporate headquarters**
A project is temporary and produces a unique result. The other options are ongoing/repetitive operations.

### Q18: **B. Agile**
Evolving requirements + need for early customer feedback = agile fits best (iterative, adaptive).

### Q19: **B. Disclose immediately and correct**
The Honesty value of the PMI Code of Ethics requires truth in communications and prompt correction of mistakes.

### Q20: **C. Project closeout templates**
OPAs are internal artifacts (templates, lessons learned, processes). Laws, market trends, and cultural factors are EEFs.

### Q21: **C. Business Acumen**
Business Acumen covers strategy, finance, and industry awareness. Power Skills = leadership; Ways of Working = PM techniques.

### Q22: **D. Uncertainty**
The Uncertainty Performance Domain covers risk, ambiguity, complexity, and change.

### Q23: **B. Gold plating**
Adding unauthorized extras is gold plating. Both gold plating and scope creep are discouraged; use change control instead.

### Q24: **B. Refuse and disclose the conflict**
Fairness requires impartial decisions and disclosure of conflicts of interest. Personal relationships should not influence procurement.

### Q25: **B. Be diligent, respectful, caring**
Stewardship is the first PMBOK 7 principle: act with integrity, care, and accountability for project resources and outcomes.

### Q26: **B. Full authority over budget and team**
In a Projectized org, the PM has full project authority. Functional orgs put authority with functional managers.

### Q27: **B. Requirement stability, customer involvement, regulatory needs**
The PMP exam expects approach selection based on context (tailoring), not preference or trend.
