## 🎯 PMP MINDSET (CRITICAL)

✅ Default actions:

- Talk to team / stakeholder first
- Coach, don't manage
- Empower team
- Communicate proactively
- Find root cause
- Iterate / adapt

❌ Almost never:

- Escalate first
- Reject changes outright
- Tell people what to do
- Submit change request immediately

---

## 🚫 4 Trap Patterns

2. ❌ "Change request" reflex
