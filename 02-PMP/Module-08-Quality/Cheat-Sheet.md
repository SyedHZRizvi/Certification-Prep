# 📋 PMP Module 8 Cheat Sheet: Quality

---

## ✅ Quality vs Grade
- **Quality** = meets requirements (always desirable)
- **Grade** = features/level (low can be fine)

---

## 🏛️ Gurus
| Person | Famous For |
|--------|------------|
| Deming | PDCA, 14 Points |
| Juran | Quality Trilogy, "fitness for use" |
| Crosby | "Quality is free", zero defects |
| Ishikawa | Fishbone diagram |
| Taguchi | Robust design |
| Shewhart | Control charts |

---

## 🔄 Cycles
- **PDCA** — Plan, Do, Check, Act (Deming)
- **DMAIC** — Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve, Control (Six Sigma)

---

## 🛠️ 7 Basic Tools
1. Cause-Effect (Fishbone/Ishikawa)
2. Flowcharts
3. Checksheets
4. Pareto chart (80/20)
5. Histograms
6. Control charts (Rule of 7)
7. Scatter diagrams

---

## 💰 COQ
**Conformance:**
- Prevention (training)
- Appraisal (testing)

**Non-Conformance:**
- Internal failure (rework)
- External failure (recalls)

🎯 Prevention is CHEAPEST.

---

## 📊 Six Sigma
- <3.4 defects per million
- DMAIC method
- 6σ = 99.99966% quality

---

## ⚖️ Tolerances vs Control Limits
- **Tolerances** — customer-specified
- **Control limits** — process-based

---

## 🎯 Mindset
- **Prevention > Inspection**
- **Built-in > Bolted-on**
- **Continuous > End-of-project**
