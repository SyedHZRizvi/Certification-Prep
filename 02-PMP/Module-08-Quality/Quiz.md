# ✏️ PMP Module 8 Quiz: Quality

## Questions

### Q1. Quality vs Grade:
A. Same thing
B. Quality = features; Grade = meets requirements
C. Quality = meets requirements; Grade = level/features
D. Both refer to cost

### Q2. The fishbone diagram is associated with:
A. Deming
B. Ishikawa
C. Crosby
D. Juran

### Q3. PDCA stands for:
A. Plan, Do, Check, Act
B. Plan, Develop, Control, Adjust
C. Performance, Defects, Costs, Actions
D. Process, Define, Control, Analyze

### Q4. Six Sigma DMAIC stands for:
A. Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve, Control
B. Define, Manage, Adjust, Improve, Close
C. Develop, Measure, Apply, Iterate, Confirm
D. Design, Make, Audit, Implement, Certify

### Q5. The 80/20 rule (Pareto):
A. 80% of work takes 20% of time
B. 80% of problems come from 20% of causes
C. 80% efficient teams have 20% turnover
D. 80% quality, 20% grade

### Q6. The "Rule of 7" in control charts indicates:
A. 7 defects per batch maximum
B. 7 consecutive points on one side = out of control
C. 7 control limits required
D. 7 process steps minimum

### Q7. Cost of Conformance includes:
A. Rework and scrap
B. Prevention and appraisal
C. Lawsuits and recalls
D. Lost reputation

### Q8. Best quality philosophy is:
A. Inspect at the end
B. Quality through prevention, built-in
C. Hire QA team
D. Audit annually

### Q9. PM identifies recurring defects in a process. Best tool to find root cause:
A. Pareto chart
B. Fishbone diagram
C. Control chart
D. Histogram

### Q10. "Quality is free" is associated with:
A. Deming
B. Juran
C. Crosby
D. Ishikawa

---

## Answers
1.C  2.B  3.A  4.A  5.B  6.B  7.B  8.B  9.B  10.C
