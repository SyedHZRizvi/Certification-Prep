# 📋 PMP Module 5 Cheat Sheet: Agile & Hybrid

---

## 🎨 Kanban
- 👀 Visualize workflow
- 🚦 Limit WIP
- 📊 Manage flow (cycle time, throughput)
- 📋 Explicit policies
- 🔄 Feedback loops
- 📈 Improve continuously

---

## ⚖️ Kanban vs Scrum

| | Scrum | Kanban |
|---|-------|--------|
| Cadence | Sprints | Continuous flow |
| Roles | PO, SM, Devs | None prescribed |
| Best for | Complex products | Steady flow / ops |

---

## 📊 8 Wastes (TIM-WOODS)
**T**ransport, **I**nventory, **M**otion, **W**aiting, **O**verproduction, **O**verprocessing, **D**efects, **S**kills (unused)

---

## 🎨 XP Practices
- 👯 Pair programming
- ✅ TDD (test-driven dev)
- 🔄 Continuous integration
- 📐 Refactoring
- 🎁 Small releases
- 👥 Collective ownership

---

## 🌟 Hybrid
Mix predictive + agile. Good when:
- Mixed certainty levels
- Regulated + innovative parts
- Hard deadlines + evolving requirements

---

## 🎯 Triangle (Fixed/Flex)

| | Fixed | Flexes |
|---|-------|--------|
| Predictive | Scope | Time/Cost |
| Agile | Time/Cost | Scope |

---

## 📊 Agile Metrics
- Velocity (points/sprint)
- Cycle time (start→done)
- Throughput (items/period)
- Lead time (request→delivery)
- CFD (cumulative flow)

---

## 🚫 PMP Agile Traps
- ❌ "PM assigns tasks" → No, team self-organizes
- ❌ "Detailed upfront plan" → No, adaptive
- ❌ "Scope locked" → No, evolves
- ❌ "PM controls standup" → No, team owns it
- ❌ "Agile = no plan" → No, continuous planning
