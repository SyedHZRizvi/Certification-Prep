# ✏️ PMP Module 4 Quiz: Business Environment

## Questions

### Q1. Project benefits are typically realized:
A. At project start
B. During the project
C. After project completion, over time
D. At sponsor sign-off

### Q2. Comparing two projects: Project A NPV $400K, Project B NPV $600K. Which is preferred?
A. Project A
B. Project B
C. Both equally
D. Need more info

### Q3. Lower is better for which?
A. NPV
B. IRR
C. Payback Period
D. ROI

### Q4. A new regulation affects your project. The PM should:
A. Continue as planned
B. Stop work immediately, no questions
C. Assess impact, propose changes, communicate to stakeholders
D. Defer to legal team

### Q5. ADKAR is a model for:
A. Project selection
B. Earned value
C. Organizational change adoption
D. Risk management

### Q6. The Business Case is:
A. The project plan
B. The justification for the project
C. The contract with the customer
D. The risk register

### Q7. BCR of 1.5 means:
A. Project is unprofitable
B. Benefits are 1.5× costs (profitable)
C. ROI is 50%
D. Payback is 1.5 years

### Q8. PM discovers a compliance gap. They should:
A. Continue and disclose at end
B. Stop, document, address, inform stakeholders
C. Hide it
D. Outsource the issue

### Q9. Stakeholders resist the new system. PM should:
A. Force adoption
B. Engage them with OCM (communication, training, support)
C. Cancel the project
D. Replace stakeholders

### Q10. Kotter's 8 steps START with:
A. Form a coalition
B. Create urgency
C. Communicate vision
D. Anchor in culture

---

## Answers
1.C  2.B  3.C  4.C  5.C  6.B  7.B  8.B  9.B  10.B
