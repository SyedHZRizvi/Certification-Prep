## ✅ After watching

Answer these in your notebook (without re-watching):

1. What does it mean to convert CapEx to OpEx, and why does the cloud do this?
2. Give one Azure service example for each of IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS.
3. What's the difference between scaling **up** and scaling **out**?
4. Name three of the six cloud benefits (HA, scalability, reliability, predictability, security, governance, manageability).
5. In the shared responsibility model, name three things that are **always** the customer's responsibility regardless of service model.

If you can answer all 5, you're ready for the [Quiz](./Quiz.md). If not, re-watch John Savill's video and the Adam Marczak IaaS/PaaS/SaaS one.
