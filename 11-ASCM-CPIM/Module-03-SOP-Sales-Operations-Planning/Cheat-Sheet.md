# 📋 Module 3 Cheat Sheet: S&OP (Sales and Operations Planning)

> One page. Print it. The 5 steps are tested *every* exam.

---

## 📅 The 5-Step S&OP Process (Wallace), MEMORIZE

| # | Step | Who Leads | Output |
|---|------|-----------|--------|
| 1 | **Product / Portfolio Review** | Product Mgmt | NPIs, transitions, EOLs |
| 2 | **Demand Review** | Sales / Marketing | Unconstrained demand plan |
| 3 | **Supply Review** | Operations | Capacity check, supply plan |
| 4 | **Pre-S&OP / Reconciliation** | Cross-functional | Decision package |
| 5 | **Executive S&OP** | CEO (Chief Executive Officer) | Ratified plan |

🧠 **P-D-S-R-E** = Product, Demand, Supply, Reconcile, Execute

---

## 🆚 S&OP vs IBP

| | S&OP | IBP |
|---|------|------|
| Horizon | 12–24 mo | 24–36+ mo |
| Scope | Demand + Supply | + Finance + Strategy |
| Currency | Units | Units AND dollars |
| Maturity | Operational | Strategic |

IBP = **mature S&OP**, not a separate process.

---

## 🧱 The 3 Aggregate Strategies

| Strategy | Production | Inventory | Workforce | Fits |
|----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|------|
| **Level** | Constant rate | Absorbs swings | Stable | Stable workforce, durable goods |
| **Chase** | Matches demand | Minimal | Flexes hard | Perishables, no inventory possible |
| **Hybrid** | Level base + chase overflow | Some | Mostly stable | Most real plants |

        ↓

        ↓

        ↓

        ↓

```

   ↓

   ↓

   ↓

```

---

## 🚦 Demand-Supply Imbalance: 4 Outcomes

| Condition | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| Demand > Supply | Add capacity, overtime, drop low-margin SKUs |
| Supply > Demand | Promotion, build backlog, idle capacity |
| Balanced | Maintain |
| Volatile vs Stable | Postponement, flex capacity, safety stock |

---

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

When you see these, often **correct**:

- ✅ "Reconcile demand, supply, and financial plans"
- ✅ "Plan at the family level"
- ✅ "Monthly rolling horizon"
- ✅ "Unconstrained demand plan, then reconcile to supply"
- ✅ "Executive ratification"

When you see these, often **wrong**:

- ❌ "S&OP plans at the SKU level"
- ❌ "Operations leads the demand review"
- ❌ "Freeze the plan after Step 5"
- ❌ "IBP and S&OP are unrelated"
- ❌ "Constrain the demand plan in Step 2"

---

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns To Recognize

- ❌ Sales and Operations each running their own forecast
- ❌ Executive S&OP without the CEO
- ❌ S&OP done weekly at SKU level (that's MPS, not S&OP)
- ❌ No financial integration in the executive plan
- ❌ Plan ratified but never replanned
