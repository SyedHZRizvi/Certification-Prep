## ✅ After Watching

Answer these reflection questions before moving on to the Quiz:

1. **Algorithm mental model:** After watching the TikTok marketing overview, can you explain in your own words why a brand-new TikTok account with zero followers can still reach 500,000 people, while a new Facebook page would struggle to reach 500?

2. **Hook diagnosis:** Watch the first 3 seconds of any TikTok ad you see today. Does it use a visual shock, a rhetorical question, or a text overlay pattern interrupt? Which of the three is most common in your niche?

3. **YouTube SEO audit:** Pick any YouTube video in a topic you know well. Check its title — does it front-load the keyword? Does the description include timestamps? How would you rewrite the title to improve CTR?

4. **Ad format selection:** A European fashion brand wants to run a YouTube campaign for a new autumn collection launch. They have a 15-second brand film and a 45-second creator-style unboxing video. Which YouTube ad format should they use for each creative, and why?

5. **GDPR connection:** A UK-based DTC brand runs TikTok Spark Ads targeting custom audiences built from website visitors. What consent mechanism must be in place for EU/UK users, and what happens to campaign performance if that mechanism is missing?
