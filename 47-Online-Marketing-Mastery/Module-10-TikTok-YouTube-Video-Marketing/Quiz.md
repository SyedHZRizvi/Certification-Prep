## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **D. Interest graph — content shown to strangers based on behavioural signals**
TikTok is built on the interest graph: it shows content to strangers based on what they engage with, not who they follow.

### Q2: **B. Video completion rate**
Video completion rate is TikTok's primary ranking signal in cascade testing; it indicates genuine viewer satisfaction.

### Q3: **D. 3 seconds**
The Hook phase is 0–3 seconds; this is the critical scroll-stopping window before the algorithm's first-pass filter.

### Q4: **B. Spark Ad**
Spark Ads boost an existing organic post; standard In-Feed Ads are created fresh inside Ads Manager.

### Q5: **C. United States and United Kingdom**
As of 2024–2025 TikTok Shop is live in the US and UK; Germany, France, and the Nordics have not yet launched it.

### Q6: **B. Second**
YouTube handles billions of searches per month, making it the second-largest search engine after Google.

### Q7: **B. Average View Duration**
AVD = Average View Duration, the average time viewers watch before leaving.

### Q8: **A. Bumper Ad**
Bumper Ads are 6-second, non-skippable ads designed for brand recall in reach campaigns.

### Q9: **D. Most users will swipe past before the 3-second threshold, signalling low interest to the algorithm and suppressing distribution**
A 10-second logo animation violates the 3-second hook rule; most users swipe past, sending a negative completion signal that suppresses distribution.

### Q10: **D. The thumbnail and title create strong curiosity but the content fails to deliver, causing viewers to leave early**
High CTR means the title/thumbnail is compelling; low AVD means the content fails to deliver on the implied promise, so viewers leave early.

### Q11: **B. Each chapter can appear as a "Key moments" rich result in Google Search, expanding organic footprint**
YouTube chapter timestamps can surface as "Key moments" rich results in Google Search, giving each chapter independent organic visibility beyond YouTube.

### Q12: **C. A cookie consent banner that obtains explicit user consent, with TikTok listed as a data processor**
Under GDPR/ePrivacy rules, a cookie consent banner obtaining explicit consent, with TikTok named as a data processor, must be in place before the pixel fires for EU visitors.

### Q13: **A. Likes, comments, shares, and follows from a Spark Ad accrue to the original organic post, building social proof**
With Spark Ads, likes, comments, shares, and follows accrue to the original organic post, building social proof; standard In-Feed Ads show zero engagement counts.

### Q14: **C. Daily Shorts as a discovery engine, one long-form video per week**
The Shorts-to-long-form funnel uses daily Shorts as a discovery engine and one weekly long-form video for depth and monetisation.

### Q15: **D. Non-Skippable In-Stream**
A 15-second brand film fits Non-Skippable In-Stream (max 15s), guaranteeing the full message is delivered for a brand-awareness goal.

### Q16: **A. 9.4%**
(3200 + 410 + 890 + 200) ÷ 50,000 × 100 = 4,700 ÷ 50,000 × 100 = 9.4%.

### Q17: **C. TikTok Shop is not yet launched in Germany; they must use link-in-bio to redirect to an external store**
TikTok Shop is not available in Germany as of 2025; the German DTC brand must drive traffic externally via link-in-bio to its own store.

### Q18: **B. 3 seconds hook, 17 seconds build, 10 seconds payoff**
Hook = 0–3s (3 seconds), Build = 3–20s (17 seconds), Payoff = 20–30s (10 seconds).

### Q19: **D. 20,000 hours**
3 × 50,000 × 8 = 1,200,000 minutes ÷ 60 = 20,000 hours of watch time per week.

### Q20: **A. The initial micro-audience was an exact niche match; the broader audience has different interests — the content may be too niche for mass appeal**
The micro-audience was a precise niche match; weaker interest alignment in the larger pool means the content may lack broad appeal beyond a tight niche.

### Q21: **C. High CTR with low Watch Time or low AVD signals to YouTube that the content disappoints viewers, suppressing search ranking despite initial click interest**
YouTube weights viewer satisfaction heavily; high CTR with low Watch Time/AVD signals the content disappoints, depressing search ranking over time.

### Q22: **A. The French brand requires a GDPR-compliant consent mechanism with the TikTok Pixel, and must ensure Standard Contractual Clauses cover data transfer outside the EU**
The French/EU brand requires a GDPR-compliant consent mechanism for pixel retargeting plus Standard Contractual Clauses for data transfer outside the EU; the US brand has no such requirement.

### Q23: **A. A video editing technique where the final frame is designed to flow back naturally to the opening frame, encouraging replays**
Loop structure means designing the final frame to flow back to the opening frame, encouraging replays — a high-weight TikTok signal.

### Q24: **C. When the viewer watches the first 5 seconds and skips**
For Skippable In-Stream (TrueView) you are not charged when a viewer watches the first 5 seconds and skips; you pay only on a 30-second view, full view if under 30s, or a click.

### Q25: **C. 85%**
TikTok Business cites that roughly 85% of TikTok videos are watched without sound, making on-screen text and captions strategically critical.
