## Algorithm Models: Interest Graph vs. Social Graph

| Dimension | Social Graph (Facebook/LinkedIn) | Interest Graph (TikTok/YT Shorts) |
|---|---|---|
| Shows content to | Your followers | Strangers who share your interests |
| New creator advantage | Low | High |
| Content shelf life | Hours–days | Weeks–months |
| Best for | Community, retargeting | Discovery, awareness, growth |

**TikTok = Pure interest graph. YouTube = Hybrid (Search = intent; Shorts/Browse = interest graph).**

---

## TikTok Cascade Testing (How the FYP Algorithm Works)

1. Seed test: 200–500 users who match broad topic signals
2. Measure: **completion rate** (primary), replays, shares, likes
3. If thresholds met → expand to 5,000–50,000
4. Repeat: each stage multiplies audience if signals hold
5. Long-tail: successful videos distribute for weeks or months

**Primary signal = Completion Rate. Optimise this above all else.**

## Hook / Build / Payoff (HBP) Framework

| Phase | Time | Goal | Technique |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Hook** | 0–3s | Stop the scroll | Visual shock, bold text, rhetorical question |
| **Build** | 3–final 10s | Deliver value, keep watching | Step reveals, "wait for it" tension |
| **Payoff** | Final 10s | Reward viewer, drive action | Surprise result, single CTA, loop back to start |

**Write the hook first. Then the ending. Then fill in the middle.**

## YouTube SEO Signals

| Signal | Weight | Key Action |
|---|---|---|
| Title + Description metadata | High | Front-load keyword; first 2–3 lines indexed by Google too |
| CTR (Click-Through Rate) | Very High | Test thumbnails; aim 4–10% |
| AVD (Average View Duration) | Very High | Hook viewers; add pattern interrupts every 60–90s |
| Watch Time (hours) | High | Post consistently; improve AVD |
| Chapter timestamps | Medium+ | Add `0:00 Intro` format for Google "Key moments" |
| Tags | Low-Medium | 500 char limit; use exact + phrase + brand |

---

## YouTube Title Formula

**[Primary Keyword] + [Benefit/Curiosity Hook] + [Qualifier]**

- Keep under 60 characters
- Front-load keyword (first 3–5 words)
- Include numbers for specificity
- Avoid clickbait without delivery (high CTR + low AVD = ranking penalty)

## GDPR Compliance Callouts

| Requirement | Why it matters | Consequence of non-compliance |
|---|---|---|
| Cookie consent banner before TikTok Pixel fires | TikTok Pixel sets third-party cookies | GDPR Art. 83: fines up to €20M or 4% global revenue |
| Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for TikTok | Pixel data may transfer outside EU | Illegal data transfer; regulatory action |
| Google Consent Mode v2 for YouTube Ads (EU) | Required for conversion tracking in EEA | Inaccurate attribution; potential GDPR exposure |
| European Accessibility Act (EAA, June 2025) | Video captions mandatory for large platforms | Non-compliance for EU-facing content |

|---|---|---|---|

## Key Formulas

**TikTok Engagement Rate**
```
ER = (Likes + Comments + Shares + Saves) / Views × 100
```

**YouTube CTR**
```
CTR = Clicks / Impressions × 100
```

**YouTube Watch Time (weekly)**
```
Watch Time = Videos × Avg Views × Avg View Duration (minutes) ÷ 60
```

**TikTok Ads Campaign Naming Convention**
```
[Objective]-[Audience]-[Creative Type]-[Date]
e.g. CONV-Lookalike25-35-UGCDemo-2025Q1
```

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns (What NOT to Do)

1. **Posting TV-style brand films on TikTok** — slow openers, logo reveals, polished production. TikTok users swipe past in 1–2 seconds.
2. **Writing the hook last** — spend 80% of scripting effort on the hook and ending, not the middle.
3. **YouTube titles with no keywords** — "My Journey" tells the algorithm nothing. "How I Did X in Y Days" is searchable.
4. **Running TikTok Ads without the Pixel installed** — no conversion data, no retargeting, no lookalikes.
5. **Using copyrighted music in monetised YouTube videos** — Content ID will mute audio or redirect revenue to rights holders.
6. **Repurposing long-form as Shorts without a native hook** — opening with "continuing from last week..." destroys completion rate.
7. **EU retargeting without GDPR consent** — illegal; fines up to €20M or 4% of global revenue.
