## The LinkedIn Algorithm — Three Phases

| Phase | Timing | What Happens | Key Signal |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1 — Bot Filter** | 0–60 min | Spam/policy check | No external links in body; max 5 hashtags |
| **Phase 2 — Small Test** | 1–3 hrs | Show to 1–5% of followers | Dwell time + early engagement velocity |
| **Phase 3 — Broad Dist.** | 3–48 hrs | Push to 2nd/3rd degree + "For You" | Phase 2 metrics exceed threshold |

**Connection Degree Reach Multiplier**

| Degree | Multiplier |
|---|---|
| 1st (direct connections) | 1.0x |
| 2nd (connections of connections) | 0.4–0.7x |
| 3rd+ / non-connected | 0.1–0.3x |

## Content Format Quick Reference

| Format | Organic Reach | Best Use | Key Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| Text-only | 1.3x (highest) | Opinion, stories, lessons | Hook in line 1; no external links in body |
| Document/Carousel (PDF) | 1.1x | Frameworks, how-to, data | Slide 1 = hook; 10–15 slides; dwell time ~45–90 sec |
| Poll | 1.2x | Research, engagement, ICP insight | Use to qualify audience interests |
| Native Video | 0.9x | Product demos, events | First 3 sec critical; captions mandatory |
| Image Post | 0.7x | Infographics, quotes | Custom design >> stock photo |
| Article/Newsletter | 0.4x feed + SEO (Search Engine Optimization) | Deep expertise | Best for SEO + newsletter subscribers |
| Sponsored Content | Paid | Lead Gen Forms, gated content | 1,200 x 628px; <150 chars body text |
| Message Ad (InMail) | Paid | Event invites, demos, trials | 30–35% open rate benchmark |

## Key Formulas

**CPL:**
```
CPL = Total Ad Spend ÷ Number of Leads
```

**ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) (Pipeline-Based):**
```
ROAS = (Leads × Average Deal Value × Close Rate) ÷ Ad Spend
Example: 50 leads × $20,000 ACV × 8% close = $80,000 pipeline ÷ $5,000 spend = 16x
```

**Employee Advocacy Multiplier:**
```
Weekly Impressions = Team Size × Avg. Connections per Employee × Post Frequency
Example: 50 employees × 500 connections × 1 post/week = 25,000 weekly impressions (free)
```

## The PEARL Thought Leadership Framework

| Letter | Element | Question it answers |
|---|---|---|
| **P** | Perspective | What is your clear, slightly controversial POV? |
| **E** | Evidence | What data/case study proves it? |
| **A** | Antagonist | What does conventional wisdom get wrong? |
| **R** | Resolution | What should readers do differently now? |
| **L** | Lead Magnet | What deeper resource can you offer (optional CTA)? |

## The HOOK–GAP–VALUE Content Framework

| Step | Purpose | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **HOOK** | Force a pause before "see more" | "I got fired for this email to our CEO (Chief Executive Officer). Here's what happened:" |
| **GAP** | Create curiosity gap; compel "see more" click | "The reason surprised everyone. It had nothing to do with money." |
| **VALUE** | Deliver insight that makes reader smarter | Concrete framework, counter-intuitive data, or actionable lesson |

## ABM Funnel on LinkedIn

```
Stage 1 — AWARENESS
Format: Sponsored Video or Carousel
Audience: All employees at target accounts (Matched Audience)
Goal: Impression share + brand recognition

Stage 2 — CONSIDERATION
Format: Document Carousel with value proposition
Audience: Specific personas (job function + seniority) within target accounts
Goal: Content engagement, dwell time

Stage 3 — DECISION
Format: Lead Gen Form or Message Ad
Audience: Engaged users (retargeting via Insight Tag or ad engagement)
Goal: MQL capture

Stage 4 — NURTURE
Format: Retargeting Sponsored Content
Audience: Form submitters who have not yet converted to meeting
Goal: Move to sales-qualified conversation
```

## Personal vs. Company Page — When to Use Each

| Task | Personal Profile | Company Page |
|---|---|---|
| Organic thought leadership | PRIMARY | Secondary (reshare) |
| All LinkedIn Ads | Must link to → | REQUIRED |
| LinkedIn Newsletter (higher engagement) | Better | Available but lower |
| Insight Tag / Retargeting | N/A | REQUIRED |
| ABM / Matched Audiences | N/A | REQUIRED |
| Analytics suite | Basic (Creator Mode) | Full |

**Key rule:** Personal profiles get 5–10x more organic reach than company pages. Lead with people; amplify with the page.

## GDPR Compliance Checklist (Europe)

| Requirement | Action |
|---|---|
| Privacy policy in Lead Gen Forms | Link your EU privacy policy in every form |
| Contact list uploads | Document lawful basis (consent or legitimate interests) |
| Insight Tag / website retargeting | Gate behind GDPR-compliant cookie consent (CMP) |
| Data retention | Define and enforce retention limits in DPA |
| Data subject rights | Provide access/deletion/portability mechanisms |
| LinkedIn DPA | Accept in Campaign Manager settings |
| Conversation Ads (EU cold outreach) | Restrict to warm/retargeting audiences only |
| Multi-language privacy policy | Set separate policy URL per language in Campaign Manager |

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns (What NOT to Do)

1. **Post only company news on your company page.** Nobody follows a page for press releases. Lead with insight; mention product last.
2. **Put external links in post body.** LinkedIn suppresses off-platform links. Always put URLs in the first comment.
3. **Run the same ad creative for more than 6 weeks.** Rotate 3+ variants monthly to fight frequency fatigue.
4. **Target under 50,000 people with Sponsored Content.** Frequency explodes, CPM skyrockets, and users hide your ads.
5. **Ignore dwell time in content design.** Every post needs a hook that makes someone stop scrolling. No hook = no reach.
6. **Wait more than 24 hours to follow up on Lead Gen Form submissions.** Response time >24 hrs yields 10x lower contact rates.
7. **Skip GDPR steps for EU campaigns.** No privacy policy link in Lead Gen Forms = legal risk and potential Campaign Manager suspension.
