## ✅ After Watching

Answer these five questions before moving to the quiz:

1. After watching the TACoS videos: what happens to ACoS and TACoS as a product builds organic rank? Which direction does each metric move, and why?
2. The FBA vs. FBM video affects advertising strategy. If FBM has lower fees but slower shipping, how should that change your break-even ACoS calculation?
3. How does Amazon's "Working Backwards" philosophy connect to the design of the second-price auction? (Hint: who does the auction serve, and how?)
4. The FBA Economics video describes Amazon's 30–45% take rate. If a product retails for £50 and Amazon takes 38%, what is the maximum ACoS that keeps the seller profitable (assume no other costs)?
5. How would you use Amazon Attribution to prove to a CFO that a £10,000 Google Ads campaign drove profitable Amazon sales?
