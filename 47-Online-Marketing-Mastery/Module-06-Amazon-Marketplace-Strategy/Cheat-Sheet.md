
| Field | US | UK | DE/FR/IT/ES |
|---|---|---|---|
| Title | 200 chars | 200 chars | 80–150 chars (category) |
| Bullet points | 5 × 500 chars | 5 × 500 chars | 5 × 500 chars |

**Backend rules:** Spaces only (no commas). No repetition. No competitor brand names. Include local-language synonyms on EU listings.

## Fulfilment Comparison — FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon) vs FBM (Fulfillment by Merchant) vs SFP

| Dimension | FBA | FBM | SFP |
|---|---|---|---|
| Prime badge | Yes (automatic) | No | Yes (if approved) |
| Buy Box advantage | Highest | Lowest | High |
| Storage fees | Yes | No | No |
| US avg fee (1 lb) | ~$3.22–$4.75 | Carrier cost | Carrier cost |
| Q4 storage rate | ~$2.40/cu ft | N/A | N/A |
| Who handles returns | Amazon | Seller | Seller (Amazon policy) |
| Best for | Standard velocity goods | Large/heavy/low-velocity | High-volume warehouse ops |

## Buy Box Factors (Priority Order)

1. Prime eligibility (FBA > SFP > FBM)
2. Landed price competitiveness
3. Seller performance metrics (ODR, LDR, VTR)
4. Stock availability
5. Shipping speed

**Key rule:** FBA + competitive price beats FBM + lowest price in most categories.

## Pan-European FBA — Decision Checklist

Enroll ONLY if ALL of the following are true:

- [ ] Monthly EU units sold > 200
- [ ] VAT registered in DE, FR, IT, ES, PL, SE, NL (7 countries)
- [ ] Responsible Person appointed under GPSR (Dec 2024)
- [ ] Product is not hazmat, perishable, or oversize
- [ ] Gross margin > 35% (to absorb VAT compliance costs)
- [ ] Product labels comply with all target-country language requirements

**UK note:** UK FBA and EU FBA are separate inventory pools post-Brexit. Budget for separate UK VAT registration (mandatory from first unit stored in UK FBA centre).

## EU Compliance Quick Reference

| Regulation | Applies To | Key Requirement |
|---|---|---|
| CE Marking | Electronics, toys, PPE, machinery | Declaration of conformity |
| REACH (1907/2006) | Chemicals, textiles, electronics | No SVHC > 0.1% |
| RoHS | Electrical/electronic equipment | No lead, mercury, etc. |
| EU 1169/2011 | All food products | Nutrition label in local language, allergens |
| EC 1924/2006 | Food supplements | Only approved health claims |
| GPSR (Dec 2024) | All consumer products | Responsible Person in EU required |

**GPSR Responsible Person cost:** Typically €500–2,000/year. Required for all non-EU sellers selling on EU Amazon marketplaces.

## EU VAT Obligation Map

| Scenario | Obligation |
|---|---|
| US seller, goods in Germany (FBA) | Register for German VAT immediately |
| US seller, goods in UK (FBA) | Register for UK VAT immediately |
| Seller with Pan-EU FBA | Register in all 7 EU FBA countries |
| B2C (Business-to-Consumer) DTC (Direct-to-Consumer) (no EU warehouse) | OSS registration if annual EU sales > €10,000 |
| UK seller, UK sales only | Register when UK revenue > £90,000/yr |

**OSS (One Stop Shop):** Allows EU sellers to file one VAT return for all EU B2C sales above €10,000/year. US/UK sellers must use the non-Union OSS scheme or register individually.

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns — What NOT To Do

1. **Copy-paste US listings to EU** — Wrong language, wrong units, wrong compliance. Always build EU listings from scratch.
2. **Enroll Pan-EU FBA before VAT is in place** — Creates immediate back-tax liability in every country where inventory lands.
3. **Use commas in backend keywords** — Amazon treats commas as characters, not separators. Use spaces only.
4. **Over-send inventory for Q4** — Long-term storage fees at $6.90/cu ft destroy margins. Send 60–90 day supply maximum.
5. **Ignore ODR until it crosses 1%** — By the time you notice it, you may already have a warning. Monitor weekly.
6. **Use unapproved health claims on EU listings** — EU law is strict. "Builds muscle" → ASIN suppressed. Use exact approved claim wording.
7. **Launch PPC to a zero-review listing** — High traffic + low conversion trains the A10 algorithm that your listing is poor quality. Get 15+ reviews first.
