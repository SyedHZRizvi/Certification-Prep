## ✅ After Watching

Answer these reflection questions in your notes — they bridge the videos back to the Reading:

1. The Trivium Group video covers US listing optimisation. Which three specific changes would you need to make to adapt that framework for an Amazon.de listing targeting German shoppers?

2. After watching the FBA vs FBM comparison, at what unit-per-month volume does FBA typically become more cost-effective than FBM for a standard 1 lb consumer product? What does this mean for your launch strategy?

3. The A10 algorithm video emphasises sales velocity. But the Reading warns against driving PPC to a listing with poor reviews. How do you resolve this tension when launching a brand-new ASIN?

4. The Pan-EU FBA expansion video mentions VAT complexity. What is the minimum number of VAT registrations a US seller would need to fully activate Pan-European FBA (include the UK)?

5. After watching the Brand Store tutorial, list three metrics you would monitor monthly in Amazon Brand Analytics to evaluate whether your Brand Store is contributing to revenue, beyond just visits.
