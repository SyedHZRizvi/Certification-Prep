## Answer Key

| Q | Ans | Explanation |
|---|-----|-------------|
| 1 | C | Amazon.de (Germany) is the largest EU marketplace by GMV, roughly 3x the size of Amazon.fr and consistently larger than Amazon.co.uk in recent years. |
| 2 | B | The A10 algorithm is fundamentally a revenue maximisation engine for Amazon. It ranks listings that generate the most revenue per search impression — a combination of CTR, conversion rate, and price. |
| 3 | C | VAT registration in the country of storage is mandatory from the first unit stored, regardless of sales volume or origin country. There is no de minimis threshold for FBA storage. |
| 4 | B | In the FISH framework: F = Feature, I = Impact, S = Social proof / Specificity, H = How-to / Use case. |
| 5 | B | German shoppers search in German, using metric units. A translated-but-not-localised listing fails on keyword indexing (English search terms not found by German shoppers) and on trust signals (pounds instead of kilograms). |
| 6 | C | The US backend search terms field accepts 250 bytes (not characters — multibyte characters count as 2 bytes). |
| 7 | C | Spaces separate keywords correctly. Commas are counted as characters and are NOT separators. Repetition wastes space without benefit. Competitor brand names violate Amazon policy. |
| 8 | B | When Amazon moves inventory to a French fulfilment centre, the seller has goods stored in France and must register for French VAT immediately. Amazon does not handle this on the seller's behalf. |
| 9 | B | Amazon Seller University (2024) cites 3–10% average conversion uplift for standard A+ Content. Premium A+ Content can achieve ~20%. |
| 10 | B | The EU Omnibus Directive requires that "was" prices in deals must reflect the lowest price charged in the preceding 30 days. Inflating a reference price before a sale to fake a larger discount is illegal across EU27 markets. |
| 11 | C | FBA receives the highest Buy Box weighting from Amazon's algorithm. SFP is second; FBM ranks lowest unless it is the only offer on the listing. |
| 12 | B | Amazon's warning threshold for ODR is 1%. Above 1% the account health dashboard flags a warning and Buy Box eligibility can be suspended. Automatic suspension typically follows repeated violations above 2%. |
| 13 | C | CE marking signifies the manufacturer's declaration that the product meets EU safety, health, and environmental requirements. It is not a government certification — it is a self-declaration backed by conformity assessment. |
| 14 | C | EC 1924/2006 (EU Health Claims Regulation) governs all nutrition and health claims on foods and supplements in the EU. Only claims on the EU register are permitted. |
| 15 | D | FBA monthly storage fees surge from approximately $0.78/cu ft (Jan–Sep) to $2.40/cu ft (Oct–Dec) — roughly a 3x increase during the peak Q4 season. |
| 16 | B | Post-Brexit, the UK is a separate customs territory. UK sellers need a UK VAT number, UK-specific inventory in UK FBA centres, and separate customs export documentation for goods moving between UK and EU. |
| 17 | C | Amazon Vine allows up to 30 Vine Voices to receive and review an enrolled ASIN. |
| 18 | B | The recommended formula is: Brand + Primary Keyword + Key Feature 1 + Key Feature 2 + Size/Count + Variant. Brand first establishes trust; primary keyword second ensures indexing. |
| 19 | B | "Builds muscle and burns fat" are unapproved health claims under EC 1924/2006. Only pre-approved claims (e.g., "contributes to normal muscle function" for Magnesium) are allowed. Using unapproved claims risks ASIN suppression and account warning. |
| 20 | C | Subscribe & Save's primary benefit is customer lifetime value — SnS subscribers purchase 3–4x more frequently than one-time buyers, dramatically improving per-customer revenue over time. |
| 21 | B | The GPSR (effective December 13, 2024) requires any non-EU seller to appoint a Responsible Person established within the EU27 who is named on the product and listing. |
| 22 | C | The A10 algorithm rewards high-converting listings with higher organic rankings. This creates a virtuous cycle: better rank → more impressions → more sales → even better rank. |
| 23 | B | SFP means the seller ships from their own facility while displaying the Prime badge. Amazon enforces strict 99%+ on-time delivery, approved carriers, and same-day/next-day dispatch. |
| 24 | B | The correct path is: register for VAT → appoint a Responsible Person → correct the labels → then request removal/relabelling. Creating a new account to circumvent a compliance hold violates Amazon's terms and can result in permanent ban. |
| 25 | C | A+ Content is not keyword-indexed but dramatically improves conversion rates through rich visuals, comparison charts, and brand storytelling. Higher conversion rate signals product quality to the A10 algorithm, indirectly supporting organic ranking. |
