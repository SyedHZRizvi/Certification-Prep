## ✅ After Watching

Reflect on these questions before moving to the quiz:

1. **Match Types in Practice:** After watching the keyword match type video, could you explain to a colleague why a florist might add "DIY," "free," and "silk" as negative keywords to their paid flower campaigns? What match type would you start with for a brand new campaign?

2. **Quality Score Reality Check:** Think about a website you use regularly. If it ran Google Ads for its own product, what do you think its Quality Score would be? What would drag it down?

3. **Bidding Strategy Decision:** A SaaS company has been running Google Ads for 3 months and just hit 45 conversions last month at an average CPA of £28. Should they move to tCPA? If yes, what target should they set first?

4. **Extension Strategy:** A UK B2B accountancy firm wants to run Google Ads targeting SME finance directors. Which three ad assets (extensions) would you recommend and why?

5. **RSA Writing Challenge:** Without looking at your notes, write 5 headline ideas (30 chars max each) for an ad group targeting the keyword `[google ads management agency london]`. Then assess: are they conceptually diverse or are they all saying the same thing?
