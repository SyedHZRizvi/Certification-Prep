Answer these from memory before moving to the quiz:

1. **Algorithm mechanics:** Name the three most important signals for TikTok's algorithm. How do they differ from Instagram's most important signal (save rate)?

2. **Platform selection:** You work for a B2B software company. Based on what you watched, what is your primary platform and why? What is the secondary?

3. **Content calendar:** What is the recommended posting cadence minimum for Instagram Reels, and what tool would you use to schedule content at scale?

4. **Influencer tiers:** A startup with a $5,000 influencer budget wants maximum ROI. Which influencer tier should they target, and approximately how many creators could they work with?

5. **Social commerce:** Name two differences between TikTok Shop and Instagram Shopping in terms of discovery mechanism and primary selling format.
