## Answer Key

| Q | Ans | Explanation |
|---|-----|-------------|
| 1 | B | A-O-F = **A**udience + **O**bjective + **F**ormat. These three factors determine platform fit. |
| 2 | C | A score of 1/3 means only one of three critical factors is satisfied. Skip the platform and invest in higher-scoring alternatives. |
| 3 | B | TikTok uses an **interest graph** — your follower count barely matters; content is distributed based on predicted user interest. Facebook uses a **social graph** — connections and relationships drive distribution. |
| 4 | B | TikTok first tests new videos with approximately 300–500 random users before deciding whether to push to larger cohorts. |
| 5 | C | Completion rate (did they watch the whole video?) and shares (especially DM shares and saves) are TikTok's most powerful signals. Likes are the weakest signal — easiest to game. |
| 6 | C | Instagram's Reels algorithm scores "entertainment value" via completion rate, replay rate, and shares from users who don't follow the account. |
| 7 | B | Instagram actively detects watermarked TikTok content and algorithmically demotes it. Always remove watermarks before cross-posting. |
| 8 | B | LinkedIn's ranking algorithm heavily weights **dwell time** — the length of time a user pauses on a post. This is why native long-form content outperforms external links. |
| 9 | C | Native document/carousel posts (PDFs uploaded directly to LinkedIn) consistently receive among the highest organic reach on the platform because they drive high dwell time as users swipe through slides. |
| 10 | C | The Netherlands has one of the highest LinkedIn penetration rates globally (>50% of working population). For professional B2B audiences, LinkedIn is the correct primary platform. |
| 11 | C | Facebook brand page organic reach is approximately **1–4%** of followers — a deliberately restricted figure designed to push brands toward paid advertising. |
| 12 | C | Under GDPR Article 6, using someone's content/image in paid advertising requires explicit written consent as the legal basis. A general hashtag indicating participation is insufficient for paid media in the EU. |
| 13 | B | Micro-influencers (10K–100K) consistently deliver the best ROI: higher engagement rates than macro/mega, lower cost per engagement, and niche audiences that align with specific buyer personas. |
| 14 | B | Germany has among the strictest influencer disclosure laws in Europe. Courts have imposed fines on influencers AND brands for insufficient disclosure. Both parties share legal risk. |
| 15 | D | The Golden Ratio recommends only **10% promotional** content: 40% Educational + 30% Entertaining + 20% Inspirational + 10% Promotional. |
| 16 | D | **utm_content** identifies the specific creative or post (e.g., "reel-15sec-v2"). utm_source = platform, utm_medium = channel type, utm_campaign = campaign name. |
| 17 | B | All major social algorithms reward **consistency**. Burst posting followed by silence damages your distribution reach because the algorithm deprioritises accounts with irregular activity. |
| 18 | B | TikTok Shop converts through entertainment and live demonstration — modelled on China's Douyin live commerce model. It is fundamentally different from catalogue-based platforms like Amazon. |
| 19 | C | The UK's ASA requires clear disclosure of all paid partnerships and gifted products. #ad and #gifted are the standard disclosure terms. Cosmetics are not exempt. |
| 20 | C | **Mention** is specifically noted for European-market-friendly operation and GDPR-compliant data handling. Talkwalker also offers EU data residency but at custom enterprise pricing. |
| 21 | B | Facebook deliberately restricts brand page reach to monetise through paid advertising. The strategic response is to invest in **employee advocacy** (individual employees posting from personal accounts) and executive thought leadership, which retains higher reach, alongside brand pages. |
| 22 | B | LinkedIn actively demotes external links (to YouTube, websites, etc.) to keep users on-platform. Native video receives dramatically higher organic reach because it keeps the user engaged on LinkedIn. |
| 23 | C | Raw likes are the weakest engagement signal and can be misleading. A savvy analyst asks about **save rate** (strongest Instagram signal indicating true value) and **engagement rate relative to reach** (not just followers). |
| 24 | B | France's 2023 influencer law requires that AI-altered images in commercial content carry a "virtual content" or "retouched image" label — one of the first laws globally to mandate this. |
| 25 | B | Without any promotional content, an editorial calendar — however well-balanced — never drives followers to take action. The Golden Ratio recommends ~10% promotional posts. Even at low frequency, CTAs are essential for conversion. |
