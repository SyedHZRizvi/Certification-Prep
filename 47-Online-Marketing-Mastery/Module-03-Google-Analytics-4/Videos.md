## ✅ After Watching

1. After the GA4 fundamentals video: open a GA4 Demo Account (google.com/analytics/web/demoAccount) and navigate to Explore → Funnel Exploration. Can you build a 4-step checkout funnel from scratch?

2. After the Consent Mode v2 video: list the four consent parameters and explain in plain English what happens to a German user who clicks "Reject All" on a CMP — what data does GA4 still collect, and how is it used?

3. After the attribution models video: pull up the Model Comparison report in a GA4 property (or the demo account). What channel would gain or lose the most credit if you switched from Last Click to Data-Driven? What does that tell you about the role of that channel?

4. After the ecommerce tracking video: sketch out the DataLayer push for a purchase event for a UK bookshop — include `currency: "GBP"`, at least two items, and a correct `transaction_id` format. Why is currency specification critical for multi-market properties?

5. After all videos: explain the Kontor Mode story from the reading in your own words to someone who has never used GA4. If they ask "why didn't you just look at the normal Analytics report?" — what do you say?
