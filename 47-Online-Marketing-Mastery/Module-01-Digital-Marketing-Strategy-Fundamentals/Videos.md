## After Watching

Pause after each video and answer these before moving on:

1. **After Adam Erhart's overview:** Can you sketch the marketing funnel from memory and name one content type appropriate for each stage? What channel would Dollar Shave Club have used for each stage?

2. **After the RACE framework video:** What is the difference between "Reach" and "Act" in the RACE model? Give a real-world example of a metric you would track at each stage for an e-commerce brand.

3. **After the buyer persona videos:** What is the single biggest difference between a demographic target and a buyer persona? Create a 5-line persona for a student taking this course (give them a name, job, goal, pain point, and preferred content channel).

4. **After the GDPR video:** Name two specific things a European email marketer must do differently compared to a North American marketer under GDPR. What is the maximum fine under GDPR, and which company received the largest fine to date?

5. **After the cookieless future video:** Name three technologies or strategies that can replace third-party cookies for audience targeting. Which of these is within your control as a marketer (i.e., not dependent on a platform)?
