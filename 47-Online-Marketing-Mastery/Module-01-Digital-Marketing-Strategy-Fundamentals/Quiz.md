## Answer Key

| Q | Ans | Explanation |
|---|-----|-------------|
| 1 | B | SOSTAC = Situation, Objectives, Strategy, Tactics, Actions, Control. Developed by PR Smith in the 1990s. |
| 2 | C | RACE = Reach, Act, Convert, Engage. The "A" is Act — encouraging interaction on-site, not just traffic. |
| 3 | B | The viral video built brand awareness at zero/near-zero cost — classic TOFU owned media. It created awareness, not direct conversions. |
| 4 | D | LTV (Lifetime Value) measures total revenue from a customer over their relationship. CAC is cost to acquire; CPA is cost per conversion; ROAS is revenue per ad spend. |
| 5 | B | CAC = £80,000 ÷ 2,000 = £40 per customer. |
| 6 | C | ROAS = £120,000 ÷ £30,000 = 4x. Memorise: ROAS = Revenue ÷ Ad Spend. |
| 7 | C | 3:1 is the standard benchmark. At 1:1 you break even; at 2:1 you're marginal; 3:1+ is healthy; 5:1+ means you could invest more aggressively. |
| 8 | B | ROAS only measures gross revenue vs ad spend — it ignores COGS, fulfilment, overhead. ROI measures net profit vs total investment. A 4x ROAS with 20% margins can still be unprofitable. |
| 9 | B | GDPR requires opt-in — explicit, affirmative consent before collecting personal data. No pre-ticked boxes. The burden is on the company to prove consent was given. |
| 10 | B | CCPA is opt-out by default — data can be collected and shared unless the consumer actively opts out via the "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" mechanism. |
| 11 | C | TOFU content creates awareness. Blog posts, viral videos, and social content are awareness plays — reaching people who don't yet know your brand. |
| 12 | B | Comparing options and reading case studies = consideration stage = MOFU. The prospect knows the category exists and is evaluating solutions. |
| 13 | D | The four walled gardens in this module are Google, Meta, Amazon, and TikTok. LinkedIn is a significant ad platform but was not included in the "walled garden" four discussed here. |
| 14 | B | The key distinction: demographics describe who (age, income, location); personas describe why and how (goals, pain points, behaviour, channels). Two 28-year-old men can have completely opposite buying behaviour. |
| 15 | B | GDPR maximum fine: €20 million OR 4% of global annual revenue, whichever is higher. This is why Meta's €1.2B fine in 2023 was possible — 4% of their global revenue. |
| 16 | C | iDEAL is the dominant payment method in the Netherlands, used in over 60% of online transactions. Failing to offer it dramatically reduces conversion rates for Dutch customers. |
| 17 | D | Revenue = $150,000. Gross margin = 25%, so gross profit = $37,500. Subtract ad spend of $50,000: $37,500 - $50,000 = -$12,500. Wait — the question asks for "actual profit," which = $150,000 × 25% margin = $37,500 gross profit, minus $50,000 ad spend = -$12,500 loss. Answer: D ($12,500... actually this is a loss of $12,500). Note: A ROAS of 3x with 25% margins loses money — ROAS must exceed 1/margin (here 1/0.25 = 4x) to break even on ad spend alone. |
| 18 | B | "Act" in RACE = micro-conversions: newsletter signup, video view, demo request, content download. "Convert" = full purchase/lead. "Reach" = traffic. |
| 19 | C | Google Consent Mode v2 allows GA4 to use behavioural modelling to fill data gaps for users who decline cookies — essential for EU market measurement compliance. |
| 20 | C | PR Smith developed SOSTAC in the 1990s. Dave Chaffey developed RACE. Philip Kotler is known for the 4Ps. Neil Patel is a digital marketing practitioner/blogger. |
| 21 | A | LTV:CAC = $180 ÷ $90 = 2:1. This is below the 3:1 healthy benchmark. Doubling spend on a marginal ratio risks scaling losses. Fix unit economics first. |
| 22 | C | CASL requires express or implied consent AND requires the sender to identify themselves in every message. It is one of the strictest anti-spam laws in the world — stricter than CAN-SPAM. |
| 23 | B | A walled garden is a closed ecosystem (Google, Meta, Amazon, TikTok) where the platform controls data, inventory, measurement, and audience. Advertisers can buy within the walls but cannot easily extract or independently verify the data. |
| 24 | B | iOS 14's App Tracking Transparency framework prevented Meta's Pixel from tracking conversions across apps for users who opted out (most did). Meta compensates by modelling — which inflates reported ROAS. Always cross-reference ad platform data with your actual analytics/CRM. |
| 25 | C | The funnel runs from broadest (most people) to narrowest: TOFU (awareness) → MOFU (consideration) → BOFU (conversion) → Retention (loyalty). This is one of the most exam-tested sequences in digital marketing. |
