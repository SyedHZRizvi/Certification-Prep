Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. Plain-language explanation of what data we collect and why.

Short version: We collect only your name and email address to give you access to the courses you enrolled in. We do not sell your data, do not run advertising, and do not share your information with anyone except the email delivery service used to send you login links.

1. Who We Are

The Cert Hub is operated by Humayun Zafar, Toronto, Ontario, Canada. For all privacy matters, you can reach the data controller at syed@transcrypts.com.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data needed to operate the service:

Data Why We Collect It Legal Basis
Email address Your unique identifier and the address we send your login link to Contract performance; necessary to deliver the service
Name Personalization within the platform and on completion certificates Contract performance
Enrolled course list Controls which course content you can access Contract performance
Session token (cookie) Keeps you signed in for 30 days so you don't re-login on every visit Legitimate interest (essential functionality); strictly necessary
Notes field (optional) Internal admin notes about your enrollment (e.g. which cohort, payment reference) Legitimate interest (record keeping)

We do not collect passwords (we use magic-link / passwordless authentication), payment card data (handled entirely by the payment processor of your choice at the time of enrollment), location data, device fingerprints, or behavioral tracking data.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for exactly the following purposes:

  • Authentication — to verify your identity when you sign in.
  • Access control — to serve you the course content you are enrolled in and block content you have not enrolled in.
  • Transactional email — to deliver your sign-in (magic link) emails.
  • Completion certificates — to populate your name on any certificate you generate.
  • Enrollment administration — internal record-keeping for your account.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose not listed above.

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

Cloudflare Workers KV stores your account record (email, name, course list). Data is distributed globally across Cloudflare's edge network, which has data centres in North America, Europe, and elsewhere. Cloudflare is a processor acting under our instructions, governed by a Data Processing Agreement and certified under applicable data protection frameworks.

Resend.com is used solely to deliver magic-link emails. Your email address is passed to Resend for each login request and is not retained by Resend beyond the delivery event. Resend's privacy policy is available at resend.com/privacy.

No other third-party services receive your personal data.

5. Cookies

We set one cookie: ch_session — a signed, encrypted JWT stored as an HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax cookie. It expires after 30 days or when you sign out (whichever comes first). This cookie is strictly necessary for the site to function; it cannot be opted out of while you are signed in.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies of any kind.

6. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your enrollment is active. If you request deletion of your account, your record will be removed from our systems within 30 days. Note that deletion terminates your course access immediately and permanently.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including if you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Canada), you may have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your account and personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction — request that we restrict processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Portability — request your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdrawal of consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time (this does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal).

To exercise any of these rights, email syed@transcrypts.com with "Privacy Request" in the subject line. We will respond within 30 days. We do not charge a fee for reasonable requests.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices: HTTPS-only delivery, HttpOnly/Secure/SameSite session cookies, HMAC-signed JWTs, single-use time-limited authentication tokens, and server-side access controls for all course content. No passwords are stored anywhere in our system.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we take all reasonable measures to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

The Cert Hub is intended for adults preparing for professional certification examinations. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the revision date. Continued use of the Site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. For material changes, we will endeavour to notify enrolled students by email.

11. Contact and Supervisory Authority

For any privacy question or to exercise a right listed in Section 7, contact:

Humayun Zafar
The Cert Hub
Toronto, Ontario, Canada
syed@transcrypts.com

If you are located in the EEA and believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.
