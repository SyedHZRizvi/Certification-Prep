Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. Plain-language explanation of what data we collect and why.

Short version: When you submit an enrollment application we collect your name, email, phone number, address, and professional background to review your application. After enrollment, we retain your email, name, and course list to give you platform access. We do not sell your data, do not run advertising, and do not share your information with third parties beyond the email delivery service and Cloudflare infrastructure described in Section 4.

1. Who We Are

The Cert Hub is operated by Humayun Zafar, Toronto, Ontario, Canada. For all privacy matters, you can reach the data controller at syed@transcrypts.com.

We do not collect passwords as your primary login method is password-based (see Section 8); payment card data is handled entirely by the payment processor at the time of enrollment; location data, device fingerprints, or behavioral tracking data are not collected.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for exactly the following purposes:

  • Authentication — to verify your identity when you sign in.
  • Access control — to serve you the course content you are enrolled in and block content you have not enrolled in.
  • Transactional email — to deliver your sign-in (magic link) emails.
  • Completion certificates — to populate your name on any certificate you generate.
  • Enrollment administration — internal record-keeping for your account.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose not listed above.

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

Cloudflare Workers KV stores your account record (email, name, course list). Data is distributed globally across Cloudflare's edge network, which has data centres in North America, Europe, and elsewhere. Cloudflare is a processor acting under our instructions, governed by a Data Processing Agreement. Cloudflare's transfers from the EEA are covered by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and Cloudflare participates in the EU-US Data Privacy Framework. See cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

Resend.com is used to deliver transactional emails (login / password-reset links and enrollment confirmation notifications). Your email address is passed to Resend for each such event. Resend is a US-based service; transfers from the EEA are governed by Standard Contractual Clauses. See resend.com/privacy.

Google Fonts and Tailwind CDN are loaded from external CDN providers (fonts.googleapis.com and cdn.tailwindcss.com) when you visit the site. This causes your IP address to be transmitted to Google LLC and Cloudflare CDN as part of normal web delivery. We intend to self-host these assets in a future update to eliminate this incidental transfer. Until then, these are disclosed as data processors for page-delivery purposes only.

No other third-party services receive your personal data. Your enrollment application data is not shared with any third party.

5. Cookies

We set one cookie: ch_session — a signed, encrypted JWT stored as an HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax cookie. It expires after 30 days or when you sign out (whichever comes first). This cookie is strictly necessary for the site to function; it cannot be opted out of while you are signed in.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies of any kind.

6. Data Retention

Enrollment application data (phone, address, employer, education fields collected on the enrollment form) is retained for 90 days if your application is not accepted, or until your account is deleted if accepted. Professional background fields (employer, job title, years of experience, motivation) are not retained after enrollment review is complete.

Active account data (email, name, enrolled course list) is retained for as long as your enrollment is active. If you request deletion of your account, your record will be removed from our systems within 30 days. Note that deletion terminates your course access immediately and permanently. We review and delete inactive accounts that have had no login activity for 36 months.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including if you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Canada), you may have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your account and personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction — request that we restrict processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Portability — request your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdrawal of consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time (this does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal).

To exercise any of these rights, email syed@transcrypts.com with "Privacy Request" in the subject line. We will respond within 30 days. We do not charge a fee for reasonable requests.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices: HTTPS-only delivery, HttpOnly/Secure/SameSite session cookies, HMAC-signed JWTs, single-use time-limited password-reset tokens, and server-side access controls for all course content. The platform uses password-based authentication as the primary sign-in method, with a magic-link / passwordless fallback available for password recovery. Passwords are stored using strong hashing; plaintext passwords are never stored.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we take all reasonable measures to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

The Cert Hub is intended for adults preparing for professional certification examinations. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the revision date. Continued use of the Site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. For material changes, we will endeavour to notify enrolled students by email.
