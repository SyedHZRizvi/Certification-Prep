# ⚡ Module 10 Cheat Sheet, Showreel & Client Work

## Four-Zone Showreel Structure

| Zone | Timing | Content |
|------|--------|---------|
| Hook | 0–5s | Single strongest piece, stop the skip |
| Body | 5–45s | 6–10 pieces, 4–8 seconds each |
| Climax | 45–55s | Longer complex piece, prove depth |
| Close | 55–60s | Name, contact, website |

---

## Ruthless Edit Rules

- 60 seconds MAX; 45 seconds preferred
- If you hesitate to include it: CUT IT
- 8 excellent pieces > 12 mixed pieces
- Strongest FIRST and LAST (primacy + recency)
- Alternate fast/slow; don't put similar styles adjacent
- Watch on a phone, not a monitor
- Watch on mute, does it work?
- Compare to your 3 favorite reels immediately after

---

## Specificity Test

For each reel piece ask: **"Why is this in the reel? What specific skill does it demonstrate?"**

If the answer is vague → CUT IT.

## Project Rate Structure

- Always include **2 rounds of revisions**, state explicitly
- Define revision: "one consolidated set of changes, submitted together"
- Additional rounds: quote separately at hourly rate
- Get all revision requests in writing

## Spec Work Policy

**Do not do spec work.**

Reason: devalues profession, normalizes unpaid creative, no recourse.

Alternative: paid pitches (small fee for pitch time) or no pitch.

---

## Scope Creep Response

When a "quick change" becomes a redesign:

> "That goes beyond the original scope. I'd be happy to quote that as a separate phase of work."

Never do scope-expanding work without agreement on compensation.

---

## Showreel Licensing (Client Work)

Can include in reel if:
1. Work is publicly released
2. No NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) covers it
3. Client credited in reel/description
4. No confidential business information exposed

When in doubt: ask the client.
