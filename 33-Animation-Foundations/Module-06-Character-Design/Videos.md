## ✅ After Watching

1. Pick any animated film. Describe the primary shape (circle/square/triangle) of the protagonist, antagonist, and mentor.
2. Apply the silhouette test to a character you like: could you identify them in pure black?
3. List three color associations from this module. Find an animated character that subverts one intentionally.
