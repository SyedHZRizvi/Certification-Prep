## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

**Q1, B.** Reel B won because of strategy: strongest shot first, no filler, 1:45 tight runtime. The work quality was comparable.

**Q2, C.** Open with your strongest shot. Recruiters decide in the first 10 seconds.

**Q3, C.** 90 seconds is ideal; 2 minutes is the absolute maximum. Every second must be strong.

**Q4, B.** The weakest shot sets the perceived quality floor. Recruiters assume it represents a common quality level.

**Q5, B.** Tailoring shows relevant expertise and avoids diluting the reel with irrelevant work.

**Q6 C.** Vimeo ad-free, high quality, password protection. Industry preferred for professional reel hosting.

**Q7, C.** ArtStation is the dominant portfolio platform in the game art industry.

**Q8, C.** 75% of animation jobs are not publicly posted; they are filled through the network.

**Q9, C.** Turntable shots (character rotating with no performance) should not be included. They show modeling, not animation performance.

**Q10, C.** Vimeo for reel hosting + ArtStation for full game-industry portfolio.

**Q11, C.** Senior Animator at Disney/Pixar = $120K–$165K US base (2025–2026).

**Q12, B.** Significant equity and bonus components multiply TC at senior+ levels at top gaming studios.

**Q13, C.** Studio = stable income + benefits + structured skill development. Primary advantage for junior animators.

**Q14, B.** Freelance primary disadvantage = variable income (feast-or-famine) + no employer-provided benefits.

**Q15, B.** The common pattern: studio first (skills + network) → freelance later (once established).

**Q16, B.** Festivals provide networking opportunities with working professionals. Not primarily for award-winning or guaranteed recruiting.

**Q17, B.** Label precisely what your specific contribution was to collaborative work.

**Q18, C.** Strongest character performance/pantomime first. Opens with maximum impact.

**Q19, B.** Consistent posting, events, and thoughtful engagement with professionals' work.

**Q20, B.** Streaming studios pay comparably or higher than feature film due to competition for talent.

**Q21, B.** LinkedIn + Vimeo reel + ArtStation/personal website = the professional standard setup.

**Q22, B.** Extract and include only the strongest 20–30 seconds. Label it. Do not include the full 3-minute film.

**Q23, C.** Base salaries are comparable at lead level; Riot's TC advantage appears at the equity/bonus level.

**Q24, B.** A polished, publicly hosted character performance that demonstrates clear emotional range, weight, and consistent improvement is the strongest professional credential.
