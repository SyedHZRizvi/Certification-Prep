## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **C. Policy**
Policy > Standard > Procedure > Guideline.

### Q2: **C. ALE**
SLE × ARO = ALE.

### Q3: **B. $50,000**
SLE = $400k × 0.25 = $100k. ALE = $100k × 0.5 = **$50,000**.

### Q4: **C. Risk transfer**
Insurance shifts loss to a third party.

### Q5: **B. Residual risk**
Risk that remains after controls.

### Q6: **B. Risk willing to pursue**
Appetite = strategic level of risk-taking.

### Q7: **B. Risk register**
Central catalog of risks + treatments + owners.

### Q8: **C. MSA**
Master Service Agreement = umbrella terms.

### Q9: **B. SOW**
Statement of Work = project-specific scope under an MSA.

### Q10: **C. Service performance metrics**
SLA = uptime, response time, remedies.

### Q11: **A. MOU**
MOU = non-binding intent. MOA = binding agreement.

### Q12: **B. ISA**
Interconnection Security Agreement governs system-to-system connections.

### Q13: **B. DPA**
GDPR mandates a Data Processing Agreement with processors.

### Q14: **A. BAA**
Business Associate Agreement is the HIPAA-required vendor contract.

### Q15: **B. Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover**
The 5 NIST CSF v1 functions. (CSF 2.0 adds "Govern.")

### Q16: **C. CIS Controls v8**
18 prioritized controls.

### Q17: **A. ISMS existence**
ISO 27001 attests to a working Information Security Management System.

### Q18: **B. Anyone handling cardholder data**
PCI-DSS scope.

### Q19: **B. Max acceptable downtime**

### Q20: **B. Max acceptable data loss in time**
If RPO = 4 hrs, you can lose up to 4 hours of data.

### Q21: **C. Hot site**
Active sync + immediate failover.

### Q22: **A. 3 copies / 2 media / 1 off-site**

### Q23: **B. Immutable / air-gapped backup**
Cannot be modified or deleted by ransomware.

### Q24: **B. Attestation**
SOC 2 Type II is an attestation by a CPA firm.

### Q25: **B. DPA + BAA**
GDPR requires DPA; HIPAA requires BAA.

### Q26: **C. Documented acceptance**
Acceptance is legitimate when documented and signed off.
