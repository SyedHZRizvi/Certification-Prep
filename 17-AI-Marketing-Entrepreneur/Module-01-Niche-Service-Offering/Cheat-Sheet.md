# 📋 Module 1 Cheat Sheet: Niche & Service Offering

> One page. Print it. Tape it to your monitor. Review before the exam.

---

## 🎯 The Niche-First Mantra

```
WHO  +  WHAT  +  WHY YOU
audience  deliverable  proof point
```

🧠 If you can't say it in one sentence, niche tighter.

---

## 🧪 The 100/10/1 Niche Test

| # | Question | Pass test |
|---|---|---|
| 100 | Can I list 100 named companies? | Yes → niche has volume |
| 10 | Can I find 10 watering holes? | Yes → marketing is affordable |
| 1 | Can I name 1 painful, shared, undersolved problem? | Yes → wedge is real |

---

## 🔪 The Three Wedge Axes

| Axis | Example | Year-2 ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue) ceiling |
|---|---|---|
| Vertical (industry) | "AI marketing for dental groups" | $300K–$700K |
| Channel (medium) | "Klaviyo expert for Shopify" | $250K–$600K |
| Deliverable (productized) | "The $1,500 Google Ads audit" | $400K–$800K |
| **Stacked (2 axes)** | "Google Ads management for recovery centers" | **$700K–$1.5M** |

🏆 **Most high-performers stack 2 axes.**

---

## 💼 Pricing Models, Pick One

| Model | New founders | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Hourly | ❌ Avoid | Punishes AI speed |
| Retainer | ✅ With rules | 3-mo min, auto-renew, capped scope, QBR |
| Productized | ✅✅ Best | Fixed scope + price + process |

## 🚫 The Six Mistakes Year-1 Founders Make

1. "I help businesses grow" positioning
2. Pricing at last-job hourly rate
3. Building the "perfect" website before getting clients
4. Saying yes to off-niche clients
5. Hiring too early
6. Confusing client work with business-building work

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

When you see these in answers, they're often correct:

- ✅ "Niche by industry + size + deliverable..."
- ✅ "Productize the deliverable..."
- ✅ "Validate with the 100/10/1 test..."
- ✅ "Same scope, same price, same process..."

When you see these, they're often **wrong**:

- ❌ "Be a full-service marketing partner..."
- ❌ "Bill hourly to be safe..."
- ❌ "Take any client who pays..."
- ❌ "Hire help in week one..."

---

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns To Recognize

- ❌ Positioning around "businesses" or "brands" generally
- ❌ Hourly billing in an AI-leveraged practice
- ❌ Saying yes to off-niche inquiries in Year 1
- ❌ Niche too narrow to find 100 companies
- ❌ Spending Year 1 on logo and brand before clients
