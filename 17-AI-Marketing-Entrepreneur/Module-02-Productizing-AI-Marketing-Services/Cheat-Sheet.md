## 📦 The 5 Fixed Anchors (MEMORIZE)

```
1. SCOPE    , same deliverables each time
2. PRICE    , same number, on the website
3. PROCESS  , same steps + tools + templates
4. TIME     , same delivery window
5. OUTCOME  , sold by result, not labor
```

🧠 Fail any one anchor → you're back in consulting hell.

## 🧮 Pricing Formula

```
Price = (hours × effective $/hr target) ÷ (1 − overhead %)
```

Year 1 target: $150–$250/hr effective
Year 2+: $300–$500/hr effective
Target margin: 60–80%

🎯 Always run the "**3x sanity check**", try selling at 3x your gut price.

---

## 🛡️ Scope Control Rules

1. Define out-of-scope in the SOW
2. Kickoff Loom, not open-ended call
3. Templatize the deliverable
4. Scope-change menu (separate SKU (Stock Keeping Unit) prices)
5. ONE revision round; overflow at flat fee

---

## 📜 SLA (Service Level Agreement) Quick Reference

| Element | Typical |
|---|---|
| Response time | 1 business day |
| Delivery window | Match SKU (5d / 14d / 21d / 28d) |
| Revisions | 1 included; $250–$500 each after |
| Office hours | M–F 9–6 local |
| Refund | 25% within 48 hr; 0% after |
| Late penalty | 10%/business day, max 50% |

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

When you see these in answers, they're often correct:

- ✅ "Fixed scope, fixed price, fixed process..."
- ✅ "Productize the audit, then sell the retainer..."
- ✅ "Templatize the deliverable..."
- ✅ "Use AI to 5x the analysis work..."
- ✅ "One revision round, flat fee for overflow..."

When you see these, they're often **wrong**:

- ❌ "Quote each client a custom price..."
- ❌ "Unlimited revisions..."
- ❌ "Open kickoff call..."
- ❌ "Bill hourly to be safe..."
- ❌ "10 SKUs to look credible..."

---

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns To Recognize

- ❌ Productized + custom scope = false product
- ❌ Long open-ended kickoff calls
- ❌ Unlimited revisions
- ❌ Vague deliverables ("we'll do some analysis")
- ❌ No out-of-scope language
- ❌ Putting 10+ SKUs on Year-1 website
