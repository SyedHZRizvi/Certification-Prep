# 📋 Module 4 Cheat Sheet: Pricing, Proposals & Contracts

> One page. Print it. Tape it to your monitor. Review before the exam.

---

## 💡 The 5 Pricing Mindsets

```
1. Price the OUTCOME, not the time
2. Price for the BUYER'S WALLET, not yours
3. NEVER quote on the call
4. ANCHOR first, then price
5. Be comfortable with NO
```

🧠 **Healthy close rate: 30–50%.** Too high = underpriced.

---

## 🧠 The Good-Better-Best Structure

| Option | Role | % chosen | Multiple of A |
|---|---|---|---|
| A — Low | Decoy | 10–20% | 1x |
| **B — Middle** | **Target** | **60–70%** | 1.5–2x |
| C — High | Anchor | 10–25% | 2.5–3x |

---

## 📝 The 7-Section Proposal

```
1. Cover + Recap (mirror their words)
2. The Problem (quantified in $)
3. Our Approach (3 phases)
4. PROJECTED OUTCOMES (the closer!)
5. Investment (3 options)
6. Timeline + Next Steps
7. Logistics + FAQ
```

🎯 **Keep it 6–10 pages.** Send with 5–7 day expiration.

---

## 📑 Critical Contract Clauses

| Clause | Typical |
|---|---|
| Payment terms | 50/50 OR Net-7 |
| IP transfer | On full payment |
| Kill fee | 25–50% of unbilled |
| Late fee | 1.5%/mo overdue |
| Liability cap | Fees paid last 6–12 mo |
| Termination | 30-day notice |
| AI disclosure | Required 2025+ |

⚖️ **Not legal advice — have a licensed attorney review your template once ($400–$1,200).**

```
Day 0   — Discovery call (45–60 min)

Day 2   — Send w/ 5–7 day expiry
```

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

When you see these in answers, they're often correct:

- ✅ "Price the outcome, not the hours..."
- ✅ "Anchor with a higher reference price..."
- ✅ "Send a proposal with 3 options..."
- ✅ "MSA + SOW structure..."
- ✅ "Include the ROI projection section..."

When you see these, they're often **wrong**:

- ❌ "Quote on the call..."
- ❌ "Match competitor pricing..."
- ❌ "Skip the contract for small jobs..."
- ❌ "30-page proposals to look credible..."
- ❌ "Accept Net 60 without question..."

---

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns To Recognize

- ❌ Quoting on the discovery call
- ❌ Single price (no good-better-best)
- ❌ Long 30-page proposals
- ❌ No ROI projection
- ❌ No kill fee or liability cap
- ❌ Unilateral acceptance of Net 60+
