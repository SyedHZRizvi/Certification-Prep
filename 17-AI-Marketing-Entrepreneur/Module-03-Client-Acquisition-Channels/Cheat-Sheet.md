## 🎯 The 7 Channels (Pick 2)

```
1. OUTBOUND      → fastest (14–45 days to first client)
2. INBOUND       → compounding (6–18 months)
3. REFERRALS     → highest close rate (35–55%)
4. NETWORKS      → relationship-based
5. PARTNERSHIPS  → white-label or co-marketing
6. EVENTS        → lumpy, high-leverage
7. PAID ADS      → only above $7,500 ACV
```

🧠 **Year-1 default: Outbound + Inbound.**

---

## 📤 Outbound Math (1,000 sends)

```
1,000 sends
→ 30 positive replies (3%)
→ 13 booked calls (45%)
→ 9 shows (70%)
→ 2 closes (22%)
→ ~$10,000 revenue (at $5K avg ACV)
```

Stack cost: ~$220–$240/mo

## ✉️ PVCC Cold Email Script

```
Subject: quick question, [first name]

Hi [first name],

Saw [specific recent activity]. Congrats.

Most [ICP] are running [channel] with two issues:
  - [specific problem 1]
  - [specific problem 2]

I run [productized service] ($X, Y days). Last 3 [audits/builds] saved/won [concrete number].

Worth a 20-minute call this week to see if we'd be a fit?

— [signature]
```

🎯 **Under 110 words.** Subject lowercase. Specific opener.

```
For each referral that closes:
  - $500 cash to referrer, OR
  - $750 service credit
  - Public thank-you (with opt-out)
```

Ask at day 60–90 after a clear win.

## 💰 Paid Ads — Min ACV Floor

| ACV | Verdict |
|---|---|
| <$5K | ❌ Don't bother |
| $5–10K | ⚠️ Marginal |
| $10–25K | ✅ Viable |
| $25K+ | ✅ Strong |

CAC typically $800–$2,500 per booked call.

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

When you see these in answers, they're often correct:

- ✅ "Pick two channels and go deep..."
- ✅ "Outbound for cashflow, inbound for compounding..."
- ✅ "Cold email under 110 words..."
- ✅ "Build a documented referral program..."

When you see these, they're often **wrong**:

- ❌ "Run all 7 channels at once..."
- ❌ "Wait for referrals to come..."
- ❌ "Paid ads for any offer..."
- ❌ "Long consultative cold email..."

---

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns To Recognize

- ❌ 7-channel "spray and pray"
- ❌ One-touch cold email sends
- ❌ Generic "Hope this finds you well" openers
- ❌ Paid ads to <$5K ACV
- ❌ Joining 5 communities at once
- ❌ Pitching in LinkedIn connection notes
