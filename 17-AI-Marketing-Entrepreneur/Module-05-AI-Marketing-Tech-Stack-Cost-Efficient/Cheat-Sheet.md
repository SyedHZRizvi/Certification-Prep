# 📋 Module 5 Cheat Sheet: AI Marketing Tech Stack

> One page. Print it. Tape it to your monitor. Review before the exam.

---

## 💸 The 4-Question Subscription Test

```
Before subscribing, EVERY tool must pass:

1. Enables a billable workflow?
2. No free/cheaper substitute does 80%?
3. Used 3+ times/week?
4. Pays for itself in 1 client interaction/quarter?

Any "no" → cancel.
```

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

When you see these in answers, they're often correct:
- ✅ "Apply the 4-question test..."
- ✅ "Pick ONE SEO tool..."
- ✅ "Skip enterprise tools Year 1..."
- ✅ "Use free tiers where they cover 80%..."
- ✅ "Tool spend should be 1–2% of revenue..."

When you see these, they're often **wrong**:
- ❌ "Subscribe to everything the influencer recommends..."
- ❌ "Both Ahrefs and SEMrush..."
- ❌ "HubSpot Marketing Hub Pro Year 1..."
- ❌ "Pirate or share seats..."
- ❌ "Upgrade plans 'to unlock features'..."

---

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns To Recognize

- ❌ Tool sprawl (Marcus's $4,200/mo)
- ❌ Multiple overlapping SEO tools
- ❌ Enterprise CRM Year 1
- ❌ No password manager / MFA
- ❌ Zapier at high volume (use n8n)
