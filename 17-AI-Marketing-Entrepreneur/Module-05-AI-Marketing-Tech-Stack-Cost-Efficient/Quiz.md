## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **D. Did a competitor recommend it?**
Test is: workflow, substitute, frequency, payback. Competitor recommendation is irrelevant.

### Q2: **B. ~$200/mo**
Tier-1 solo, scrappy. LLM + image + SEO + billing + free everything else.

### Q3: **B. ~$430/mo**
Tier-2 adds outbound stack on top of Tier-1.

### Q4: **C. Crypto wallet**
Not part of an agency operations stack.

### Q5: **B. ChatGPT Plus OR Claude Pro at $20/mo**
Pick one paid; use the free tier of the other as a secondary.

### Q6: **B. Make sweet spot; n8n at high volume; Zapier for hard connectors**
The cost curve analysis from the reading.

### Q7: **B. ONE of Ahrefs Lite OR SEMrush Pro**
Both = redundant. Pick one.

### Q8: **B. GA4 + Search Console + Looker Studio + Microsoft Clarity (free)**
Sufficient for 90% of Year-1 needs.

### Q9: **B. Bonsai ($25) or PandaDoc ($35)**
All-in-one proposals + contracts + invoicing.

### Q10: **C. Notion Free**
Notion Free is great for solos. The other three are enterprise overkill.

### Q11: **B. $4,200/mo with $0 revenue**
The cautionary tale from the opening story.

### Q12: **B. 1–2% of revenue**
Median spend per Hampton + Indie Hackers surveys.

### Q13: **B. ~$235/mo**
Apollo $79 + Smartlead $39 + 3 inboxes $18 + SalesNav $99.

### Q14: **B. Apollo at ~1/10 the cost**
Standard solo replacement for enterprise ZoomInfo.

### Q15: **A. ~$6/mo**
Hetzner / Hostinger VPS for n8n self-hosting.

### Q16: **A. PandaDoc or Bonsai with custom AI-use clauses**
Both let you embed custom clauses.

### Q17: **B. Real free tiers (Cloudflare Workers, Clarity, Notion free, ChatGPT free)**
Not ToS-violating shortcuts.

### Q18: **B. ~$280/mo**
Hampton + Indie Hackers anonymized average.

### Q19: **B. Under $100/mo**
Designjoy's leverage is process + brand, not tools.

### Q20: **B. Tool sprawl**
The condition Marcus's stack illustrated.

### Q21: **B. The outbound stack (~$235/mo)**
If you're inbound-only, you don't need cold email infrastructure.

### Q22: **B. FREE heatmap + session-replay tool from Microsoft**
A genuine free Hotjar replacement.

### Q23: **B. Cheap but critical security hygiene**
A YubiKey ($50 once) + 1Password ($3/mo) is the cheapest insurance you can buy.

### Q24: **B. 2+ teammates, admin controls, or $5K+/mo recurring**
Don't upgrade prematurely.

### Q25: **C. Both Ahrefs Standard AND SEMrush Guru simultaneously**
Classic tool sprawl. Pick one.
