## 🏦 The 4-Account Setup (Day 1)

```
1. Business checking   ← all client $ deposits
2. Business savings    ← TAX BUCKET (30% of every deposit)
3. Personal checking   ← owner draws / salary
4. Personal savings    ← long-term wealth
```

🧠 **Move 30% to tax bucket the moment a payment arrives.**

---

## 💰 The 30-30-40 Rule (Adapt to Your Situation)

```
For every $1 that hits business checking:

30% → TAX BUCKET
30% → OPERATING EXPENSES
40% → OWNER PAY
```

S-corp at higher profit: 25 / 25 / 50

🧠 Profit First variant: 5 accounts (Income, Profit, Owner Pay, Tax, OpEx)

## 🏗️ Entity Structures (US)

| Entity | Best for | SE tax savings |
|---|---|---|
| Sole prop | Year 1 / <$50K | None |
| LLC (single member) | Year 1–2 / <$80K | None |
| **LLC + S-corp election** | **$80K–$400K profit** | **Significant** |
| C-corp | Raising VC | N/A — different model |

International equivalents: UK Ltd, German GmbH, French SAS/SARL, Canadian fed corp, Australian Pty Ltd, Spanish SL.

---

## 📆 US Quarterly Estimated Tax Deadlines

```
Q1 (Jan–Mar)  → Apr 15
Q2 (Apr–May)  → Jun 15
Q3 (Jun–Aug)  → Sep 15
Q4 (Sep–Dec)  → Jan 15 (next year)
```

🎯 **Safe harbor:** pay 100% of last year's tax (110% if AGI >$150K).

## 🪙 US Retirement Vehicles for Solo Operators

| Vehicle | Approx limit (2024–2025) |
|---|---|
| Solo 401(k) employee | $23,500 (+$7,500 if 50+) |
| Solo 401(k) employer | Up to 25% SE income or W-2 wages |
| Combined 401(k) max | ~$70K (~$77.5K+ if 50+) |
| SEP-IRA | ~25% SE income, capped ~$69K |
| Roth IRA | $7,000 (income limits) |
| HSA (with HDHP) | $4,150 single / $8,300 family |

🧠 **Triple-tax advantage: HSA only.**

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

When you see these in answers, they're often correct:
- ✅ "Separate personal + business banking..."
- ✅ "Transfer the tax bucket immediately..."
- ✅ "Quarterly estimates required..."
- ✅ "Consult a CPA on entity structure..."
- ✅ "Track gross + operating margin..."

When you see these, they're often **wrong**:
- ❌ "Skip quarterly taxes..."
- ❌ "S-corp with $0 salary..."
- ❌ "Aggressive personal deductions..."
- ❌ "Wait until April..."
- ❌ "Mix personal + business accounts..."

---

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns To Recognize

- ❌ No tax bucket → April surprise
- ❌ No quarterly estimates → IRS penalties
- ❌ Personal + business commingled → can't see real margin
- ❌ Aggressive deductions → audit risk
- ❌ Bookkeeping deferred → year-end chaos
- ❌ No emergency fund → cashflow crisis kills business
