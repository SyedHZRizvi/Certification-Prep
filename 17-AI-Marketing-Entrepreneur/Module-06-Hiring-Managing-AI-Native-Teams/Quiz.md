## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **B. Sustained 50+ hr/wk, specific bottleneck >15 hr/wk, active revenue turned away**
The 3 legitimate triggers. Ego and "looking established" are not.

### Q2: **B. Generate 1.5–2x their cost in freed capacity or new revenue within 90 days**
The standard agency hire-payback rule.

### Q3: **C. $50,000/mo**
Per the opening story: 4 new clients × $5,500 + retained capacity = ~$50K/mo new ARR.

### Q4: **D. Prefers in-person 8 hr/day synchronous work**
AI-native operators are async-first.

### Q5: **B. Refuses to use AI ("I'm a real writer")**
Hard pass.

### Q6: **C. The paid work-sample test**
Work output beats behavioral.

### Q7: **B. 2–3x**
Schmidt & Hunter (1998); replicated by Google, IDEO, Stripe.

### Q8: **A. 3 hours, paid at hourly rate, real task tied to job**
Paid, real-task, time-boxed.

### Q9: **D. Buying email lists from data brokers**
Not a hiring source, and not advisable.

### Q10: **B. An EOR like Deel, Remote.com, or Multiplier**
EORs handle compliance for international FT hires.

### Q11: **B. $48K–$72K**
Per the 2026 comp band table.

### Q12: **B. $65K–$120K**
Per the 2026 comp band table.

### Q13: **D. There is no legal difference**
False. The IRS distinguishes carefully; misclassification creates tax/legal risk.

### Q14: **B. Setup, business context, client tour, tool deep-dive, first small task**
The standard Week 1.

### Q15: **B. On day 1**
30/60/90 milestones should be transparent from day 1.

### Q16: **D. The hire's personal preferences**
SOPs are about the process, not the person.

### Q17: **B. 3–5x output**
The AI-augmented delivery multiplier target.

### Q18: **B. Remain solo by choice**
Williams has publicly stated this on My First Million and Starter Story.

### Q19: **A. Output quality, AI usage, speed, communication, process**
The reading's rubric dimensions.

### Q20: **B. "Would you re-hire them tomorrow?"**
The most telling reference-check question.

### Q21: **B. Adds 15–25% on top of 85%-of-market base**
The standard variable-comp structure.

### Q22: **A. 5–15% of annual net profit, allocated by tenure × performance**
Standard agency profit-share structure.

### Q23: **B. Wrong hire, plan for it**
A documented agency-founder mantra.

### Q24: **B. Pairing them with prompt templates, approved AI workflows, and house-style guides**
The hire produces in your voice from week 2.

### Q25: **C. A $180K senior strategist with no current need**
Hiring senior without need wrecks margins.
