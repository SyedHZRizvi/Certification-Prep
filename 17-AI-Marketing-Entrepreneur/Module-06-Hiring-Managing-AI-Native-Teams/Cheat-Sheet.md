## ⏰ The 3 Hiring Triggers

```
1. 50+ hr/wk sustained for 8+ weeks with overflow demand
2. ONE specific bottleneck task >15 hr/wk that can be delegated
3. 2+ inbound prospects/mo being turned away

If none → you are hiring too early.
```

🧠 **The 1.5x rule:** new hire = 1.5–2x their cost in freed capacity or new revenue within 90 days.

---

## 🧬 The AI-Native Operator Profile

**5 traits to screen for:**
1. Daily AI usage (Claude/ChatGPT 50%+ of work)
2. Loves shipping fast
3. Self-teaches new tools in 3–5 hr
4. Async-first communicator
5. Process-oriented (uses SOPs, then improves)

**3 red flags:**
- ❌ "I work better in person" (rigid)
- ❌ Refuses to use AI
- ❌ Brittle ego / can't take feedback

## 🎯 Work-Sample Interview Funnel

```
1. App screen      (15 min)
2. Intro call       (30 min)
3. PAID WORK SAMPLE (2–4 hr) ← the deciding step
4. Final call       (30 min) + references
```

Rubric (1–10 each):
- Output quality (35%)
- AI usage (20%)
- Speed (15%)
- Communication (15%)
- Process (15%)

Hire on **7.5+ aggregate**.

## 📜 Week 1–4 Onboarding

| Week | Goal |
|---|---|
| 1 | Context: setup, 90-min biz Loom, client tour |
| 2 | Shadowing: pair on real work |
| 3 | Driving: lead one workflow with review |
| 4 | Solo: independent client work, daily 15-min check-ins |

**Share 30/60/90 plan on day 1.**

---

## 📋 Every SOP Has

```
1. Trigger      (when this fires)
2. Inputs       (prereqs)
3. Steps        (numbered)
4. Tools        (links)
5. Output       (definition of done)
6. QA checklist (pre-delivery)
7. Owner
8. Notes / gotchas
```

## 🚫 The 8 Hiring Mistakes

1. Hiring before workload exists
2. Hiring managers who can't ship
3. Hiring people who hate AI
4. Skipping paid work-sample
5. No SOPs ready
6. No 30/60/90 plan
7. Overpaying vs. market
8. Hiring in your image (clones)

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

When you see these in answers, they're often correct:
- ✅ "Hire only when math forces you..."
- ✅ "Paid work-sample test..."
- ✅ "AI-native operator..."
- ✅ "30/60/90 plan from day 1..."
- ✅ "EOR for international hires..."

When you see these, they're often **wrong**:
- ❌ "Hire to look established..."
- ❌ "Behavioral interviews suffice..."
- ❌ "Unpaid 40-hour project..."
- ❌ "Senior hire when no need..."
- ❌ "Misclassify employees as contractors to save tax..."

---

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns To Recognize

- ❌ Hiring before SOPs exist
- ❌ Behavioral-only interviews
- ❌ Unpaid work samples
- ❌ No reference checks
- ❌ Compensating above market without need
- ❌ Hiring clones of yourself
