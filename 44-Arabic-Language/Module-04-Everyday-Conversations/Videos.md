## Immersion Resources

- **Al Jazeera Arabic:** [arabic.aljazeera.net](https://arabic.aljazeera.net) — start with headlines, build up to articles
- **Rotana Music TV:** Watch Arabic music videos with lyrics on screen — excellent for ear training
- **Netflix/Shahid:** Arab streaming platforms with Arabic subtitles — Egyptian dramas are great for dialect exposure
- **Italki / Preply:** Find an Arabic tutor (native speaker) for conversation practice — Gulf, Egyptian, or Levantine
