## Arabic-Urdu Literary Heritage Chain

Arabic poetry forms → Persian poets adapted them → Urdu inherited them all:

- غزل (ghazal): Arabic origin → Persian masters (Rumi, Hafiz) → Urdu (Mir, Ghalib, Faiz)
- قصیدہ (qaṣīda): Arabic panegyric → Persian royal courts → Urdu
- دیوان (dīwān): Arabic collected works format → Urdu دیوان of every major poet
- مثنوی (mathnawī): Arabic form → Persian Masnavi of Rumi → Urdu

**Key fact:** The word algorithm comes from الخُوَارِزْمِي (al-Khwarizmi), the 9th-century Baghdad mathematician. Algebra comes from الجَبْر (al-jabr), the title of his book.
