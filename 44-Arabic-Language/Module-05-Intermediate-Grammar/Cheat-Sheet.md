# Module 5 Cheat Sheet — Intermediate Arabic Grammar

## Past Tense Conjugation (الماضي) — كَتَبَ (to write)

| Person | Arabic | Transliteration |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| He | كَتَبَ | kataba |
| She | كَتَبَتْ | katabat |
| You (m) | كَتَبْتَ | katabta |
| You (f) | كَتَبْتِ | katabti |
| I | كَتَبْتُ | katabtu |
| They (m. pl) | كَتَبُوا | katabū |
| They (f. pl) | كَتَبْنَ | katabna |
| You (m. pl) | كَتَبْتُم | katabtum |
| We | كَتَبْنَا | katabnā |
| They (m. dual) | كَتَبَا | katabā |

## Present Tense Conjugation (المضارع) — يَكْتُبُ

| Person | Arabic | Transliteration |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| He | يَكْتُبُ | yaktubu |
| She | تَكْتُبُ | taktubu |
| You (m) | تَكْتُبُ | taktubu |
| You (f) | تَكْتُبِينَ | taktubīna |
| I | أَكْتُبُ | aktubu |
| They (m. pl) | يَكْتُبُونَ | yaktubūna |
| You (m. pl) | تَكْتُبُونَ | taktubūna |
| We | نَكْتُبُ | naktubu |

**Future:** add سَـ before verb → سَيَكْتُبُ (he will write)
**Stronger future:** سَوْفَ يَكْتُبُ

## Idaafa (الإضافة) — Possession

**Structure:** First noun (loses tanwīn, no الـ) + Second noun (genitive)

| Arabic | English |
|--------|---------|
| كِتَابُ الطَّالِبِ | the student's book |
| بَيْتُ الأُسْتَاذِ | the professor's house |
| بَابُ المَسْجِدِ | the door of the mosque |
| كَلَامُ اللّٰهِ | the speech of God |

**Pronominal suffixes:**

| Suffix | Meaning | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| ـِي | my | كِتَابِي |
| ـُكَ | your (m) | كِتَابُكَ |
| ـُكِ | your (f) | كِتَابُكِ |
| ـُه | his | كِتَابُه |
| ـُهَا | her | كِتَابُهَا |
| ـُنَا | our | كِتَابُنَا |
| ـُكُم | your (pl) | كِتَابُكُم |
| ـُهُم | their | كِتَابُهُم |

| Situation | Adjective form | Example |
|-----------|----------------|---------|

| Masculine singular definite | m. sg. def | الكِتَابُ الجَدِيدُ |
| Feminine singular indefinite | f. sg. indef | سَيَّارَةٌ جَدِيدَةٌ |
| Feminine singular definite | f. sg. def | السَّيَّارَةُ الجَدِيدَةُ |
| Human masculine plural | m. pl | الطُّلَّابُ المُجِدُّونَ |

| NON-HUMAN plural (any gender) | f. sg. | الكُتُبُ الجَدِيدَةُ |

## Negation Summary (النفي)

| Negation | Tense | Mood | Example |
|----------|-------|------|---------|
| لَا | Present | Indicative | لَا يَكْتُبُ (he doesn't write) |
| لَم | Past | Jussive (-apocopate) | لَم يَكْتُبْ (he did not write) |
| لَن | Future | Subjunctive (-manṣūb) | لَن يَكْتُبَ (he will not write) |
| لَيْسَ | "is not" | — | لَيْسَ طَبِيبًا (not a doctor) |

## Arabic Verb Forms I–X (أوزان الفعل)

| Form | Pattern | Semantic function | Example |
|------|---------|-------------------|---------|
| I | فَعَلَ / يَفْعَلُ | Basic | كَتَبَ (write) |
| II | فَعَّلَ / يُفَعِّلُ | Intensify / cause | عَلَّمَ (teach) |
| III | فَاعَلَ / يُفَاعِلُ | Reciprocal | كَاتَبَ (correspond) |
| IV | أَفْعَلَ / يُفْعِلُ | Causative | أَخْرَجَ (produce) |
| V | تَفَعَّلَ / يَتَفَعَّلُ | Reflexive of II | تَعَلَّمَ (learn) |
| VI | تَفَاعَلَ / يَتَفَاعَلُ | Mutual of III | تَكَاتَبَ (exchange letters) |
| VII | اِنْفَعَلَ / يَنْفَعِلُ | Passive / reflexive | اِنْكَسَرَ (be broken) |
| VIII | اِفْتَعَلَ / يَفْتَعِلُ | Reflexive / resultative | اِجْتَمَعَ (gather) |
| IX | اِفْعَلَّ / يَفْعَلُّ | Colours & defects | اِحْمَرَّ (become red) |
| X | اِسْتَفْعَلَ / يَسْتَفْعِلُ | Seek / deem | اِسْتَغْفَرَ (seek forgiveness) |

## Key Conjunctions (حروف الربط)

| Arabic | English | Use |
|--------|---------|-----|
| وَ | and | joins nouns/clauses |
| لَكِن / لَكِنَّ | but | contrast |
| لِأَنَّ | because | reason |
| فَـ / إِذَنْ | so / therefore | result |
| إِذَا | if | conditional |
| أَوْ | or | alternative |
| ثُمَّ | then | sequence |
