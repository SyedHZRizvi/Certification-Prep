## Intermediate Grammar Reference Books

- **Madinah Arabic Books 1–3:** The classical structured approach — all three books available free online
- **Al-Kitaab fii Taʿallum al-ʿArabiyya:** Georgetown University Press — the standard US university Arabic textbook
- **Modern Standard Arabic Grammar** by El-Gamal — excellent analytical reference
- **Hans Wehr Dictionary:** Essential — all entries organised by trilateral root
