## Additional Learning Resources

- **ArabicPod101** — Structured lessons from absolute beginner to advanced: [arabicpod101.com](https://www.arabicpod101.com)
- **Madinah Arabic** — The classic Islamic University of Madinah textbook series, free online
- **Duolingo Arabic** — Good for alphabet drilling and basic vocabulary
- **Quran.com** — Read the Quran with transliteration and word-by-word translation to build vocabulary
