# Module 1 Quiz — أساسيات اللغة العربية (Foundations)

Test your knowledge of the Arabic alphabet, numbers, greetings, and Arabic-Urdu cognates.

---

**1.** How many letters are in the Arabic alphabet?

- A) 26
- B) 28
- C) 30
- D) 32

**Answer: B) 28**

---

**2.** Arabic is written and read in which direction?

- A) Left to right
- B) Top to bottom
- C) Right to left
- D) Bottom to top

**Answer: C) Right to left**

---

**3.** How many positional forms does each Arabic letter have?

- A) 2 (initial and final)
- B) 3 (initial, medial, final)
- C) 4 (isolated, initial, medial, final)
- D) 1 (only one form)

**Answer: C) 4 (isolated, initial, medial, final)**

---

**4.** Which vowel mark is called "fatha" and produces a short "a" sound?

- A) ـِ
- B) ـُ
- C) ـَ
- D) ـْ

**Answer: C) ـَ**

---

**5.** What does the Arabic word كِتَاب (kitāb) mean?

- A) Time
- B) Book
- C) Knowledge
- D) World

**Answer: B) Book**

---

**6.** The Arabic word عِلْم (ʿilm) corresponds to which Urdu word with the same meaning?

- A) وقت
- B) علم
- C) دنیا
- D) کتاب

**Answer: B) علم**

---

**7.** What is the Arabic word for "one" (number 1)?

- A) اِثْنَان
- B) ثَلَاثَة
- C) وَاحِد
- D) عَشَرَة

**Answer: C) وَاحِد**

---

**8.** What is the standard Islamic greeting in Arabic?

- A) مَرْحَبًا
- B) صَبَاحُ الخَيْر
- C) السَّلَامُ عَلَيْكُم
- D) كَيْفَ حَالُك؟

**Answer: C) السَّلَامُ عَلَيْكُم**

---

**9.** How do you say "Thank you" in Arabic?

- A) عَفْوًا
- B) نَعَم
- C) مَعَ السَّلَامَة
- D) شُكْرًا

**Answer: D) شُكْرًا**

---

**10.** What does مَعَ السَّلَامَة (maʿa s-salāma) mean?

- A) Good morning
- B) Please
- C) Goodbye
- D) Welcome

**Answer: C) Goodbye**

---

**11.** Which letter produces the deep pharyngeal sound unique to Arabic (also found in Urdu)?

- A) ف
- B) ع
- C) ب
- D) ل

**Answer: B) ع**

---

**12.** The mark ـّ (shadda) indicates:

- A) A short "a" vowel
- B) The end of a word
- C) A doubled consonant
- D) A question marker

**Answer: C) A doubled consonant**

---

**13.** What is the Arabic number for "ten"?

- A) سَبْعَة
- B) تِسْعَة
- C) عَشَرَة
- D) ثَمَانِيَة

**Answer: C) عَشَرَة**

---

**14.** MSA stands for:

- A) Modern Spoken Arabic
- B) Modern Standard Arabic
- C) Middle Standard Arabic
- D) Moroccan Standard Arabic

**Answer: B) Modern Standard Arabic**

---

**15.** The Arabic word وَقْت (waqt) means "time." Which Urdu word is its cognate?

- A) علم
- B) دنیا
- C) وقت
- D) کتاب

**Answer: C) وقت**

---

**16.** Which of these Arabic letters does NOT connect to the following letter?

- A) ب
- B) ت
- C) د
- D) س

**Answer: C) د** (daal is a non-connecting letter)

---

**17.** How do you say "Good morning" in Arabic?

- A) مَسَاءُ الخَيْر
- B) صَبَاحُ الخَيْر
- C) مَرْحَبًا
- D) إِلَى اللِّقَاء

**Answer: B) صَبَاحُ الخَيْر**

---

**18.** What percentage of Urdu vocabulary is borrowed from Arabic (approximately)?

- A) 10%
- B) 20%
- C) 30%+
- D) 50%+

**Answer: C) 30%+**

---

**19.** The Arabic word اِمْتِحَان (imtiḥān) means:

- A) Lesson
- B) Teacher
- C) School
- D) Exam

**Answer: D) Exam**

---

**20.** Which dialect family is most widely understood across the Arab world due to media influence?

- A) Gulf (خليجي)
- B) Egyptian (مصري)
- C) Levantine (شامي)
- D) Maghrebi (مغربي)

**Answer: B) Egyptian (مصري)**

---

**21.** What is the Arabic word for "five"?

- A) أَرْبَعَة
- B) سِتَّة
- C) خَمْسَة
- D) سَبْعَة

**Answer: C) خَمْسَة**

---

**22.** The vowel mark kasra (ـِ) produces which sound?

- A) Short "a"
- B) Short "u"
- C) Short "i"
- D) No vowel

**Answer: C) Short "i"**

---

**23.** Which Arabic word means "please" (when addressing a male)?

- A) شُكْرًا
- B) عَفْوًا
- C) مِنْ فَضْلِك
- D) أَهْلًا

**Answer: C) مِنْ فَضْلِك**

---

**24.** The Arabic and Urdu word for "journey / travel" (سَفَر / سفر) is an example of:

- A) A false cognate
- B) An Arabic-Urdu cognate (shared word)
- C) A Persian loanword in both languages
- D) A modern invention

**Answer: B) An Arabic-Urdu cognate (shared word)**

---

**25.** What is the Arabic word for "twenty"?

- A) عَشَرَة
- B) اِثْنَا عَشَر
- C) ثَمَانِيَة عَشَر
- D) عِشْرُون

**Answer: D) عِشْرُون**

---

**26.** Which statement about Modern Standard Arabic (MSA) is correct?

- A) It is only spoken in Egypt
- B) It is based on a mix of dialects
- C) It is used in formal writing, news media, and education across the Arab world
- D) It is the same as Quranic Arabic with no differences

**Answer: C) It is used in formal writing, news media, and education across the Arab world**
