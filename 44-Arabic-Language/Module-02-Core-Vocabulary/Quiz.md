# Module 2 Quiz — المفردات الأساسية (Core Vocabulary)

Test your knowledge of Arabic vocabulary across family, body, food, home, verbs, adjectives, and time.

---

**1.** What is the Arabic word for "mother"?

- A) أَب
- B) أُخْت
- C) أُمّ
- D) جَدَّة

**Answer: C) أُمّ**

---

**2.** Which Arabic word means "grandfather"?

- A) عَمّ
- B) جَدّ
- C) خَال
- D) اِبْن

**Answer: B) جَدّ**

---

**3.** The Arabic word for "maternal aunt" (خَالَة) is a cognate of which Urdu word?

- A) پھوپھی
- B) بہن
- C) خالہ
- D) نانی

**Answer: C) خالہ**

---

**4.** Which Arabic word means "heart" and is also an Urdu cognate?

- A) رَأْس
- B) يَد
- C) جِلْد
- D) قَلْب

**Answer: D) قَلْب**

---

**5.** What does جِسْم (jism) mean?

- A) Face
- B) Body
- C) Skin
- D) Bone

**Answer: B) Body**

---

**6.** The Arabic word for "coffee" is:

- A) شَاي
- B) مَاء
- C) قَهْوَة
- D) حَلِيب

**Answer: C) قَهْوَة**

---

**7.** What is the Arabic word for "chicken"?

- A) لَحْم
- B) سَمَك
- C) دَجَاج
- D) بَيْض

**Answer: C) دَجَاج**

---

**8.** The popular Arabic dish كَبْسَة (kabsa) is associated with which country?

- A) Egypt
- B) Jordan
- C) Morocco
- D) Saudi Arabia

**Answer: D) Saudi Arabia**

---

**9.** What does مَطْعَم (maṭʿam) mean?

- A) Kitchen
- B) Restaurant
- C) Market
- D) Food

**Answer: B) Restaurant**

---

**10.** The Arabic word مَكْتَب (maktab) means:

- A) Bedroom
- B) Kitchen
- C) Desk / office
- D) Garden

**Answer: C) Desk / office**

---

**11.** What is the Arabic word for "door"? (Also an Urdu cognate for "chapter")

- A) نَافِذَة
- B) سِلَّم
- C) سَقْف
- D) بَاب

**Answer: D) بَاب**

---

**12.** Which Arabic verb means "to understand"?

- A) عَرَفَ
- B) فَهِمَ
- C) دَرَسَ
- D) سَمِعَ

**Answer: B) فَهِمَ**

---

**13.** What does ذَهَبَ (dhahaba) mean?

- A) To come
- B) To sit
- C) To go
- D) To run

**Answer: C) To go**

---

**14.** The Arabic verb سَافَرَ (sāfara) means:

- A) To buy
- B) To travel
- C) To return
- D) To forget

**Answer: B) To travel**

---

**15.** Which adjective pair correctly shows masculine / feminine for "big"?

- A) كَبِير / كَبِيرَة
- B) جَدِيد / جَدِيدَة
- C) صَغِير / صَغِيرَة
- D) حَارّ / حَارَّة

**Answer: A) كَبِير / كَبِيرَة**

---

**16.** What is the opposite of طَوِيل (tall/long) in Arabic?

- A) صَغِير
- B) قَدِيم
- C) قَصِير
- D) بَطِيء

**Answer: C) قَصِير**

---

**17.** The Arabic word فَقِير (faqīr) means "poor" and is cognate with which Urdu word?

- A) امیر
- B) غریب
- C) فقیر
- D) بڑا

**Answer: C) فقیر**

---

**18.** What does الآن (al-ān) mean?

- A) Yesterday
- B) Tomorrow
- C) Now
- D) Soon

**Answer: C) Now**

---

**19.** What is the Arabic word for "Friday"?

- A) الخَمِيس
- B) الأَحَد
- C) الجُمُعَة
- D) السَّبْت

**Answer: C) الجُمُعَة**

---

**20.** Which time expression means "always"?

- A) أَبَدًا
- B) دَائِمًا
- C) أَحْيَانًا
- D) قَرِيبًا

**Answer: B) دَائِمًا**

---

**21.** The Arabic word for "eye" is:

- A) أُذُن
- B) أَنْف
- C) عَيْن
- D) لِسَان

**Answer: C) عَيْن**

---

**22.** What does أَحَبَّ (aḥabba) mean?

- A) To hate
- B) To forget
- C) To want
- D) To love

**Answer: D) To love**

---

**23.** The Arabic dish فَلَافِل (falāfil) is made from:

- A) Rice and lamb
- B) Fried chickpeas
- C) Semolina and honey
- D) Grilled fish

**Answer: B) Fried chickpeas**

---

**24.** What is the Arabic word for "Thursday"?

- A) الاِثْنَيْن
- B) الثُّلَاثَاء
- C) الأَرْبِعَاء
- D) الخَمِيس

**Answer: D) الخَمِيس**

---

**25.** Which Arabic verb means "to buy"?

- A) بَاعَ
- B) اِشْتَرَى
- C) أَخَذَ
- D) أَعْطَى

**Answer: B) اِشْتَرَى**

---

**26.** The Arabic word حَمَّام (ḥammām) — bathroom — is borrowed into Urdu as:

- A) گھر
- B) کمرہ
- C) حمام
- D) باورچی خانہ

**Answer: C) حمام**
