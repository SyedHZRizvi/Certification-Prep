## Vocabulary Building Tips

- **Anki:** Use the free Anki flashcard app with an Arabic deck — spaced repetition is the most efficient method for vocabulary
- **Labelling:** Stick Arabic labels on objects around your home (باب on the door, طاولة on the table)
- **Quran vocabulary:** The Quran uses approximately 2,000 unique root words — learning 500 roots covers 80%+ of the text
