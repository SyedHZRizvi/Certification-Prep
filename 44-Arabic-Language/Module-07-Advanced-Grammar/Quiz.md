# Module 7 Quiz — Advanced Grammar

Test your knowledge of the dual, verb-subject agreement, verb forms II–X, hollow verbs, defective verbs, and kāna constructions.

---

**1.** What is the nominative dual form of كِتَاب (book)?

- A) كِتَابَيْنِ
- B) كِتَابَانِ
- C) كِتَابَاتٌ
- D) كُتُبَانِ

---
