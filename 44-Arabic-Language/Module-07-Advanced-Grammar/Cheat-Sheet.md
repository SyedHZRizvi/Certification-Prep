# Module 7 Cheat Sheet — Advanced Grammar (القواعد المتقدمة)

## The Dual (المثنى)

| Case | Masculine | Feminine |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Nominative | ـَانِ (-āni): كِتَابَانِ | ـتَانِ (-tāni): مَدِينَتَانِ |
| Acc./Gen. | ـَيْنِ (-ayni): كِتَابَيْنِ | ـتَيْنِ (-tayni): مَدِينَتَيْنِ |
| In iḍāfa | Drop نِ: كِتَابَا الطَّالِبِ | Drop نِ: مَدِينَتَا مِصْر |

**Dual verb/pronoun:** هُمَا (they two) | كَتَبَا (they two wrote) | يَكْتُبَانِ (they two write)

## Verb-Subject Agreement

| Word Order | Rule | Example |
|------------|------|---------|
| Verb FIRST | Verb = singular | ذَهَبَ الطُّلَّابُ |
| Subject FIRST | Verb agrees fully | الطُّلَّابُ ذَهَبُوا |

## Verb Forms II–X Summary

| Form | Pattern | Function | Example |
|------|---------|----------|---------|
| II | فَعَّلَ / يُفَعِّلُ | Intensify / cause | عَلَّمَ (teach) |
| III | فَاعَلَ / يُفَاعِلُ | Reciprocal | سَاعَدَ (help) |
| IV | أَفْعَلَ / يُفْعِلُ | Causative | أَرْسَلَ (send) |
| V | تَفَعَّلَ / يَتَفَعَّلُ | Reflexive of II | تَعَلَّمَ (learn) |
| VI | تَفَاعَلَ / يَتَفَاعَلُ | Mutual of III | تَكَاتَبَ (exchange letters) |
| VII | اِنْفَعَلَ / يَنْفَعِلُ | Passive/reflexive | اِنْكَسَرَ (be broken) |
| VIII | اِفْتَعَلَ / يَفْتَعِلُ | Reflexive/resultative | اِجْتَمَعَ (convene) |
| X | اِسْتَفْعَلَ / يَسْتَفْعِلُ | Seek / request | اِسْتَغْفَرَ (seek forgiveness) |

## Hollow Verbs (الأفعال الجوفاء)

| Person | قَالَ (to say) | بَاعَ (to sell) |
|--------|--------------|--------------|
| He (past) | قَالَ | بَاعَ |
| I (past) | قُلْتُ | بِعْتُ |
| He (present) | يَقُولُ | يَبِيعُ |
| I (present) | أَقُولُ | أَبِيعُ |

**Common hollow verbs:** كَانَ (was), قَالَ (said), نَامَ (slept), زَارَ (visited), طَارَ (flew), فَاتَ (passed)

---

## Defective Verbs (الأفعال الناقصة)

| Person | مَشَى (to walk) | دَعَا (to call) |
|--------|----------------|----------------|
| He (past) | مَشَى | دَعَا |
| I (past) | مَشَيْتُ | دَعَوْتُ |
| He (present) | يَمْشِي | يَدْعُو |
| I (present) | أَمْشِي | أَدْعُو |

**Common defective verbs:** بَكَى (cried), رَجَا (hoped), نَسِيَ (forgot), لَقِيَ (met)

## كَانَ and Its Sisters

Predicate (خَبَر) takes **accusative case**:

| Verb | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| كَانَ | was | كَانَ طَالِبًا — He was a student |
| أَصْبَحَ | became (morning) | أَصْبَحَ مُدِيرًا |
| صَارَ | became | صَارَ كَبِيرًا |
| ظَلَّ | remained | ظَلَّ صَامِتًا |
| مَا زَالَ | still is | مَا زَالَ يَعْمَلُ |
| بَاتَ | spent the night (in a state) | بَاتَ حَزِينًا |

## Form Derivation Chains (Root ع-ل-م)

| Form | Verb | Meaning |
|------|------|---------|
| I | عَلِمَ | to know |
| II | عَلَّمَ | to teach (cause to know) |
| IV | أَعْلَمَ | to inform |
| V | تَعَلَّمَ | to learn (for oneself) |
| X | اِسْتَعْلَمَ | to enquire / seek information |

---

## Memory Tips for Urdu Speakers

- **تَعَلَّمَ (to learn)** — Urdu تعلیم (taʿlīm — education) and معلم (muʿallim — teacher) come from the same root ع-ل-م
- **اِسْتِغْفَار (seeking forgiveness)** — used in Urdu/Persian also as استغفار
- **Hollow verb pattern:** When the middle letter is weak, it collapses in conjugation — just like Urdu/Persian often drops weak letters in verbs
- **Form VIII marker: ت** — watch for the inserted ت: اِجْتَمَعَ (j-t-m-ʿ from ج-م-ع)
