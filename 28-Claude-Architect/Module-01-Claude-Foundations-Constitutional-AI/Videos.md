## 🏆 Best channels to subscribe to

| Channel | Why |
|---------|-----|
| **Anthropic (official)** | First-party tutorials, model launches, fireside chats with the founders. The authoritative source. |
| **Andrej Karpathy** | Deepest free LLM education on the internet. "Intro to LLMs" and "Zero to Hero" are required watching. |
| **AI Explained** | Honest, no-hype model comparisons. Calls out marketing nonsense. |
| **Dwarkesh Patel** | Long-form interviews with frontier-lab founders including Dario Amodei. |
| **Lex Fridman** | Long-form interviews; Dario Amodei episode is required listening for this module. |
| **DeepLearning.AI** | Andrew Ng's structured short courses; some are co-built with Anthropic. |
| **AI Coffee Break with Letitia** | German-Romanian researcher; phenomenal paper walkthroughs including the Bai et al. paper. |
| **Hard Fork (NYT podcast)** | Kevin Roose & Casey Newton; weekly AI news with sane analysis. Often features Anthropic news. |
