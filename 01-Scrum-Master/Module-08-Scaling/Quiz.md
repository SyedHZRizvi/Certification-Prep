# ✏️ Module 8 Quiz: Scaling Scrum

## Questions

### Q1. Five Scrum Teams work on the same product. How many Product Owners?
A. 1
B. 5
C. As many as needed
D. 1 per region

### Q2. Five Scrum Teams work on the same product. How many Product Backlogs?
A. 1
B. 5
C. 1 plus team-level backlogs
D. Up to the org

### Q3. Per the Scrum Guide, when a team is too large, the right move is:
A. Add hierarchical management
B. Split into multiple Scrum Teams sharing same Product Goal/Backlog/PO
C. Make the team a "super team"
D. Keep Scrum but extend the Sprint

### Q4. Nexus is best used for:
A. 1 team
B. 3-9 Scrum Teams on the same product
C. 50+ teams
D. Hardware projects only

### Q5. Feature teams differ from component teams in that they:
A. Are smaller
B. Own one tech layer
C. Are cross-functional and deliver end-to-end value
D. Are self-managing while component teams are not

### Q6. SAFe is:
A. Part of the official Scrum Guide
B. A scaling framework, not part of the Scrum Guide
C. A type of Scrum Master certification
D. The Scrum.org scaling framework

### Q7. Multiple Scrum Teams on the same product share which DoD?
A. Each team has its own DoD
B. A single, common Definition of Done
C. The PO sets each team's DoD
D. DoD doesn't apply at scale

### Q8. The "Nexus Integration Team" is responsible for:
A. Hiring developers
B. Coordinating dependencies and ensuring an integrated Increment
C. Managing the budget
D. Replacing the Scrum Master

### Q9. Component teams are an anti-pattern because:
A. They're too small
B. They create dependencies and bottlenecks
C. They're too expensive
D. They violate the Scrum Guide

### Q10. Scrum of Scrums is:
A. A formal Scrum event
B. A common cross-team coordination practice (NOT in Scrum Guide)
C. A type of Sprint
D. A Scrum.org certification

---

## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **A. 1**
ONE PO per product, even when scaled.

### Q2: **A. 1**
ONE Product Backlog. ONE source of truth.

### Q3: **B. Split into multiple teams sharing PG/PB/PO**
Direct from Scrum Guide.

### Q4: **B. 3-9 Scrum Teams**
Nexus's sweet spot.

### Q5: **C. Cross-functional, end-to-end**
Feature teams own complete features.

### Q6: **B. Scaling framework, not in Scrum Guide**
SAFe is its own thing.

### Q7: **B. Single common DoD**
Quality must be consistent across teams on same product.

### Q8: **B. Coordinating dependencies, integrated Increment**
The integration team's purpose.

### Q9: **B. Create dependencies and bottlenecks**
Hand-offs slow delivery.

### Q10: **B. Common practice, NOT in Scrum Guide**
SoS is XP/Scrum tradition, not formally part of Scrum.

---

## 📊 Score: 9-10 → 🏆 / 7-8 → ✅ / <7 → 🔁

## 🃏 Anki

- "Same product = 1 PO, 1 PB, 1 PG, 1 DoD"
- Nexus sweet spot: 3-9 teams
- Feature teams > component teams
- SAFe NOT in Scrum Guide
