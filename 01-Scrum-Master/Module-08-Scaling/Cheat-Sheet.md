# 📋 Module 8 Cheat Sheet: Scaling Scrum

---

## 🏗️ Multiple Teams, ONE Product = SHARE

| What | Count |
|------|-------|
| Product Owner | **1** |
| Product Backlog | **1** |
| Product Goal | **1** |
| Definition of Done | **1 (shared)** |
| Sprint cadence | **Aligned** |

---

## 🎯 Frameworks At A Glance

| Framework | Size | Source | In Scrum Guide? |
|-----------|------|--------|-----------------|
| **Nexus** | 3-9 teams | Scrum.org | Compatible |
| **SAFe** | 50+ teams | Scaled Agile Inc. | ❌ |
| **LeSS** | 8+ teams | Larman/Vodde | Compatible |
| **Scrum of Scrums** | Any | Tradition | ❌ |

---

## 🆕 What Nexus Adds

- 🆕 **Nexus Integration Team** (handles dependencies)
- 🆕 **Nexus Sprint Goal**
- 🆕 **Nexus Sprint Backlog**
- 🆕 **Nexus Daily Scrum** (representatives)
- 🆕 **Nexus Sprint Planning, Review, Retro**

---

## ✅ Feature vs Component Teams

| Feature Team ✅ | Component Team ❌ |
|------------------|-------------------|
| Cross-functional | One tech layer |
| End-to-end value | Hand-offs needed |
| Self-sufficient | Bottlenecks |

---

## 🚫 Scaling Anti-Patterns

- ❌ Multiple POs same product
- ❌ Different DoDs per team
- ❌ Out-of-sync Sprints
- ❌ One mega-team of 30+
- ❌ Component teams
