## 🎯 The 4 Values (MEMORIZE)

```
1. Individuals & Interactions  >  Processes & Tools
2. Working Software            >  Comprehensive Documentation
3. Customer Collaboration      >  Contract Negotiation
4. Responding to Change        >  Following a Plan
```
🧠 Memory: **"I Walk Calmly Right"** (Individuals, Working, Collaboration, Respond)

---

## 🔬 The 3 Pillars of Empiricism

```
   TRANSPARENCY
        ↓
    INSPECTION
        ↓
    ADAPTATION
        ↺ (loops back)
```

| Pillar | Meaning | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Transparency | Everyone sees the same truth | Visible burndown chart |
| Inspection | Regularly check work/process | Sprint Review demo |
| Adaptation | Change based on what you saw | Adjust next sprint |

---

## 📜 5 Critical Agile Principles

1. ⚡ **Continuous delivery** of valuable software
2. 🔄 **Welcome change**, even late
3. 📅 **Frequent delivery** (weeks, not months)
4. 🙋 **Build around motivated individuals**, give support, trust them
5. 🪞 **Reflect & adapt** at regular intervals (= Retrospective)

---

## 🆚 Agile vs Waterfall (Quick Reference)

| | Waterfall | Agile |
|---|-----------|-------|
| Plan | Big upfront | Continuous |
| Delivery | One big release | Many small releases |
| Change | Resisted | Welcomed |
| Customer | Beginning + end | Continuous |
| Best for | Known, stable | Uncertain, evolving |

---

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns To Recognize

- ❌ **ScrumBut** — "We do Scrum, BUT we skip retros"
- ❌ **Big upfront design** — Trying to plan everything before coding
- ❌ **Manager-driven team** — Scrum Master assigning work
- ❌ **No customer feedback** — Building in a vacuum
- ❌ **Status-meeting Daily Scrum** — Daily for the manager, not the team

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

When you see these in answers, they're often correct:
- ✅ "Coach the team..."
- ✅ "Facilitate a discussion..."
- ✅ "Inspect and adapt..."
- ✅ "Make work transparent..."
- ✅ "Empower the team..."

When you see these, they're often **wrong**:
- ❌ "The Scrum Master assigns..."
- ❌ "Stick to the original plan..."
- ❌ "Skip the [event] this time..."
- ❌ "The Project Manager decides..."
- ❌ "Document everything before..."
