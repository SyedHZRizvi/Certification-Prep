# 🏃 Scrum Master Track (PSM I)

> **Goal:** Pass the Professional Scrum Master I exam from Scrum.org with 85%+
> **Duration:** 4 weeks part-time
> **Cost:** $200 USD (lifetime cert, no renewal)

---

## 🎯 What You'll Learn

By the end of this track, you'll be able to:
- Explain why Agile/Scrum exists and why it works
- Recite the Scrum framework cold (3 roles, 5 events, 3 artifacts)
- Coach a team through real Scrum scenarios
- Spot anti-patterns ("ScrumBut", "Zombie Scrum")
- Pass the PSM I with confidence

## ⚠️ The 7 Most Common Reasons People Fail

1. ❌ Didn't read the Scrum Guide enough times (read it 3+!)
2. ❌ Studied "best practices" instead of the framework as written
3. ❌ Confused old Scrum Guide (pre-2020) with the current one (2020 update)
4. ❌ Treated Scrum Master as a "team manager" (they're NOT)
5. ❌ Memorized facts but couldn't apply to scenarios
6. ❌ Didn't take the free Open Assessments multiple times
7. ❌ Time pressure — practiced under leisurely conditions, not 45-sec/question pace
