# 📋 Module 5 Cheat Sheet: Artifacts & Commitments

---

## 📦 The Mapping (MEMORIZE!)

| Artifact | Commitment |
|----------|------------|
| **Product Backlog** | **Product Goal** |
| **Sprint Backlog** | **Sprint Goal** |
| **Increment** | **Definition of Done (DoD)** |

🧠 *"Pretty Pumpkins Sit Sweetly In Dough"* — silly mnemonic if you need one.

---

## 📋 Product Backlog
- ✅ Emergent (always evolving)
- ✅ Ordered by PO
- ✅ Single source of truth
- ✅ Contains: features, bugs, spikes, tech debt
- ✅ Refinement is ONGOING (~10% capacity)
- 🎯 Hosts the Product Goal

## 📋 Sprint Backlog
- ✅ **Owned by Developers** (NOT PO!)
- ✅ Three parts: **Goal + PBIs + Plan**
- ✅ Updated daily
- ✅ Plan is for delivering the Increment
- 🎯 Hosts the Sprint Goal

## 📦 Increment
- ✅ Working, usable product
- ✅ Multiple per Sprint allowed (2020)
- ✅ Must meet DoD
- ✅ Releasable anytime (PO decides)
- 🎯 Bound by Definition of Done

---

## 🎯 The 3 Commitments

### Product Goal
- Long-term target, ONE per product
- Lives in Product Backlog
- PO accountable

### Sprint Goal
- Single objective for THIS Sprint
- Created in Sprint Planning collaboratively
- Provides flex (scope flexes, goal stays)

### Definition of Done
- Quality checklist for ALL Increments
- Created by Developers (or org)
- Applies to every PBI
- Can evolve (toward higher quality)

---

## 🆚 DoD vs Acceptance Criteria

| Definition of Done | Acceptance Criteria |
|--------------------|---------------------|
| Applies to ALL items | Per item |
| Quality bar | Functional bar |
| Mostly stable | Per-item |
| Devs/org set | PO sets |

---

## 🚫 Anti-Pattern Traps

- ❌ Multiple Product Backlogs
- ❌ PO updating Sprint Backlog directly
- ❌ Different DoDs per developer
- ❌ Skipping DoD "just this once"
- ❌ Static, unchanging backlog
