# 📋 Module 7 Cheat Sheet: Servant Leadership

---

## 🛡️ The Mantra

> "Scrum Master = **true leader who serves** the team, the PO, and the organization."

---

## 🎭 The 5 Stances

1. 🛡️ **Servant Leader** — default, removes impediments
2. 🎓 **Coach** — questions, not answers
3. 🛠️ **Facilitator** — runs effective events
4. 🧩 **Teacher** — explains Scrum & practices
5. 🚧 **Change Agent** — org-level barriers

---

## 🆚 Mode Comparison

| Mode | Primary Tool | When |
|------|--------------|------|
| Teaching | Direct instruction | New skill needed |
| Mentoring | Sharing experience | Career growth |
| Coaching | Questions | Performance, insight |
| Facilitating | Designing meetings | Group conversations |
| Consulting | Advice | Specific question |

---

## 🚧 Impediment Levels

1. **Within Team** — direct coaching
2. **Team ↔ Org** — work with mgrs/peers
3. **Org-wide** — change agent role

---

## 🎯 Tell vs Coach

**Tell when:**
- Scrum Guide rule (timebox, attendees)
- Safety/compliance
- Emergency

**Coach when:**
- "How" questions team should solve
- Team dynamics
- Practice improvements

---

## 🚫 Servant Leadership is NOT

- ❌ Doormat
- ❌ Cheerleader (toxic positivity)
- ❌ PM with new title
- ❌ Status secretary

---

## 🎯 Power Phrases For Exam

✅ Right answers often contain:
- "Coach the team..."
- "Facilitate a discussion..."
- "Educate the manager..."
- "Help the team self-organize..."

❌ Wrong answers often contain:
- "Tell the team..."
- "Assign the work..."
- "Decide for the team..."
- "Provide names..."
