# 📋 Module 3 Cheat Sheet: The Roles

---

## 🎭 The 3 Accountabilities

### 👤 Product Owner — "What & Why"
- ✅ Maximizes value
- ✅ Manages Product Backlog
- ✅ Develops Product Goal
- ✅ ONE person, not a committee
- ❌ Doesn't tell devs HOW
- ❌ Doesn't estimate

### 🛡️ Scrum Master — "How It's Working"
Serves THREE groups:
1. **Scrum Team** — coaches, removes impediments
2. **Product Owner** — backlog techniques
3. **Organization** — Scrum adoption

- ❌ NOT a manager
- ❌ NOT a project manager
- ❌ NO authority to assign work
- ✅ Servant leader / true leader who serves

### 🛠️ Developers — "How"
- ✅ Anyone doing the work (coders, testers, designers, etc.)
- ✅ Create Sprint plan
- ✅ Estimate work
- ✅ Self-managing
- ✅ Cross-functional (collectively)

---

## 🚫 What's NOT Allowed

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Multiple POs? | ❌ NO |
| PO + SM same person? | ❌ NO |
| Project Manager? | ❌ Doesn't exist in Scrum |
| Scrum Master assigns work? | ❌ NO |
| Sub-teams within the team? | ❌ NO |

---

## ✅ What IS Allowed

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| One SM for multiple teams? | ✅ Yes |
| PO + Developer same person? | ✅ Technically yes |
| SM + Developer same person? | ✅ Yes (common in small teams) |
| Stakeholders at Sprint Review? | ✅ Yes |

---

## 🆚 Scrum Master vs Project Manager

| SM | PM |
|----|----|
| Serves | Leads |
| Coaches | Directs |
| Removes impediments | Manages tasks |
| No assignment authority | Assigns work |
| Process owner | Project owner |
| Embraces change | Controls change |

🚨 In Scrum: NO PM. NO TEAM LEAD. NO HIERARCHY.

---

## 🎯 Exam Power Phrases

✅ Right answers often contain:
- "The Product Owner decides..."
- "The Developers self-organize..."
- "The Scrum Master coaches..."
- "Refer the request to the Product Owner..."

❌ Wrong answers often contain:
- "The PM assigns..."
- "The team lead decides..."
- "Stakeholders prioritize..."
- "The Scrum Master tells the team..."
