# 📋 Module 4 Cheat Sheet: The 5 Events

---

## ⏱️ The Magic Numbers: 8-15-4-3 (for 4-week Sprint)

| Event | Timebox (4-wk) | Timebox (2-wk) | Timebox (1-wk) |
|-------|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| Sprint | 4 weeks | 2 weeks | 1 week |
| Sprint Planning | **8 hr** | 4 hr | 2 hr |
| Daily Scrum | **15 min** | 15 min | 15 min |
| Sprint Review | **4 hr** | 2 hr | 1 hr |
| Sprint Retrospective | **3 hr** | 1.5 hr | 45 min |

---

## 🎯 Sprint Planning, 3 Topics

1. **WHY** is this Sprint valuable? → Sprint Goal
2. **WHAT** can be done? → PBIs selected
3. **HOW** will work get done? → Plan / tasks

**Output:** Sprint Backlog (Goal + PBIs + plan)

---

## 🤝 Daily Scrum

- 🕒 15 min, daily, same time/place
- 👥 Developers (PO/SM optional unless they're devs too)
- 🎯 Inspect Sprint progress, adapt Sprint Backlog, plan next 24 hrs
- 🆕 Format **NOT** prescribed since 2020 Guide

---

## 🎁 Sprint Review

- 🎯 Inspect **product** & adapt **backlog**
- 👥 Scrum Team + **STAKEHOLDERS** (the only event!)
- 🚫 NOT just a demo, collaborative working session
- 🚫 NOT a sign-off / acceptance gate

---

## 🪞 Sprint Retrospective

- 🎯 Inspect **process** (people, interactions, tools, DoD)
- 👥 Scrum Team only, NO stakeholders
- 📋 Output: at least one improvement to act on

---

## 🚫 NOT Scrum Events (Trap Answers)

- ❌ Backlog Refinement (it's an *activity*)
- ❌ Sprint Zero
- ❌ Demo (separate from Review)
- ❌ Pre-Planning
- ❌ Scrum of Scrums (scaling-only)
- ❌ Stakeholder Check-in

---

## 🎭 Critical Distinctions

| | Review | Retro |
|---|--------|-------|
| Subject | Product | Process |
| Audience | + stakeholders | Team only |
| Length (4-wk) | 4 hr | 3 hr |
| Order | First | Second |

---

## 🚦 Sprint Rules

- ✅ Quality NEVER decreases
- ✅ Scope can be clarified with PO
- ❌ Sprint Goal can't be endangered
- ❌ Sprints not extended
- ⚠️ Only PO can cancel (rare)
- 📈 Each Sprint = 1+ usable Increment
