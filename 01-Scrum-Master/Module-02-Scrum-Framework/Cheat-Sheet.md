## 🎯 Scrum In One Line

> *"A lightweight framework for delivering value via empiricism + lean thinking, on complex problems."*

---

## 🏗️ The Framework

### **1 TEAM** (10 or fewer people)
- 1 Product Owner
- 1 Scrum Master
- Developers (the rest)

### **5 EVENTS**
1. **Sprint** (the container, ≤1 month)
2. **Sprint Planning** (≤8 hrs for 4-wk sprint)
3. **Daily Scrum** (15 min/day, same time/place)
4. **Sprint Review** (≤4 hrs for 4-wk sprint)
5. **Sprint Retrospective** (≤3 hrs for 4-wk sprint)

### **3 ARTIFACTS** → **3 COMMITMENTS**
| Artifact | Commitment |
|----------|------------|
| Product Backlog | **Product Goal** |
| Sprint Backlog | **Sprint Goal** |
| Increment | **Definition of Done** |

---

## 💎 The 5 Scrum Values: **FCCRO**
- **F**ocus
- **C**ommitment
- **C**ourage
- **R**espect
- **O**penness

🧠 *"Frogs Can Cook Real Onions"*

---

## 🚦 Sprint Rules (MEMORIZE)

- ✅ Timeboxed: ≤1 month
- ✅ Same length consistently
- ✅ Quality does NOT decrease
- ✅ Scope clarified with PO as needed
- ❌ Sprints are NOT extended
- ❌ Sprint Goal not endangered by changes
- ⚠️ Only PO can cancel (rare)

---

## 🚫 Scrum Does NOT Prescribe

- ❌ Estimation method
- ❌ Specific tools
- ❌ "Done" criteria (team defines)
- ❌ Story format
- ❌ Programming practices
- ❌ Documentation level

> "Scrum is a framework, not a methodology."

---

## 🆕 2020 Scrum Guide Changes

| Old | New |
|-----|-----|
| Development Team | Developers |
| 3 Roles | 3 Accountabilities |
| 3 Daily Scrum questions | No prescribed format |
| Empiricism only | Empiricism + Lean Thinking |
| Servant Leader | True Leader Who Serves |

---

## 🎯 Exam Power Phrases

✅ Often correct:
- "The team self-organizes"
- "Scope is negotiated with the Product Owner"
- "Inspect and adapt"
- "The Increment must meet the Definition of Done"
- "Only the PO can cancel"

❌ Often wrong:
- "Extend the Sprint"
- "Skip the [event]"
- "Lower quality to meet deadline"
- "Scrum Master assigns tasks"
- "PM decides priorities"
