# 🎥 Module 2 Videos: The Scrum Framework

> Watch in order. Take notes. After all videos, you should be able to draw the Scrum framework from memory.

---

## 🎯 ESSENTIAL VIEWING (~50 minutes)

### 1. "Scrum in Under 10 Minutes" — Hamid Mirza
- ⏱️ 9 minutes
- 🔗 Search YouTube: **"Scrum in Under 10 Minutes Hamid"**
- 🎯 Quick big-picture view

### 2. "Scrum Framework Explained" — Scrum.org (OFFICIAL)
- ⏱️ 12 minutes
- 🔗 https://www.youtube.com/c/Scrumorg → search "Scrum Framework"
- 🎯 The most accurate, exam-aligned version

### 3. "Scrum Guide 2020 Updates" — Scrum.org
- ⏱️ 15 minutes
- 🔗 Search YouTube: **"Scrum Guide 2020 Updates Scrum.org"**
- 🎯 ⭐ Critical — what changed from old to new guide. EXAM ESSENTIAL.

### 4. "Scrum Framework Walkthrough" — Mountain Goat Software
- ⏱️ 14 minutes
- 🔗 Search YouTube: **"Mountain Goat Software Scrum Walkthrough"**
- 🎯 Mike Cohn's tour through every piece

---

## 📚 RECOMMENDED (~40 min additional)

### 5. "5 Scrum Values" — Scrum.org
- ⏱️ 8 minutes
- 🔗 Search: **"5 Scrum Values Scrum.org"**
- 🎯 Each value, one-by-one with examples

### 6. "Scrum is NOT a Methodology" — Geoff Watts
- ⏱️ 11 minutes
- 🔗 Search YouTube: **"Geoff Watts Scrum methodology"**
- 🎯 Critical mindset shift — framework vs methodology

### 7. "The Sprint - The Heart of Scrum" — Lucas Caicoya
- ⏱️ 10 minutes
- 🔗 Search YouTube: **"Sprint Heart of Scrum"**
- 🎯 Why Sprints are timeboxed, what makes them work

### 8. "Empiricism vs Defined Process Control" — Ken Schwaber (Scrum co-creator)
- ⏱️ 12 minutes
- 🔗 Search YouTube: **"Ken Schwaber Empiricism"**
- 🎯 From the man himself — original thinking
