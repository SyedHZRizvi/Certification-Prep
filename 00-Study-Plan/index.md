# 📅 Study Plan

> Three guides that structure your prep from kickoff to exam day. Read them in order — or jump to whatever you need most right now.

---

## 🚀 [Quick Start Guide](./Quick-Start-Guide/)

**Read first.** A 10-minute intro that helps you decide which path to take, sets up your tools (Anki, timer, notebook), and gets you doing your first module in under an hour.

**Best for:** anyone just starting, or anyone who's been stuck in "research mode" for too long.

---

## 📅 [The 12-Week Study Plan](./12-Week-Study-Plan/)

**The day-by-day schedule** that takes you from zero to certified. Built around 10 hours/week of study with realistic deload weeks. Covers both Scrum Master and PMP back-to-back.

**Best for:** anyone who wants a clear, opinionated path with no decision fatigue.

What's inside:
- Weeks 1–4: Scrum Master prep
- Weeks 5–12: PMP prep
- Daily breakdowns with reading, video, quiz allocations
- Buffer days for mock exams and review
- Day-of-exam checklist

---

## 🎯 [Exam Day Tips](./Exam-Day-Tips/)

**Read the morning of your exam.** Mental tactics, time management strategies, common trap-question patterns, and how to recover when you hit a hard stretch.

**Best for:** the day before and the day of your exam — keep this open in another tab.

What's covered:
- The night before (what to do, what NOT to do)
- Morning routine that's research-backed
- PSM I-specific strategies (45 sec/question)
- PMP-specific strategies (~77 sec/question, breaks, traps)
- What to do if you fail (it happens — here's the path forward)
