# 📋 Module 6 Cheat Sheet: Email Marketing & AI Personalization

> One page. Print it. Tape it to your monitor. Review before the exam.

---

## 📊 The Headline Stat

**$36 ROI (Return on Investment) per $1 spent** on email (DMA *Marketer Email Tracker*, most-cited).

---

## 🧱 The 5 Email Foundations

1. **List building** (permission, lead magnets, double opt-in)
2. **Segmentation** (demo + behavior + lifecycle + preference)
3. **Deliverability** (SPF/DKIM/DMARC + hygiene + engagement)
4. **Cadence** (right frequency per audience)
5. **Templates** (mobile-first, plain-text fallback, one CTA, alt text)

---

## 🔐 Authentication (REQUIRED post-Feb 2024)

| Record | Proves |
|---|---|
| **SPF** | Authorized servers can send for your domain |
| **DKIM** | Email wasn't tampered with in transit |
| **DMARC** | Policy for when SPF/DKIM fail + reporting |

Plus: one-click unsubscribe + complaint rate < 0.3%.

---

## 🎯 Drip vs Lifecycle

| | Drip | Lifecycle |
|---|---|---|
| Trigger | Time | Behavior |
| Adaptive | No | Yes |
| Example | Day 1, 3, 7 emails | Cart-abandonment, browse-abandon |

## 📋 Compliance Quick Reference

| Law | Jurisdiction | Top penalty |
|---|---|---|
| **CAN-SPAM (Controlling the Assault of Non-Solicited Pornography and Marketing Act)** | US | $50,000+/violation |
| **GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)** | EU + anyone marketing to EU | 4% global revenue or €20M |
| **CASL (Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation)** | Canada | CA$10M |
| **CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) / CPRA** | California | $7,500/intentional |

🚨 CAN-SPAM unsubscribe window: **10 business days**.

---

## 📉 The iOS 15 MPP Wrinkle

iOS 15 (Sep 2021) → Mail Privacy Protection auto-prefetches images → **open rate is inflated and unreliable**. Use **click rate** and **conversion rate** for engagement decisions.

---

## 🚨 Common Traps

- ❌ "Open rate is most important" (post-iOS 15, no)
- ❌ "Drip = lifecycle" (no, time vs behavior)
- ❌ "GDPR is only for EU companies" (no, extraterritorial)
- ❌ "Buy a list to grow fast" (illegal + tanks reputation)
- ❌ "SPF/DKIM/DMARC are nice-to-haves" (mandatory for bulk)
- ❌ Skip the welcome series

---

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

✅ Often correct: "Permission-based list," "Behavior-triggered lifecycle," "Authenticate with SPF/DKIM/DMARC," "Click + conversion over open rate," "Predictive send time"

❌ Often wrong: "Buy a list," "Send to inactive subscribers anyway," "Open rate is the gold standard," "Drip is the same as lifecycle"
