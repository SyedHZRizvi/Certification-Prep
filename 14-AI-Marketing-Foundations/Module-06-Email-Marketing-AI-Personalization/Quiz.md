## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **C. $36 per $1 spent**
The DMA *Marketer Email Tracker* number widely cited across HubSpot, Litmus, and McKinsey reports.

### Q2: **B. SPF, DKIM, DMARC**
The three DNS-based email authentication standards. Required by Gmail/Yahoo for bulk senders.

### Q3: **B. SPF, DKIM, DMARC + one-click unsubscribe + complaint rate <0.3%**
Feb 2024 enforcement. Hard cutoff for >5K daily Gmail sends.

### Q4: **B. Time-based linear sequences**
Drip = "Day 1, Day 3, Day 7..." regardless of behavior.

### Q5: **B. Behavior-triggered, often multi-channel sequences across the customer journey**
Lifecycle adapts to action. The exam-correct distinction from drip.

### Q6: **B. The welcome series**
Klaviyo and HubSpot benchmark reports consistently rank welcome series highest in per-email revenue.

### Q7: **C. Open rate**
iOS 15 MPP auto-prefetches images, inflating opens. Click and conversion now matter more.

### Q8: **B. Klaviyo**
Dominant in Shopify e-commerce. Public on NYSE in 2023.

### Q9: **B. 10 business days**
CAN-SPAM grace period before you must stop sending.

### Q10: **B. Anyone marketing to EU residents, regardless of company location**
GDPR's extraterritorial scope is a frequent exam trap.

### Q11: **B. Subscriber clicks a confirmation link after signing up**
Double opt-in reduces spam and improves deliverability.

### Q12: **B. Phrasee**
The most-cited enterprise AI-subject-line tool with published case studies.

### Q13: **B. When each individual subscriber is most likely to open**
The predictive send-time AI feature in Klaviyo, HubSpot, Mailchimp, etc.

### Q14: **B. Email permanently failed (bad address)**
Hard bounce = permanent. Soft bounce = temporary (full inbox, server down).

### Q15: **B. Native data sync with Shopify-style platforms + predictive analytics**
Klaviyo's competitive edge per public S-1 and founder interviews.

### Q16: **B. Email blocks that vary by subscriber attribute or behavior**
"Dynamic content" lets you ship one campaign that renders differently per recipient.

### Q17: **B. 4% of global revenue OR €20M (whichever higher)**
The widely-quoted GDPR cap.

### Q18: **C. $50,000+ per violation**
CAN-SPAM penalties can compound to tens of thousands per email, and are publicly enforced by the FTC.

### Q19: **B. ConvertKit / Kit or Beehiiv**
Both designed for creators/newsletters with appropriate tooling and monetization.

### Q20: **B. Truly inactive subscribers being removed from the list**
Sunsetting is the practice of removing long-inactive subscribers to protect deliverability.

### Q21: **C. $75 million**
Per Axios reporting on the October 2020 Insider Inc. acquisition.

### Q22: **B. Click rate / conversion rate**
Post-MPP, click and conversion are the reliable engagement signals.

### Q23: **B. Lifecycle (triggered, behavior-based)**
A behavior-triggered sequence. Drip would be time-based; this is event-triggered.

### Q24: **B. Subject line + preview text**
Highest leverage: opens drive every downstream metric.
