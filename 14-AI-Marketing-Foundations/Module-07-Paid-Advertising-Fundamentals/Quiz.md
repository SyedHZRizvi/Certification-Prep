## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **B. Bid × ad quality × user signals determines winner**
Modern ad auctions are not pure highest-bidder. Quality and predicted action matter.

### Q2: **B. Expected CTR + ad relevance + landing page experience**
Google's documented three Quality Score components.

### Q3: **C. Performance Max**
Google's flagship AI-driven multi-placement campaign type, launched 2021.

### Q4: **C. Advantage+ Shopping**
Meta's equivalent for e-commerce. The most-confused name pair on cross-platform exams.

### Q5: **C. 2.5×**
Break-even ROAS ≈ 1/margin. 40% margin → 1/0.4 = 2.5× minimum (before opex).

### Q6: **B. iOS 14.5 ATT broke client-side pixel attribution**
CAPI moves conversion tracking to server-side, recovering signal lost to App Tracking Transparency.

### Q7: **B. It generally has 5–20× higher conversion rate than cold prospecting**
Retargeting works on warm traffic. Caveat: frequency caps matter.

### Q8: **A. Google Search Ads**
Search captures existing demand, the user already typed their need. Best for BOFU.

### Q9: **B. Problem-Agitate-Solve**
Classic direct-response copy framework.

### Q10: **B. Asset groups + audience signals + goal**
PMax requires diverse creative + audience hints. Without rich inputs, performance suffers.

### Q11: **C. Target ROAS / Target CPA / Maximize Conversions**
"Smart bidding" strategies require enough conversion data to model from.

### Q12: **C. $100 billion**
eMarketer's 2025 projection for US RMN spend, fastest-growing digital ad category.

### Q13: **D. HubSpot RMN**
HubSpot is a CRM/marketing platform, not a Retail Media Network. The major RMNs are Amazon, Walmart, Target, Instacart, Kroger, Best Buy, Home Depot.

### Q14: **B. The pervasiveness of waste in digital ad supply chains**
Pritchard's transparency speech and P&G's $200M cut became the canonical case study in *MIT Sloan* and *HBR*.

### Q15: **B. Roughly 13% incremental conversion lift + 25% CAC improvement**
Per Wayfair's Google Marketing Live and trade press disclosures.

### Q16: **B. Audiences modeled on the characteristics of an existing customer seed list**
Lookalikes are ML-based modeling from a seed audience. Available across Meta, Google, TikTok.

### Q17: **B. PMax + manual brand + targeted prospecting + test budgets**
The portfolio approach is the 2026 best practice; pure single-strategy is rarely optimal.

### Q18: **B. Search captures existing demand**
The single biggest strategic difference between intent-driven (search) and interest-driven (social) ads.

### Q19: **C. Manual CPC or Maximize Clicks**
Smart bidding requires conversion history. New accounts should build that history first.

### Q20: **C. April 2021**
iOS 14.5 with App Tracking Transparency launched April 2021.

### Q21: **D. Best ROI Always**
Not a Google bidding strategy. The real strategies are Maximize Clicks/Conversions, Target CPA/ROAS, Manual CPC, Enhanced CPC.

### Q22: **B. Useful to protect against competitor bidding + capture organic-confused users**
Branded search is widely-taught as a defensive necessity.

### Q23: **B. Higher Quality Score yields lower CPC and better positions**
Quality Score isn't a price; it's an efficiency multiplier in the auction.

### Q24: **B. Modern social ad video creative (especially TikTok / Reels)**
HSO is the dominant short-vertical-video framework: stop the scroll, demonstrate, ask.
