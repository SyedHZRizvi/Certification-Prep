## 🧱 The 3 SEO Pillars

| Pillar | Lives In |
|---|---|
| **On-Page** | Title tags, meta descriptions, H1/H2/H3, body content, internal links, alt text, URL slug |
| **Technical** | Speed, mobile, HTTPS, sitemaps, robots.txt, canonical, schema, crawl budget |
| **Off-Page** | Backlinks, brand mentions, reviews, social signals, E-E-A-T |

---

## 🎖️ E-E-A-T (added second E in Dec 2022)

```
E - Experience       (first-hand: have you done it?)
E - Expertise        (knowledgeable: do you know it?)
A - Authoritativeness (recognized: do peers cite you?)
T - Trustworthiness  (the site itself: HTTPS, accuracy)
```

🚨 YMYL ("Your Money or Your Life", health/finance/legal/safety) = higher E-E-A-T bar.

## 🔧 Core Web Vitals (2026)

| Metric | Stands For | Good |
|---|---|---|
| **LCP** | Largest Contentful Paint | ≤ 2.5 s |
| **INP** | Interaction to Next Paint (replaced FID March 2024) | ≤ 200 ms |
| **CLS** | Cumulative Layout Shift | ≤ 0.1 |

## 🏷️ Most Common Schema Types

| Type | Use For |
|---|---|
| `Article` / `BlogPosting` | Blog posts |
| `Product` + `Offer` + `AggregateRating` | E-commerce products |
| `LocalBusiness` | Brick-and-mortar |
| `FAQPage` | Q&A sections |
| `HowTo` | Step-by-step guides |
| `BreadcrumbList` | Site nav hierarchy |
| `Event` | Webinars, conferences |
| `Review` | Individual reviews |

🛠️ Validate at `search.google.com/test/rich-results`.

---

## 🤖 GEO / AEO, Get Cited by AI Overviews

1. **Citations to authoritative sources** in your content
2. **FAQ sections** (H2 = question, 1–3 sentence answer)
3. **Statistics and data** the AI can summarize
4. **Expert quotes** with named authors and credentials
5. **Direct, declarative answer language** ("X is Y because Z")
6. **Strong E-E-A-T**: author bios, first-hand experience
7. **Freshness** (regularly update high-value posts)

---

## 🔑 Modern Keyword Workflow

```
1. Seed list (5–10 topics)
2. AI expansion (ChatGPT: TOFU / MOFU / BOFU variants)
3. Pull volume + KD in Ahrefs / SEMrush / Moz
4. Group into pillars + clusters
5. Prioritize by volume × intent × value × quick-win potential
6. Pick top 20 for next 3 months
```

---

## 🏗️ Programmatic SEO Rule of Thumb

✅ Database + template + AI-assisted unique value per page = fine
❌ Lightly-edited AI boilerplate at scale = "Scaled Content Abuse" (Google March 2024)

| **Voice SEO** | Conversational, question-shaped queries (overlaps with GEO) |

---

## 🚨 Common Traps

- ❌ Saying "E-A-T" (it's been E-E-A-T since Dec 2022)
- ❌ Citing FID (replaced by INP March 2024)
- ❌ "Keyword density" advice from 2010
- ❌ "Just publish more AI content" (Scaled Content Abuse demotes it)
- ❌ Confusing on-page (your content) with technical (your infrastructure)

---

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

✅ Often correct: "Improve E-E-A-T," "Add structured data," "Optimize Core Web Vitals," "Build topic clusters," "Cite authoritative sources"

❌ Often wrong: "Increase keyword density," "Buy backlinks," "Hide content," "Mass-publish AI articles"
