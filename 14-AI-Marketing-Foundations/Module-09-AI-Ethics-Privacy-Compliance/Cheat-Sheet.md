## 🤖 EU AI Act (March 2024) — 4 Tiers

| Tier | Examples | Requirement |
|---|---|---|
| **Unacceptable** | Social scoring, real-time biometric ID | **Banned** |
| **High-risk** | Hiring, credit, education | Heavy compliance |
| **Limited risk** | Chatbots, AI content, deepfakes | **Disclose** |
| **Minimal risk** | Spam filters | None special |

🚨 Most marketing AI = **Limited Risk = disclosure required**.

Penalties up to **7% global rev OR €35M** for prohibited.

## 🇺🇸 US AI Enforcement (2026)

- **FTC Section 5** — unfair/deceptive practices
- **FTC Endorsement Guides** (updated 2023) — AI endorsements must disclose
- **Algorithmic disgorgement** — destroy improperly-trained models
- **Colorado AI Act** (May 2024) — first state-level; effective Feb 2026
- **NYC Local Law 144** (July 2023) — bias audits for employment AI
- **NIST AI RMF 1.0** (Jan 2023) — voluntary but influential
- **ISO/IEC 42001** (Dec 2023) — international AI standard

## 🍪 Cookie Deprecation Timeline

- 2017 → Apple ITP
- April 2021 → iOS 14.5 ATT (~75% opt-out)
- 2021–2024 → multiple Google deprecation postponements
- 2025 → Google pivots to user-choice model
- 2026 → fragmented: Safari/Firefox blocked default, Chrome opt-in

**Google's reply:** Privacy Sandbox (Topics, Protected Audience, Attribution Reporting APIs)

## 💧 First-Party + Zero-Party Data

| Type | Definition |
|---|---|
| **First-party** | You collect directly, with consent, on your own properties |
| **Zero-party** | User explicitly volunteers (preferences, surveys) |
| **Second-party** | Another company's first-party data shared with you |
| **Third-party** | Aggregated from multiple sources by a vendor (declining) |

**Major CDPs:** Segment, mParticle, Tealium, BlueConic, Lytics

## 📋 Disclosure Cheat Sheet

| Content | Required Disclosure |
|---|---|
| Sponsored / paid post | "#ad" or "paid partnership" |
| AI-generated endorsement | Disclose AI source |
| Deepfake | Disclose; some banned in EU |
| Sponsored content / native | "Sponsored" label |
| Email | Sender info + unsubscribe (CAN-SPAM) |

## 🚨 Common Traps

- ❌ "GDPR only applies in EU" (no — extraterritorial)
- ❌ "Cookies will be gone by [date]" (Google moved to user-choice)
- ❌ "AI content is unregulated" (EU AI Act + FTC apply)
- ❌ "First-party data is unlimited" (still subject to GDPR/CCPA)
- ❌ "Once consented, we can do anything" (consent must be specific)
- ❌ Confusing NYC LL 144 (employment AI) with EU AI Act (general)

---

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

✅ Often correct: "Lawful basis," "First-party + zero-party + consent-aware," "Disclose AI-generated content," "Bias audit," "Data minimization," "Privacy by design"

❌ Often wrong: "Buy third-party data," "Skip disclosure," "Bias is unavoidable, ignore it," "Anonymous so no consent needed"
