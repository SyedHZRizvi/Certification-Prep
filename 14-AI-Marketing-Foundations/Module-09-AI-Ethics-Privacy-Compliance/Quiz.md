## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **B. May 25, 2018**
GDPR's enforcement date. The 2-year preparation window expired in May 2018.

### Q2: **C. 4% of global annual revenue OR €20M, whichever is higher**
The widely-cited GDPR cap.

### Q3: **A. March 2024**
The EU AI Act was approved by the European Parliament in March 2024, the world's first comprehensive AI law.

### Q4: **C. Limited risk (disclosure required)**
Chatbots, AI-generated content, deepfakes, all "limited risk" with transparency/disclosure obligations.

### Q5: **B. 100K**
CPRA increased CCPA's 50K threshold to 100K California consumers.

### Q6: **B. Children under 13**
COPPA = Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, US, 1998 (updated 2013).

### Q7: **B. Gender Shades (Buolamwini, 2018)**
The MIT Media Lab study that surfaced commercial face-recognition error disparities across demographics.

### Q8: **C. €1.2 billion**
Meta's May 2023 fine for transferring EU user data to the US without adequate safeguards.

### Q9: **B. Consent (one of six valid bases)**
GDPR's six lawful bases: consent, contract, legal obligation, vital interest, public task, legitimate interest.

### Q10: **B. FTC Endorsement Guides + EU AI Act**
Both apply. FTC enforces in the US; EU AI Act requires AI-generated-content disclosure.

### Q11: **B. Erasure**
The right to erasure is colloquially the "right to be forgotten."

### Q12: **D. Lookalike API**
Not a Privacy Sandbox API. The real Privacy Sandbox APIs include Topics, Protected Audience (formerly FLEDGE), Attribution Reporting.

### Q13: **B. Bias audits for automated employment decision tools**
NYC's pioneering AI bias law, effective July 2023.

### Q14: **A. LGPD**
Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados, Brazil's law since 2020.

### Q15: **B. PIPL**
Personal Information Protection Law, China, 2021.

### Q16: **C. PDPA**
Personal Data Protection Act, Singapore, 2014 (updated 2021).

### Q17: **B. Data collected by your company, with consent, on your own properties**
The standard first-party data definition.

### Q18: **B. Data users explicitly volunteer (preferences, intent, surveys)**
Coined by Forrester in 2018. Highest-trust data signal.

### Q19: **B. A voluntary US framework increasingly cited by enforcement agencies**
Voluntary, but functionally important, agencies like the FTC reference it.

### Q20: **A. Forcing destruction of AI models trained on improperly obtained data**
A novel and increasingly-used FTC remedy. Notable: a 2022 Cambridge Analytica-related case and 2024 Rite Aid case.

### Q21: **B. First-party + zero-party data + consent-aware tracking + MMM + incrementality**
The canonical 2026 measurement/growth stack.

### Q22: **A. Segment, mParticle, Tealium, BlueConic, Lytics**
The major CDP players. Klaviyo is an ESP; the others listed are not CDPs.

### Q23: **B. Added by CPRA in 2023**
A new right CPRA added that wasn't in original CCPA.

### Q24: **B. Pursue companies for inflated AI capability claims, biased decisions, and undisclosed AI endorsements**
The FTC's three main AI-enforcement avenues under Section 5.
