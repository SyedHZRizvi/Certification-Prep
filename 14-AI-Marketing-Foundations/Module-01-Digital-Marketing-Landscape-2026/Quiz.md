## 🎯 Answers + Explanations

### Q1: **B. Your own company blog**
Owned media is anything you control. A sponsored post is paid, a backlink is earned, a Google ad is paid.

### Q2: **C. A free trial signup page**
Free trial signup = high intent = bottom of funnel. Blog posts, YouTube explainers, and CEO podcasts are TOFU/MOFU content.

### Q3: **A. Revenue from ads ÷ ad spend**
ROAS is revenue-based. ROI is profit-based. This is the most-traps-per-question topic on the Meta and Google exams.

### Q4: **C. The Trade Desk**
The Trade Desk is an independent DSP, it competes *against* the walled gardens. The walled gardens are Google, Meta, Amazon, TikTok (and arguably Microsoft).

### Q5: **B. TOFU SEO content**
Top-of-funnel "what is X?" content gets answered directly in AI Overviews without a click. BOFU branded search and bottom-of-funnel pages are far less affected.

### Q6: **C. The customer**
Lauterborn (1990) reframed Product/Price/Place/Promotion as Customer/Cost/Convenience/Communication, the customer-centric mix.

### Q7: **B. Clayton Christensen**
Harvard's Christensen popularized JTBD with the milkshake example. His book *Competing Against Luck* (2016) is the canonical reference.

### Q8: **C. 3:1**
The widely-taught healthy benchmark for SaaS and consumer subscription is LTV:CAC ≥ 3:1. Below 2:1 is unprofitable; above 5:1 may indicate under-investment in growth.

### Q9: **B. Marketing Qualified Lead**
MQL = a lead the marketing team has scored as ready to hand off to sales. Standard HubSpot/Salesforce terminology.

### Q10: **C. April 2021**
iOS 14.5 launched in April 2021. The ATT prompt caused the famous 75–80% opt-out rate that broke Meta's attribution model.

### Q11: **A. Microsoft Advertising**
Microsoft Advertising runs Bing Search + LinkedIn Ads + Microsoft Audience Network. Strong B2B performance because of the LinkedIn signal.

### Q12: **B. Planning framework**
SOSTAC = Situation, Objectives, Strategy, Tactics, Action, Control. It's a planning/structuring framework, not a funnel or measurement model.

### Q13: **C. Coca-Cola "Create Real Magic"**
Launched March 2023 with OpenAI partnership. Widely cited in HBR, WARC, and MIT Sloan Management Review as the first big-brand GenAI campaign.

### Q14: **B. Cost per 1,000 impressions**
"Mille" is Latin for thousand. CPM is the brand-awareness pricing unit; CPC is click-driven; CPA is conversion-driven.

### Q15: **C. ROI**
Conflating ROAS and ROI is McKinsey's #1 cited attribution error. ROAS uses revenue; ROI uses profit.

### Q16: **A. Advantage+ Shopping**
Meta's AI-driven shopping campaign type. Common cross-platform exam trap: don't call it Performance Max (that's Google's product).

### Q17: **B. Activation**
Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Referral, Revenue, Dave McClure's 2007 startup metrics.

### Q18: **B. A list of email subscribers who signed up on your own website**
First-party = you collected it yourself, with consent, on your own properties. Lookalikes and vendor lists are not first-party.

### Q19: **A. Reach, Act, Convert, Engage**
Chaffey's RACE adds an explicit post-purchase Engage stage.

### Q20: **B. It is an econometric, aggregate-level attribution method**
MMM works at the aggregate level, doesn't require user tracking, and is the most-resilient method post-cookie. Hence its 2024–2026 resurgence.

### Q21: **A. Diminished click-through despite ranking**
This is the zero-click search problem. You can rank #1 organically and still lose the traffic to the AI Overview at the top.

### Q22: **C. Intuit**
Intuit acquired Mailchimp in 2021 for ~$12B in cash and stock. Most-tested digital-marketing M&A fact of the decade.

### Q23: **B. Auction-based, machine-traded ad buying**
Programmatic = software-traded inventory through real-time bidding (RTB). Distinct from "direct" insertion orders negotiated by humans.

### Q24: **C. Most mature marketers combine MMM, incrementality testing, platform-reported attribution, and first-party tracking**
This is the 2026 industry consensus, repeated in every Gartner, Forrester, and McKinsey marketing-measurement report.
