## 🧭 The Four Channel Buckets (POESM)

| Bucket | You Control? | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Paid** | Yes (if you pay) | Google Ads, Meta Ads, sponsored posts |
| **Owned** | Yes | Your blog, email list, app |
| **Earned** | No (you influence) | PR mentions, organic backlinks, reviews |
| **Shared** | Partially | UGC, Reddit threads, community discussions |

🧠 Memory: "**P**lease **O**wn **E**very **S**ale" — Paid, Owned, Earned, Shared.

## 🎯 The Funnel — Three Layers (HubSpot's Flavor)

```
   TOFU  → "I have a problem"     → SEO, YouTube, podcasts, social
    ↓
   MOFU  → "Comparing solutions"  → Email, webinars, case studies
    ↓
   BOFU  → "Ready to buy"         → Paid search, branded ads, free trial
```

| Funnel | Stands For | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| AIDA | Attention, Interest, Desire, Action | Classic copywriting |
| AARRR | Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Referral, Revenue | Startup growth |
| RACE | Reach, Act, Convert, Engage | Digital-native plans |
| TOFU/MOFU/BOFU | Top, Middle, Bottom of Funnel | HubSpot world |

## 💸 The Marketing Math (memorize the formulas)

| Term | Formula | Quick read |
|---|---|---|
| **CAC** | Total spend ÷ new customers | Cost to win one |
| **LTV** | Avg revenue × avg lifespan | Total value of one |
| **LTV:CAC** | LTV ÷ CAC | Target ≥ 3:1 |
| **AOV** | Revenue ÷ orders | Bigger basket = better |
| **CPM** | Spend ÷ (impressions ÷ 1000) | Awareness pricing |
| **CPC** | Spend ÷ clicks | Auction efficiency |
| **CPA** | Spend ÷ conversions | Channel-level CAC |
| **ROAS** | Revenue ÷ ad spend | **NOT** the same as ROI |

🚨 ROAS ≠ ROI. ROAS uses revenue; ROI uses profit.

## 🌊 The Five 2026 Shifts

1. **AI Overviews** kill TOFU clicks (60% zero-click in US).
2. **Cookie deprecation** + iOS 14.5 broke deterministic attribution.
3. **Walled-garden consolidation** — open web shrinking, CTV growing.
4. **GenAI** reset content + image + video economics.
5. **Probabilistic attribution** (MMM + incrementality + platform-reported + first-party).

## 📜 Frameworks Cornell/Harvard Drill

- **4Ps** (Kotler/McCarthy) — Product, Price, Place, Promotion
- **4Cs** (Lauterborn) — Customer, Cost, Convenience, Communication
- **JTBD** (Christensen, Harvard) — what job is the product hired for
- **OKRs / KPIs** — objectives + measurable results
- **SOSTAC** — Situation, Objectives, Strategy, Tactics, Action, Control

---

## ⚠️ Anti-Patterns To Recognize

- ❌ Treating ROAS as profit (it's revenue / spend, not profit)
- ❌ Last-click attribution as the only model
- ❌ TOFU SEO as the centerpiece of a 2026 content plan
- ❌ "More channels = more reach" — usually means more cost, less mastery
- ❌ Conflating Performance Max (Google) with Advantage+ (Meta)
- ❌ Buying email lists ("first-party data" must be collected with consent)

## 🏆 Exam Power Phrases

When you see these, they're often correct:
- ✅ "First-party data..."
- ✅ "Incrementality testing..."
- ✅ "Diversify across walled gardens + open web..."
- ✅ "Consent-driven measurement..."
- ✅ "MMM + platform-reported + first-party..."

When you see these, they're often wrong:
- ❌ "Rely on third-party cookies..."
- ❌ "Use only last-click attribution..."
- ❌ "Buy a list of prospects..."
- ❌ "Disable Consent Mode..."
